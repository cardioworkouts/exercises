Basic Exercise Plan For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you new to exercise and unsure where to start? Do you feel overwhelmed by the plethora of information available online? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we will provide you with a basic exercise plan for beginners that is easy to follow and will help you kickstart your fitness journey.

Starting a new exercise routine can be daunting, especially if you're not sure what exercises to do or how to do them. You may also be worried about injuring yourself or not seeing any results. These are common concerns for beginners and can often lead to them giving up before they even start.

The target of our basic exercise plan for beginners is to provide you with a simple and effective routine that will help you build strength, improve your cardiovascular fitness, and boost your overall health and wellbeing. Our plan includes a mix of cardio and strength exercises that can easily be done at home or at the gym.

Overall, our basic exercise plan for beginners includes:

Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardiovascular exercise is any form of exercise that gets your heart rate up and increases your breathing rate. It is important for improving your cardiovascular fitness, burning calories, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. Examples of cardio exercises include walking, jogging, cycling, swimming, and dancing.

Personally, I love going for a walk in the morning. It helps me clear my mind and get some fresh air. I usually aim to walk for 30 minutes at a moderate pace. If I'm feeling up to it, I might jog for a few minutes or add some hills to my route to increase the intensity.

Strength Training

Strength training is any form of exercise that uses resistance to build muscle. It is important for improving your muscle strength and endurance, increasing your metabolism, and reducing the risk of injury. Examples of strength exercises include bodyweight exercises such as squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks, as well as weightlifting exercises such as deadlifts, bench press, and shoulder press.

When I first started strength training, I found bodyweight exercises to be the easiest to do at home. I would do a circuit of squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks, with 30 seconds of rest in between each exercise. I would aim to complete three rounds of the circuit, gradually increasing the number of reps as I got stronger.

Stretching and Flexibility

Stretching and flexibility exercises are important for improving your range of motion, reducing the risk of injury, and promoting relaxation. Examples of stretching exercises include static stretches, dynamic stretches, and yoga poses.

I like to end my workout with some gentle stretching exercises to help me cool down and relax. My favourite stretches include hamstring stretches, quad stretches, and shoulder stretches. I usually hold each stretch for 30 seconds, taking deep breaths in and out to help me relax.

How to Get Started

Now that you know what exercises to do, it's time to get started. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your basic exercise plan for beginners:

  • Start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.
  • Listen to your body and stop if you feel any pain or discomfort.
  • Make it a habit by scheduling your workouts into your daily routine.
  • Mix it up by trying different exercises and workouts to keep things interesting.
  • Track your progress by keeping a workout journal or using a fitness app.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need any equipment to do these exercises?

A: No, all of the exercises in our basic exercise plan for beginners can be done with just your bodyweight. However, if you have access to weights or resistance bands, you can use them to increase the intensity of your workouts.

Q: How often should I do these exercises?

A: We recommend doing these exercises at least three times a week, with rest days in between. However, you can adjust the frequency and duration of your workouts based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can I do these exercises if I have a health condition?

A: It's important to consult with your doctor before starting any new exercise routine, especially if you have a health condition. They can advise you on any modifications or precautions you need to take.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Results vary depending on your fitness level, diet, and other lifestyle factors. However, with consistent effort and dedication, you should start to see improvements in your strength, endurance, and overall fitness within a few weeks.

Conclusion of Basic Exercise Plan for Beginners

Starting a new exercise routine can be challenging, but with the right plan and mindset, it can also be rewarding. Our basic exercise plan for beginners provides you with a simple and effective way to improve your fitness and wellbeing. Remember to start slowly, listen to your body, and have fun!
