10 Best Free Fitness Apps For Android

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to stay fit and healthy without breaking the bank? Look no further than your Android phone. With the abundance of free fitness apps available on the Google Play Store, you can easily achieve your fitness goals without spending a single dime.

Whether you're trying to lose weight, build muscle, or simply stay active, there's a fitness app out there that can help you achieve your goals. In this post, we'll explore the best free fitness apps for Android and how they can benefit you.

Here are the top 10 best free fitness apps for Android:

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that helps you track your calorie intake and exercise. With a database of over 11 million foods, you can easily log your meals and snacks and keep track of your progress. The app also allows you to connect with friends and join community groups for extra motivation.

2. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club offers a variety of workouts designed by Nike trainers. The app includes over 190 free workouts ranging from strength training to yoga. You can also customize your own workout plan and track your progress.

3. Google Fit

Google Fit is a simple app that tracks your activity and helps you set fitness goals. The app also syncs with other fitness apps on your phone, making it easy to keep track of your progress in one place.

4. FitOn

FitOn offers a range of free workout videos led by celebrity trainers. The app includes a variety of workouts, from HIIT to yoga, and allows you to track your progress and connect with friends for extra motivation.

5. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a simple app that offers quick, high-intensity workouts that you can do anywhere. The app includes a variety of workouts designed to target specific areas of your body, and allows you to customize your workout plan.

6. Sworkit

Sworkit offers a range of free workouts, from strength training to yoga. The app allows you to customize your workout plan and provides video demonstrations of each exercise.

7. JEFIT

JEFIT is a strength training app that offers a variety of exercises and workout plans. The app includes a database of over 1,300 exercises and allows you to create and customize your own workout plan.

8. Endomondo

Endomondo is a popular app that tracks your workouts and provides audio feedback during your exercise. The app also allows you to connect with friends for extra motivation and compete with others in challenges.

9. Daily Burn

Daily Burn offers a variety of free workouts led by certified trainers. The app includes a range of workouts, from cardio to strength training, and allows you to track your progress and connect with friends for extra motivation.

10. Yoga Studio

Yoga Studio offers a range of free yoga classes for all levels. The app includes over 75 classes and allows you to customize your own yoga practice.

Overall, these free fitness apps for Android offer a range of benefits, from tracking your progress to connecting with friends for extra motivation. With so many options available, there's no excuse not to stay fit and healthy.

Personal Experience with MyFitnessPal

I have been using MyFitnessPal for over a year now, and it has been a game-changer for me. The app is easy to use and allows me to track my calorie intake and exercise with ease. I love that I can connect with friends and join community groups for extra motivation. Overall, MyFitnessPal has helped me achieve my weight loss goals and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Personal Experience with Nike Training Club

I recently started using Nike Training Club, and I am already hooked. The app offers a range of workouts designed by Nike trainers, and I love that I can customize my own workout plan. The app also tracks my progress and provides audio cues during my workout, which keeps me motivated and on track.

How to Choose the Best Free Fitness App for You

When choosing a free fitness app, it's important to consider your fitness goals and preferences. If you're looking to lose weight, an app that tracks your calorie intake and exercise may be the best choice for you. If you prefer yoga or strength training, there are apps available that cater to those preferences. It's also important to consider the app's user interface and ease of use.

How to Make the Most of Your Free Fitness App

To make the most of your free fitness app, it's important to use it consistently and track your progress. Set specific goals and use the app's features to help you achieve them. Connect with friends for extra motivation and support, and don't be afraid to try new workouts and exercises.

Conclusion of Best Free Fitness Apps for Android

With so many free fitness apps available on the Google Play Store, there's no excuse not to stay fit and healthy. Whether you prefer yoga, strength training, or cardio, there's an app out there that can help you achieve your fitness goals. So what are you waiting for? Download one of these free fitness apps today and start your fitness journey.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are these fitness apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, most of these fitness apps offer workouts suitable for all fitness levels, including beginners.

Q: Can I track my progress using these apps?

A: Yes, most of these apps allow you to track your progress and set fitness goals.

Q: Do these apps require any equipment?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps require equipment, while others offer equipment-free workouts.

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, these apps are completely free to download and use. Some may offer in-app purchases for additional features, but the basic app is free.
