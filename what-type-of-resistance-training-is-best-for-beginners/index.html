What Type Of Resistance Training Is Best For Beginners

Written by Petter Sep 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you new to the world of fitness and wondering where to start with resistance training? There are so many different types of resistance training out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your fitness goals. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

Starting a new exercise routine can be intimidating, especially if you're not sure what type of resistance training is best for you. You may be worried about getting injured, or not seeing results fast enough. You may also be unsure about how to use the equipment or how to properly do the exercises.

The best type of resistance training for beginners is bodyweight exercises. These exercises use your own body weight to create resistance and build strength. Bodyweight exercises are great for beginners because they are low-impact, don't require any equipment, and can be done anywhere.

So why are bodyweight exercises the best type of resistance training for beginners? Let's dive into the details.

Why Bodyweight Exercises are Best for Beginners

When I first started my fitness journey, I was intimidated by the weight room and all the machines. I wasn't sure how to use them or if I was doing the exercises correctly. That's when I discovered bodyweight exercises.

Bodyweight exercises are a great way to build strength without putting too much strain on your body. They are also very versatile and can be modified to suit your fitness level. For example, if you're just starting out, you can do a modified push-up on your knees instead of a full push-up.

Bodyweight exercises also help to improve your balance and coordination, which is important for overall fitness. Plus, you don't need any equipment, so you can do them anywhere, anytime.

How to Incorporate Bodyweight Exercises into Your Routine

If you're new to bodyweight exercises, it's important to start slow and gradually build up your strength. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Start with basic exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks
  • Do 2-3 sets of each exercise, with 10-15 reps per set
  • Take breaks as needed, but try to keep your heart rate up
  • Gradually increase the number of reps and sets as you get stronger

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need any equipment for bodyweight exercises?

No, bodyweight exercises don't require any equipment. You can do them anywhere, anytime.

2. How often should I do bodyweight exercises?

You should aim to do bodyweight exercises at least 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between.

3. Can bodyweight exercises help me lose weight?

Yes, bodyweight exercises can help you lose weight by building muscle and increasing your metabolism.

4. How long will it take to see results from bodyweight exercises?

It depends on your fitness level and how often you do the exercises. With consistent effort, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Conclusion

If you're looking to get started with resistance training, bodyweight exercises are the way to go. They are safe, effective, and can be done anywhere, anytime. Remember to start slow, gradually build up your strength, and listen to your body. With consistent effort, you'll be on your way to a stronger, healthier you!
