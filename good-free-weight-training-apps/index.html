Best Free Weight Training Apps For Exercise Enthusiasts

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to take your weight training to the next level and get maximum results? With the help of technology, you can improve your weight training techniques and push beyond your limits. In this article, we will explore some of the best free weight training apps that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Weight training can be challenging, especially if you are a beginner or trying to break a plateau. You may find yourself struggling to achieve your desired results or not knowing which exercises to perform. Fortunately, there are several apps available that can help you overcome these challenges and achieve your fitness goals.

If you are looking for good free weight training apps, then you have come to the right place. Here are some of the best free weight training apps you can download:

1. Nike Training Club

The Nike Training Club app is a comprehensive fitness app that offers a range of free workouts, including strength and weight training. With this app, you can choose from a variety of workouts that cater to your fitness level and goals. The app also provides personalized training plans that can help you stay on track and achieve your fitness goals.

I have been using the Nike Training Club app for a while now, and I love how it offers a range of workouts that cater to different fitness goals. The app also allows you to track your progress and offers tips on how to improve your technique.

2. JEFIT

JEFIT is a popular weight training app that offers a range of free workouts and customizable training plans. With this app, you can track your progress and monitor your performance. The app also offers a range of social features, including the ability to connect with other users and share your progress with friends.

I have found the JEFIT app to be particularly useful when it comes to tracking my progress and monitoring my performance. The app also offers a range of exercises that cater to different muscle groups, which makes it easy to create customized workout plans.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a popular weight training app that uses AI technology to create customized workout plans. With this app, you can input your fitness goals and preferences, and the app will create a personalized workout plan for you. The app also offers a range of features, including exercise videos, progress tracking, and workout summaries.

I recently started using the Fitbod app, and I love how it creates customized workout plans that cater to my fitness goals. The app also provides detailed workout summaries, which makes it easy to track my progress.

Why Use Free Weight Training Apps?

Free weight training apps can be a valuable tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness and achieve their goals. Here are some of the benefits of using free weight training apps:

  • Access to a range of workouts
  • Customizable training plans
  • Progress tracking and monitoring
  • Exercise videos and tips
  • Social features and community support

How to Choose the Best Free Weight Training App for You

Choosing the best free weight training app can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. Here are some factors to consider when choosing a weight training app:

  • Your fitness goals
  • Your fitness level
  • Your preferred workout style
  • The features offered by the app
  • Your budget

4. StrongLifts 5x5

StrongLifts 5x5 is a popular weight training app that focuses on the 5x5 training method. This method involves performing five sets of five reps for each exercise, with a focus on compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses. The app is designed to help you build strength and muscle mass.

I have used the StrongLifts 5x5 app in the past, and I love how it focuses on compound exercises that target multiple muscle groups. The app also provides detailed instructions on how to perform each exercise, which makes it easy to improve your technique.

5. BodySpace

BodySpace is a popular weight training app that offers a range of free workouts and customizable training plans. With this app, you can track your progress and monitor your performance. The app also offers a range of social features, including the ability to connect with other users and share your progress with friends.

I have found the BodySpace app to be particularly useful when it comes to creating customized workout plans. The app also provides detailed workout summaries, which makes it easy to track my progress.

Question and Answer

Q: Are free weight training apps as effective as paid apps?

A: Yes, free weight training apps can be just as effective as paid apps, especially if you choose an app that caters to your fitness goals and preferences.

Q: Can weight training apps help me build muscle mass?

A: Yes, weight training apps can help you build muscle mass, especially if you choose an app that focuses on compound exercises and progressive overload.

Q: Are weight training apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, weight training apps can be suitable for beginners, especially if you choose an app that provides detailed instructions and guidance on how to perform each exercise.

Q: Do weight training apps provide personalized training plans?

A: Yes, many weight training apps provide personalized training plans that cater to your fitness goals and preferences.

Conclusion of Good Free Weight Training Apps

Good free weight training apps can be a valuable tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness and achieve their goals. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced weight trainer, there is an app out there that can help you reach your full potential. By choosing the right app and staying consistent with your workouts, you can achieve your fitness goals and become the best version of yourself.
