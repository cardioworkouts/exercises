What is Cardio?

Cardio, short for cardiovascular exercise, is any form of exercise that increases your heart rate and respiratory rate. The goal of cardio exercises is to improve the health of your heart and lungs, promote weight loss, and improve overall fitness levels. Cardio exercises can be done indoors or outdoors and can range from low to high intensity.

When I started my fitness journey, I was intimidated by cardio exercises. I didn't know where to start, and I didn't want to embarrass myself in front of others. But I started with brisk walking for 30 minutes a day, and gradually increased the intensity and duration of my cardio exercises. Brisk walking is an excellent low-impact cardio exercise for beginners.

Best Cardio Exercises for Weight Loss for Beginners

1. Brisk Walking

Brisk walking is an excellent low-impact cardio exercise for beginners. It is easy on your joints, burns calories, and can be done indoors or outdoors. Start with a 30-minute brisk walk, gradually increasing the intensity and duration of your walks. You can also incorporate hills or stairs to increase the intensity of your walks.

2. Cycling

Cycling is another low-impact cardio exercise that can help you lose weight. You can cycle outdoors or indoors on a stationary bike. Start with a 30-minute ride, gradually increasing the intensity and duration of your rides. You can also incorporate intervals of high intensity to increase the calorie burn.

3. Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that can help you burn a significant amount of calories. It is a low-impact exercise that is easy on your joints. Start with a 30-minute swim, gradually increasing the intensity and duration of your swims. You can also incorporate different strokes to work different muscle groups.

4. Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a great way to get your heart rate up and burn calories. They are a high-intensity cardio exercise that can be done anywhere. Start with a set of 20 jumping jacks, gradually increasing the number of sets and reps.

The Benefits of Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercises have numerous benefits for your overall health and well-being. Some of the benefits of cardio exercises include:

- Improved heart health

- Increased lung capacity

- Weight loss

- Improved mood and mental health

- Reduced risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, high blood pressure, and heart disease

How Often Should You Do Cardio?

The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises per week or 75 minutes of high-intensity cardio exercises per week. It is best to spread out your cardio exercises throughout the week to give your body time to recover.

Combining Cardio with Strength Training

While cardio exercises are great for weight loss, combining them with strength training can help you achieve optimal results. Strength training helps build muscle, which in turn increases your metabolism and calorie burn. Aim to do strength training exercises at least twice a week, targeting different muscle groups.

When I started incorporating strength training into my fitness routine, I saw significant changes in my body composition. My muscles became more defined, and I burned more calories at rest.
