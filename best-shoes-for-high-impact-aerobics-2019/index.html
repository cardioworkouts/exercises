Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear running shoes for high impact aerobics?

A: While running shoes can provide you with some cushioning and support, they may not be the best option for high impact aerobics. Running shoes are designed for forward motion, while high impact aerobics involve a lot of lateral movements. It is best to invest in shoes that are specifically designed for high impact aerobics.

Q: How often should I replace my high impact aerobics shoes?

A: It is recommended that you replace your high impact aerobics shoes every 6-8 months, or after 300-500 miles of use. This will ensure that your shoes continue to provide you with the necessary cushioning and support during your workout sessions.

Q: Can I wear cross training shoes for high impact aerobics?

A: Cross training shoes can be a good option for high impact aerobics, as they provide you with the necessary cushioning and support for a variety of different movements. However, it is important to make sure that the shoes you choose are specifically designed for high impact aerobics.

Q: Can I wear high impact aerobics shoes for other types of workouts?
