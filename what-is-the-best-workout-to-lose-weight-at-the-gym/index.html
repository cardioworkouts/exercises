What Is The Best Workout To Lose Weight At The Gym

Written by Robby Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying multiple diets and not seeing any results? Do you feel like you are not making any progress in your weight loss journey? If you answered yes to these questions, then you are not alone. Many people struggle with losing weight and finding the best workout to achieve their goals.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to weight loss is not knowing what exercises to do at the gym to get the best results. It can be overwhelming to walk into a gym and not know where to start or what machines to use.

The best workout to lose weight at the gym is a combination of cardio and strength training. Cardio exercises will help you burn calories and fat, while strength training will help you build muscle and increase your metabolism. By doing both, you will see the best results in terms of weight loss and muscle toning.

In summary, the best workout to lose weight at the gym is a combination of cardio and strength training. This article will dive deeper into the specifics of each exercise and how to incorporate them into your workout routine.

Cardio Exercises

When it comes to cardio exercises, there are a variety of options to choose from. The most popular ones include running on the treadmill, using the elliptical machine, cycling on a stationary bike, and using the stair climber. Each of these exercises will help you burn calories and fat, but it is important to switch up your routine to keep your body challenged.

Personally, I like to start my cardio routine with a 10-minute warm-up on the elliptical. Then, I will move on to the treadmill and do a combination of running and walking for 30 minutes. I like to finish my routine with 10 minutes on the stair climber. By switching up my routine, I am able to challenge my body and see better results.

Strength Training Exercises

Strength training exercises are just as important as cardio exercises when it comes to weight loss. They help you build muscle, increase your metabolism, and tone your body. Some examples of strength training exercises include weightlifting, squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks.

Personally, I like to focus on one muscle group per day when I am strength training. For example, on Monday, I will focus on my chest and do exercises like bench press and push-ups. On Tuesday, I will focus on my legs and do exercises like squats and lunges. This allows me to fully target each muscle group and see better results.

Combining Cardio and Strength Training

The key to the best workout to lose weight at the gym is to combine both cardio and strength training exercises. This can be done by alternating between the two or doing a circuit-style workout where you do both in one session.

For example, you could start with 10 minutes on the elliptical, move on to 10 minutes of weightlifting, do 10 minutes on the treadmill, do 10 minutes of lunges and squats, and finish with 10 minutes on the stair climber. By doing both cardio and strength training in one session, you are able to maximize your results.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you succeed in your weight loss journey:

  1. Set realistic goals for yourself
  2. Track your progress
  3. Switch up your routine to keep your body challenged
  4. Stay consistent with your workouts and nutrition

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio and strength training?

A: It is recommended to do at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week and strength train each muscle group at least twice a week.

Q: Should I lift heavy weights or light weights?

A: It is recommended to lift a weight that is challenging for you, but not too heavy that you sacrifice proper form. Aim for 8-12 repetitions per set.

Q: Can I do cardio and strength training on the same day?

A: Yes, you can do both cardio and strength training on the same day. Just make sure to switch up your routine and not do the same exercises every day.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: It varies for each person, but you can expect to see results in 4-6 weeks if you stay consistent with your workouts and nutrition.

Conclusion of What is the Best Workout to Lose Weight at the Gym

The best workout to lose weight at the gym is a combination of cardio and strength training exercises. By incorporating both into your routine and staying consistent, you will see the best results in terms of weight loss and muscle toning. Remember to set realistic goals, track your progress, and switch up your routine to keep your body challenged.
