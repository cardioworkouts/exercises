1. "One Margarita" by Luke Bryan

When it comes to the best country workout songs of 2020, "One Margarita" by Luke Bryan is a must-have. This upbeat song is all about letting loose and having a good time, making it the perfect addition to your workout playlist. The catchy chorus and high-energy beat will keep you moving and grooving all workout long.

Personally, I love listening to "One Margarita" during my cardio workouts. The fast-paced beat helps me keep a steady pace and the uplifting lyrics remind me to have fun and enjoy the moment. Whether I'm running on the treadmill or cycling on the stationary bike, this song always helps me power through my workout.

2. "I Hope" by Gabby Barrett

If you're looking for a song that will motivate you to push through the toughest parts of your workout, look no further than "I Hope" by Gabby Barrett. This powerful song is all about overcoming obstacles and fighting for what you want, making it the perfect anthem for your fitness journey.

Personally, I love listening to "I Hope" during my strength training workouts. The inspiring lyrics remind me to keep pushing, even when the weights get heavy and my muscles are burning. This song helps me stay focused and motivated, and it always makes me feel like I can conquer anything.

3. "Hard to Forget" by Sam Hunt

If you're looking for a song that will get you in the zone and help you power through your workout, "Hard to Forget" by Sam Hunt is the perfect choice. This upbeat song has a catchy beat and infectious chorus that will keep you moving and grooving all workout long.

Personally, I love listening to "Hard to Forget" during my high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts. The fast-paced beat helps me keep up with the intervals, and the energy of the song keeps me motivated even when I'm feeling tired. This song always helps me push through my workout and finish strong.

Why Country Music is Great for Workouts

There are many reasons why country music is great for workouts. For one, it's often upbeat and high-energy, which can help you stay motivated and focused during your workout. Additionally, many country songs have inspiring lyrics that can help you push through the toughest parts of your workout and stay positive.

Another reason why country music is great for workouts is that it often tells a story. Whether it's about overcoming obstacles, falling in love, or just having a good time, country songs can help you connect with the emotions and experiences of the artist. This can be especially inspiring during your workout, as you're pushing yourself to overcome your own obstacles and achieve your own goals.

How to Create the Perfect Country Workout Playlist

If you're ready to create the perfect country workout playlist, here are a few tips to get you started:

  1. Start with some of the top hits from 2020, like "One Margarita," "I Hope," and "Hard to Forget."
  2. Include a mix of upbeat and slower songs to keep things interesting and provide variety.
  3. Look for songs with inspiring lyrics that will help you stay motivated and focused during your workout.
  4. Consider adding songs from a variety of country sub-genres, such as classic country, modern country, and country rock.
  5. Finally, don't be afraid to switch up your playlist from time to time! Adding new songs and removing old ones can help keep your workouts fresh and exciting.

Conclusion of Best Country Workout Songs 2020

The best country workout songs of 2020 are the perfect addition to any workout playlist. With high-energy beats and inspiring lyrics, these songs will help you power through your workout and reach your fitness goals. Whether you're running on the treadmill, lifting weights, or doing a HIIT workout, these top hits from Luke Bryan, Gabby Barrett, and Sam Hunt are sure to keep you motivated and moving. So what are you waiting for? Add some of these songs to your playlist today and get ready to crush your next workout!
