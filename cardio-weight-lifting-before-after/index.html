What is Cardio Weight Lifting Before After?

Cardio weight lifting before after is a workout routine that combines cardiovascular exercises and weightlifting. This type of workout routine is designed to help you burn fat, build muscle, and improve your overall health and fitness. It involves performing cardio exercises such as running, cycling, or jumping jacks, followed by weightlifting exercises such as squats, lunges, or deadlifts.

When you perform cardio exercises, your heart rate increases, and your body starts to burn fat. Weightlifting exercises help you build muscle, which in turn helps you burn even more fat. The combination of cardio and weightlifting is a powerful way to get fit and achieve your fitness goals.
