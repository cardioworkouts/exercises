Can Resistance Training Help You Lose Weight

Written by Aprilia Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight? Have you been trying various diets and exercises, but nothing seems to work? If yes, then resistance training might just be the answer you are looking for.

Many people think that only cardio exercises like running, cycling, or swimming can help them lose weight. However, resistance training or weight lifting can also be an effective way to shed those extra pounds.

The short answer is yes, resistance training can help you lose weight. In fact, it can be more effective than cardio exercises alone.

Resistance training, also known as strength training, involves using weights or resistance bands to work your muscles. This type of exercise helps to build lean muscle mass and boost your metabolism, which in turn helps you burn more calories even when you are not working out.

My Experience with Resistance Training for Weight Loss

Before I started resistance training, I used to do only cardio exercises like running and cycling. Although I was losing some weight, I wasn't seeing the desired results.

Then, a friend suggested that I try resistance training. I was skeptical at first, but I gave it a try. To my surprise, I started seeing results within a few weeks. I not only lost weight, but I also felt stronger and more toned.

Resistance training helped me to build lean muscle mass, which in turn boosted my metabolism and allowed me to burn more calories even when I wasn't working out.

The Benefits of Resistance Training for Weight Loss

Here are some of the benefits of resistance training for weight loss:

Builds Lean Muscle Mass

Resistance training helps to build lean muscle mass, which is important for weight loss. The more muscle you have, the more calories you burn, even when you are not working out.

Boosts Metabolism

Resistance training also helps to boost your metabolism, which is essential for weight loss. A higher metabolism means that your body burns more calories at rest, which can help you lose weight faster.

Burns More Calories

Resistance training burns more calories than cardio exercises alone. This is because weight lifting exercises require more energy and effort, which in turn burns more calories.

Tones Your Body

Resistance training helps to tone your body and improve your overall physique. This is because it helps to build lean muscle mass and reduce body fat.

FAQs About Resistance Training and Weight Loss

Q: How often should I do resistance training to lose weight?

A: It is recommended to do resistance training at least two to three times per week for weight loss.

Q: What types of resistance training exercises are best for weight loss?

A: Compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses are some of the best resistance training exercises for weight loss.

Q: Can I do resistance training at home without equipment?

A: Yes, you can do resistance training at home without equipment by using your own body weight. Exercises like push-ups, squats, and lunges are great for weight loss.

Q: Do I need to lift heavy weights to lose weight?

A: No, you don't need to lift heavy weights to lose weight. Lighter weights with higher reps can also be effective for weight loss.

Conclusion

Resistance training can be a great way to lose weight and improve your overall health and fitness. It helps to build lean muscle mass, boost your metabolism, burn more calories, and tone your body. So, if you are looking to lose weight, give resistance training a try and see the results for yourself.
