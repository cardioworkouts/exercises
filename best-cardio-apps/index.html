Best Cardio Apps A Beginner s Guide To Staying Fit

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you find it challenging to keep up with your fitness routine? Or are you tired of going to the gym every day? If yes, then you are not alone. Many people struggle to stay motivated and consistent with their workout plans. However, with the help of the best cardio apps, you can achieve your fitness goals without leaving your home.

Cardiovascular exercises are an essential part of any fitness routine. They help improve heart health, burn calories, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases. However, finding the right cardio workout can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be challenging to choose the right one that meets your fitness needs.

The good news is that there are many best cardio apps available that can help you achieve your fitness goals. These apps offer a variety of workouts, from high-intensity cardio to low-impact exercises, making them suitable for beginners and advanced fitness enthusiasts alike.

In this article, we will discuss the best cardio apps that can help you stay fit and healthy. We will cover their features, benefits, and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

1. Nike Training Club

One of the best cardio apps available is the Nike Training Club app. It offers a variety of workouts, including cardio, strength training, yoga, and mobility exercises. The app is suitable for all fitness levels, and you can customize your workout plan based on your fitness goals.

I have been using the Nike Training Club app for the past six months, and it has helped me stay consistent with my workout routine. The app offers a variety of workouts that keep me motivated and engaged. Additionally, the app offers personalized coaching and training plans, making it an excellent choice for beginners.

2. Aaptiv

If you enjoy listening to music while working out, then Aaptiv is the perfect cardio app for you. It offers audio-based workouts, including running, cycling, and strength training. Each workout is accompanied by a curated playlist that keeps you motivated and energized.

I have been using Aaptiv for the past year, and it has been a game-changer for my fitness routine. The app offers a variety of workouts suitable for all fitness levels. Additionally, the app tracks your progress and provides personalized recommendations based on your fitness goals.

3. Fitbit Coach

If you own a Fitbit device, then the Fitbit Coach app is an excellent choice for you. The app offers personalized workouts based on your fitness level and goals. Additionally, it provides real-time coaching and feedback, making it an excellent choice for beginners.

I have been using the Fitbit Coach app for the past few months, and it has helped me stay consistent with my fitness routine. The app offers a variety of workouts that keep me motivated and challenged. Additionally, the app syncs with my Fitbit device, making it easy to track my progress.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not a traditional cardio app, MyFitnessPal is an excellent tool for tracking your diet and exercise. The app offers a database of over 11 million foods, making it easy to track your daily calorie intake. Additionally, the app integrates with other fitness apps, making it easy to track your progress.

I have been using MyFitnessPal for the past few years, and it has been a game-changer for my fitness routine. The app helps me track my daily calorie intake and provides personalized recommendations based on my fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all these apps offer workouts suitable for beginners. Additionally, they provide personalized coaching and recommendations based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can I use these apps without any fitness equipment?

A: Yes, most of these apps offer workouts that do not require any equipment. However, some workouts may require minimal equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands.

Q: Are these apps free to use?

A: Most of these apps offer a free version with limited features. However, to access all the features, you may need to purchase a premium subscription.

Q: Can I use these apps on my smartwatch?

A: Yes, most of these apps offer smartwatch compatibility, making it easy to track your workouts on the go.

Conclusion

Cardiovascular exercises are an essential part of any fitness routine. With the help of the best cardio apps, you can achieve your fitness goals without leaving your home. The Nike Training Club, Aaptiv, Fitbit Coach, and MyFitnessPal are some of the best cardio apps available that can help you stay fit and healthy. These apps offer a variety of workouts suitable for all fitness levels, making them an excellent choice for beginners and advanced fitness enthusiasts alike. So, download these apps today and start your fitness journey!
