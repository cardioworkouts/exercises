The Best Cardio Workout Apps For Iphone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to get in shape and stay healthy? Do you struggle with finding the motivation to exercise regularly? Cardio workout apps for iPhone may be the solution you need. With the convenience of having a personal trainer in your pocket, these apps can help you stay on track and reach your fitness goals.

Cardio workout apps for iPhone are designed to help users achieve their cardio fitness goals. These apps provide a range of workouts that are tailored to the user's fitness level and goals. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build endurance, or increase your overall fitness level, there is a cardio workout app for you.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at some of the best cardio workout apps for iPhone. We'll explore their features, benefits, and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is one of the most popular cardio workout apps for iPhone. It offers a wide range of workouts, including cardio, strength, and yoga. The app also includes personalized training plans, workout reminders, and progress tracking. One of the best features of Nike Training Club is that it offers workouts for all fitness levels, from beginner to advanced.

Personally, I have been using Nike Training Club for the past year and have seen a significant improvement in my overall fitness level. The app is easy to use and the workouts are challenging yet achievable. Plus, I love the fact that I can customize my workout plan based on my goals and fitness level.

2. Aaptiv

Aaptiv is another popular cardio workout app for iPhone. It offers audio-based workouts that are led by certified personal trainers. The app includes a range of workouts, from running and cycling to strength training and yoga. Aaptiv also provides personalized training plans and progress tracking.

One of the things I love about Aaptiv is that the audio-based workouts make it easy to stay motivated and engaged throughout the entire workout. The trainers provide encouragement and guidance throughout the workout, which helps me push myself harder and achieve better results.

3. MapMyRun

MapMyRun is a popular cardio workout app for runners. It offers a range of features, including GPS tracking, personalized training plans, and progress tracking. The app also allows users to connect with other runners and share their progress.

As a runner, I find MapMyRun to be an essential tool for tracking my runs and monitoring my progress. The GPS tracking feature is especially helpful for mapping out new routes and tracking my pace and distance.

4. Zombies, Run!

Zombies, Run! is a unique cardio workout app that combines running with a zombie apocalypse game. The app includes over 200 missions, each designed to simulate a real-life zombie apocalypse scenario. The app also includes progress tracking and personalized training plans.

I've used Zombies, Run! in the past and it's a fun and engaging way to get in a cardio workout. The missions are challenging yet achievable, and the game-like format makes it easy to stay motivated and engaged throughout the workout.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all of these apps offer workouts for all fitness levels, including beginners.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the workout. Some workouts may require equipment, while others can be done with no equipment at all.

Q: Can I customize my workout plan?

A: Yes, all of these apps offer personalized training plans that can be customized based on your goals and fitness level.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: All of these apps offer a free version, but some features may be limited. Premium versions of the apps are also available for a fee.

Conclusion

Cardio workout apps for iPhone are a great way to stay on track and achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, there is an app out there for you. By using these apps regularly, you can improve your cardio fitness level and overall health.
