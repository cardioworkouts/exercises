Best Personal Trainer Apps For Clients

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to stay motivated and keep track of your fitness goals? Do you struggle to find time to go to the gym or afford a personal trainer? Luckily, with the rise of technology and smartphones, there are several apps available to help you achieve your fitness goals in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we will explore the best personal trainer apps for clients.

It can be challenging to find the motivation to exercise, especially if you are working long hours or have a busy schedule. Additionally, hiring a personal trainer can be expensive and may not be an option for everyone. Thankfully, personal trainer apps can provide you with a customized workout plan and daily reminders to help you stay on track.

What are the Best Personal Trainer Apps for Clients?

There are several personal trainer apps available on the market, but some stand out from the rest in terms of features and usability. Here are some of the best personal trainer apps for clients:

1. Aaptiv

Aaptiv is a popular personal trainer app that offers audio-based workouts with expert trainers. The app provides a variety of classes, including running, cycling, strength training, and yoga. Additionally, Aaptiv offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and tracks your progress. The app costs $14.99 per month, but you can try it for free for seven days.

2. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free personal trainer app that offers a variety of workouts and fitness plans. The app provides video tutorials and audio instructions from expert trainers, making it easy to follow along. Additionally, Nike Training Club offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and tracks your progress. The app also includes challenges and rewards to help you stay motivated.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a personal trainer app that offers customized workout plans based on your fitness goals and equipment available. The app uses machine learning algorithms to create a personalized workout plan that adapts to your progress. Additionally, Fitbod tracks your progress and provides recommendations for future workouts. The app costs $9.99 per month or $59.99 per year.

4. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular fitness app that offers a variety of features to help you achieve your fitness goals. The app allows you to track your food intake and exercise, providing you with a personalized calorie goal based on your fitness goals. Additionally, MyFitnessPal offers personalized workout plans and tracks your progress. The app is free to use, but you can upgrade to premium for additional features.

Personal Experience with Personal Trainer Apps

I have personally used Aaptiv and Nike Training Club, and both have been instrumental in helping me achieve my fitness goals. Aaptiv provides challenging and engaging audio-based workouts with expert trainers, while Nike Training Club offers a variety of workouts and personalized plans. Both apps have helped me stay motivated and on track with my fitness journey.

Benefits of Using Personal Trainer Apps

There are several benefits to using personal trainer apps, including:

1. Customized Workouts:

Personal trainer apps offer customized workout plans based on your fitness goals and equipment available. This allows you to create a workout plan that works for you and adapts to your progress.

2. Convenience:

With personal trainer apps, you can workout in the comfort of your own home or on the go. This saves you time and money on gym memberships or personal trainers.

3. Accountability:

Personal trainer apps provide daily reminders and track your progress, helping you stay accountable and motivated to achieve your fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q. Are personal trainer apps suitable for beginners?

A. Yes, personal trainer apps offer customized workout plans for beginners and adapt to your progress.

Q. Can personal trainer apps replace a personal trainer?

A. While personal trainer apps provide customized workout plans, they cannot replace the personalized guidance of a personal trainer.

Q. Are personal trainer apps expensive?

A. Personal trainer apps range from free to $14.99 per month, making them an affordable option for many people.

Q. Can personal trainer apps be used without equipment?

A. Yes, many personal trainer apps offer bodyweight workouts that require no equipment.

Conclusion of Best Personal Trainer Apps for Clients

Personal trainer apps offer a convenient and affordable way to achieve your fitness goals. With customized workout plans, daily reminders, and progress tracking, personal trainer apps provide the accountability and motivation needed to succeed. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there is a personal trainer app available for you.
