Resistance Band Exercises PDF for Upper Body

One of my favorite resistance band exercises pdf for the upper body is the bicep curl. To perform this exercise, stand on the resistance band with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold the handles with your palms facing up. Slowly curl the handles up towards your shoulders, keeping your elbows close to your body. Lower the handles back down to the starting position and repeat for 10-12 reps.

Another great upper body exercise is the overhead press. To perform this exercise, stand on the resistance band with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold the handles at shoulder height with your palms facing forward. Press the handles up overhead, keeping your elbows close to your body. Lower the handles back down to the starting position and repeat for 10-12 reps.

Resistance Band Exercises PDF for Lower Body

One of my favorite resistance band exercises pdf for the lower body is the squat. To perform this exercise, place the resistance band around your thighs, just above your knees. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and squat down, keeping your knees in line with your toes. Stand back up to the starting position and repeat for 10-12 reps.

Another great lower body exercise is the glute bridge. To perform this exercise, lie on your back with your knees bent and the resistance band around your thighs, just above your knees. Lift your hips up towards the ceiling, squeezing your glutes at the top. Lower your hips back down to the starting position and repeat for 10-12 reps.

Tips for Resistance Band Exercises PDF

When performing resistance band exercises pdf, it's important to choose the right level of resistance for your fitness level. Start with a lighter resistance band and gradually work your way up to a heavier one as you get stronger.

It's also important to maintain proper form throughout each exercise. Keep your core engaged and your back straight to avoid injury.
