Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout Strengthen Your Core In No Time

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you struggle to find the right core workout that suits your fitness level? Are you tired of doing the same exercises over and over again? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then the Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout is perfect for you. This workout is specifically designed for those who are just starting their fitness journey or are looking for a low-intensity workout that still delivers results.

The Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout targets your core muscles, which includes your abs, obliques, and lower back. These muscles play a crucial role in stabilizing your body and improving your posture. Weak core muscles can lead to an increased risk of injury and back pain. This workout is perfect for those who want to improve their core strength and stability, reduce the risk of injury, and tone their midsection.

The Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout is a 10-minute routine that includes six exercises. Each exercise targets a specific area of your core muscles, ensuring that every muscle is worked out effectively. This workout is perfect for those who want to get a quick and effective workout in without spending an hour in the gym.

In summary, the Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout is a low-intensity 10-minute workout that targets your core muscles. It is perfect for those who are just starting their fitness journey or are looking for a low-impact routine that still delivers results.

The Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout has numerous benefits, including:

How to Perform Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout?

To perform the Fitness Blender Beginner Core Workout, follow these steps:

Exercise 1: Marching Bridge

Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground. Lift your hips off the ground, creating a straight line from your shoulders to your knees. Alternate lifting your left and right leg while keeping your hips lifted.

Exercise 2: Toe Touch Crunch

Lie on your back with your legs straight up in the air. Lift your upper body off the ground and reach your hands towards your toes.

Exercise 3: Seated Russian Twist

Sit on the ground with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground. Lean back slightly and lift your feet off the ground. Hold a weight or a water bottle with both hands and twist your torso from side to side.

Exercise 4: Bird Dog Crunch

Start on all fours with your hands under your shoulders and knees under your hips. Lift your left arm and right leg off the ground and bring them towards each other, crunching your abs. Return to the starting position and repeat on the other side.

Exercise 5: Pilates Scissors

Lie on your back with your legs straight up in the air. Lower your right leg towards the ground while keeping your left leg straight up. Alternate lowering your legs while keeping your abs engaged.

Exercise 6: Plank

Start in a push-up position with your hands under your shoulders and your body in a straight line. Hold this position for 30-60 seconds, keeping your abs engaged and your hips in line with your shoulders.
