Resistance Band Russian Twist

The resistance band Russian twist is an effective exercise for burning belly fat. To perform this exercise, sit on the floor with your legs bent, and your feet flat on the floor. Hold the resistance band with both hands and twist your torso to the right, then to the left. Repeat for 10-15 reps.

Resistance Band Bicycle Crunch

The resistance band bicycle crunch is another effective exercise for burning belly fat. To perform this exercise, lie on your back with your knees bent and your feet flat on the floor. Hold the resistance band with both hands and lift your shoulders off the floor. Bring your right elbow towards your left knee, then switch sides. Repeat for 10-15 reps.
