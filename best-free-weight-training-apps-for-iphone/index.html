Best Free Weight Training Apps For Iphone

Written by Robby Nov 28, 2023 · 5 min read

If you are someone who loves to hit the gym or do home workouts, you know the importance of having a good workout app on your phone. But with so many options available in the App Store, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best free weight training apps for iPhone that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

The main target of this post is to help fitness enthusiasts find the best free weight training apps for iPhone. We understand the pain points of searching for the right app, which is why we have compiled a list of the top-rated apps that can help you with your weight training and fitness journey.

Here are some of the best free weight training apps for iPhone:

1. Fitbod

Fitbod is a personal training app that creates custom workout plans based on your fitness goals, strength, and available equipment. The app's algorithm generates new workouts every time you use it, ensuring that you never get bored with your routine. Fitbod also tracks your progress, provides detailed exercise instructions, and allows you to connect with other Fitbod users.

2. JEFIT

JEFIT is a popular workout app that offers a variety of features, including personalized workout plans, exercise tracking, and body stat tracking. The app also has a large exercise library with detailed instructions and animations, making it easy to learn new exercises. JEFIT also allows you to connect with other users and join workout challenges.

3. Nike Training Club

The Nike Training Club app offers a variety of workouts, ranging from bodyweight exercises to full gym workouts. The app's workouts are designed by Nike Master Trainers and offer personalized recommendations based on your fitness level and goals. Nike Training Club also offers workout plans, nutrition advice, and allows you to connect with friends for motivation.

4. StrongLifts 5x5

StrongLifts 5x5 is a weightlifting app that focuses on the 5x5 training method. The app offers a simple and effective workout plan, with only three exercises per session. The app also tracks your progress, provides detailed exercise instructions, and allows you to customize your workout plan based on your goals and fitness level.

5. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular fitness app that offers a variety of features, including calorie and macro tracking, exercise tracking, and meal planning. The app also offers a large food database, making it easy to track your nutrition. MyFitnessPal also allows you to connect with friends and join challenges for motivation.

Why You Should Use a Weight Training App

Using a weight training app can have many benefits. Firstly, it can help you stay organized and on track with your workouts. The app can provide you with a personalized workout plan, track your progress, and offer exercise instructions and tips. Secondly, a weight training app can help you stay motivated and accountable. Many apps offer social features, such as connecting with friends and joining challenges, which can help you stay on track with your fitness goals.

How to Choose the Right Weight Training App

When choosing a weight training app, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, look for an app that offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. Secondly, look for an app that has a large exercise library with detailed instructions and animations. Thirdly, consider an app that offers social features, such as connecting with friends and joining challenges.

My Personal Experience with Best Free Weight Training Apps for iPhone

I have been using the Fitbod app for over a year now, and it has been a game-changer for my workouts. The app's personalized workout plans have helped me stay on track with my fitness goals, and I love that the app generates new workouts every time I use it. The app's tracking features also allow me to see my progress and adjust my workouts accordingly. Overall, I highly recommend the Fitbod app for anyone looking for a personalized and effective workout plan.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all the apps mentioned in this post are free to download and use. However, some apps offer premium features that may require a subscription or one-time payment.

Q: Can these apps replace a personal trainer?

A: While these apps can provide you with personalized workout plans and exercise instructions, they cannot replace the knowledge and expertise of a personal trainer. If you are new to weight training or have specific fitness goals, working with a personal trainer may be beneficial.

Q: Do these apps work for all fitness levels?

A: Yes, most of these apps offer personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. However, it is always important to listen to your body and adjust your workouts accordingly.

Q: Can I use these apps for home workouts?

A: Yes, all the apps mentioned in this post offer home workout options that require little to no equipment.

Conclusion of Best Free Weight Training Apps for iPhone

Weight training apps can be a great tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness and achieve their goals. With the variety of apps available in the App Store, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. However, by considering your fitness level, goals, and personal preferences, you can find the best free weight training app for your needs. We hope this post has helped you in your search for the perfect fitness app!
