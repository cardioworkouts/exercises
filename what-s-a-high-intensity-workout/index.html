What s A High Intensity Workout

Written by Robby Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours at the gym and not seeing the results you want? Do you want to challenge yourself and take your fitness to the next level? If so, a high intensity workout may be just what you need.

Many people struggle to see results from traditional workouts because they don't push themselves hard enough. They may be doing the same exercises at the same intensity level every time they hit the gym. This can lead to a plateau in their progress and frustration with their lack of results.

A high intensity workout, on the other hand, is a type of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or lower intensity work. This type of workout is designed to push your body to its limits and can be a great way to break through a plateau and see real results.

In this article, we'll explore what a high intensity workout is, the benefits of this type of exercise, and how to incorporate it into your fitness routine.

My Experience with High Intensity Workouts

As a personal trainer, I've seen firsthand the benefits of high intensity workouts. Not only do they help my clients see results faster, but they also keep their workouts interesting and challenging.

Personally, I love incorporating high intensity workouts into my own fitness routine. Whether it's a HIIT (high intensity interval training) session on the treadmill or a circuit of bodyweight exercises, these workouts always leave me feeling energized and accomplished.

The Benefits of High Intensity Workouts

There are numerous benefits to incorporating high intensity workouts into your fitness routine. Here are just a few:

1. Increased calorie burn: High intensity workouts can help you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time than traditional workouts.

2. Improved cardiovascular health: The intense nature of these workouts can help improve your heart health and increase your endurance.

3. Time-efficient: If you're short on time, a high intensity workout can be a great way to get in a quick, effective workout.

4. Variety: There are countless ways to incorporate high intensity exercises into your workout, which can help keep your routine interesting and prevent boredom.

How to Incorporate High Intensity Workouts into Your Fitness Routine

There are many ways to incorporate high intensity workouts into your fitness routine. Here are a few ideas:

1. HIIT: High intensity interval training involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. This can be done on a treadmill, bike, or with bodyweight exercises.

2. Circuit Training: Create a circuit of exercises that target different muscle groups and perform them back to back with minimal rest.

3. Tabata: This type of workout involves 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for a total of 8 rounds.

Tips for Getting Started with High Intensity Workouts

If you're new to high intensity workouts, here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Start Slow: If you're not used to high intensity exercise, start with shorter workouts and gradually increase the intensity and duration as you get stronger.

2. Listen to Your Body: It's important to push yourself during a high intensity workout, but it's also important to listen to your body and rest when needed.

3. Focus on Form: Proper form is crucial for preventing injury and getting the most out of your workout.

Question and Answer

Q: Can high intensity workouts be dangerous?

A: Like any form of exercise, high intensity workouts can be dangerous if not done properly. It's important to start slow, listen to your body, and focus on proper form to prevent injury.

Q: How often should I do high intensity workouts?

A: This depends on your fitness level and goals. It's recommended to do high intensity workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between.

Q: Can beginners do high intensity workouts?

A: Yes, beginners can do high intensity workouts, but it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration as strength and endurance improves.

Q: Do I need special equipment for high intensity workouts?

A: No, high intensity workouts can be done with bodyweight exercises or simple equipment like dumbbells or resistance bands.

Conclusion of What's a High Intensity Workout

A high intensity workout can be a great way to challenge yourself and see real results in your fitness journey. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, there are countless ways to incorporate high intensity exercises into your routine. Just remember to start slow, listen to your body, and focus on proper form to get the most out of your workout.
