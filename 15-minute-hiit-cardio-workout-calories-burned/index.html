What is a 15 Minute HIIT Cardio Workout?

A 15 minute HIIT cardio workout is a type of exercise that involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest. The goal of this type of training is to get your heart rate up and keep it elevated throughout the workout. This type of workout can be done with or without equipment and can be modified to suit your fitness level.

How to Do a 15 Minute HIIT Cardio Workout

Here is an example of a 15 minute HIIT cardio workout that you can do at home:

  1. Warm up for 2-3 minutes with some light cardio (e.g. jumping jacks, jogging in place)
  2. Do 30 seconds of high-intensity exercise (e.g. burpees, jumping lunges)
  3. Rest for 30 seconds
  4. Repeat steps 2-3 for a total of 5 rounds
  5. Cool down for 2-3 minutes with some stretching

Remember to listen to your body and modify the workout as needed. If you're new to HIIT workouts, you may want to start with shorter intervals or longer rest periods until you build up your fitness level.
