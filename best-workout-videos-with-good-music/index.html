Best Workout Videos With Good Music A Guide To Get Fit And Have Fun

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of boring workout routines? Do you feel like you need motivation to exercise? If you answered yes, then you're in the right place. In this article, we will introduce you to the best workout videos with good music that will help you achieve your fitness goals while enjoying the process.

Working out can be challenging, and sometimes it's hard to stay motivated. But the right music can make all the difference. It can help you push through the tough parts of your workout and make you feel more energized. However, finding the right workout video with good music can be overwhelming, with so many options available online.

That's why we've compiled a list of the best workout videos with good music to make it easier for you to choose. These videos are suitable for people of all fitness levels and can be done at home or in the gym.

1. PopSugar Fitness

PopSugar Fitness is a popular YouTube channel that offers a variety of workout videos with upbeat music. They have a vast collection of videos that range from dance workouts to high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga. They also have videos for specific body parts like abs, legs, and arms. The videos are led by certified trainers who guide you through the exercises, making it easy to follow.

I personally love PopSugar Fitness because the trainers are fun and encouraging, and the music is always on point. I never get bored of their workouts, and I always feel energized after completing them.

2. Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is another YouTube channel that offers free workout videos with good music. The videos are led by husband and wife team Daniel and Kelli, who have over ten years of experience in the fitness industry. They offer a wide range of workouts, from low-impact to high-intensity, and provide modifications for different fitness levels.

Their music selection is also great, and the videos are shot in a way that makes it easy to follow along. I like Fitness Blender because the workouts are challenging but doable, and I always feel like I've had a good workout after completing them.

3. Beachbody

Beachbody is a popular fitness company that offers a variety of workout programs, including P90X, Insanity, and 21-Day Fix. They also have a streaming service called Beachbody On Demand that gives you access to thousands of workout videos with good music. The videos are led by certified trainers and range from dance workouts to weightlifting to yoga.

What I like about Beachbody is that they have a program for everyone, no matter what your fitness level or goals are. Plus, the music is always on point, which makes the workouts more enjoyable.

Why are these the best workout videos with good music?

The workout videos listed above are the best because they offer a variety of workouts that are suitable for people of all fitness levels. The trainers are certified, which means they know what they're doing and can guide you through the exercises safely. Plus, the music is always upbeat and motivational, which helps to keep you going.

How to choose the right workout video with good music?

When choosing a workout video with good music, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, think about what type of workout you enjoy doing. Do you like dancing or prefer something more low-impact? Secondly, consider your fitness level. If you're a beginner, look for videos that offer modifications for different fitness levels. Lastly, listen to the music in the video. Make sure it's something that you enjoy and will motivate you to keep going.

4. Zumba

Zumba is a dance fitness program that has taken the world by storm. It combines Latin and international music with dance moves to create a fun and effective workout. You can find Zumba classes at most gyms, but if you prefer to workout at home, there are also plenty of Zumba videos available online.

I love Zumba because it doesn't feel like a workout. The music is fun and catchy, and the dance moves are easy to follow. Plus, it's a great way to improve your coordination and get your heart rate up.

5. Blogilates

Blogilates is a YouTube channel created by certified fitness instructor Cassey Ho. She offers a wide range of workout videos that focus on Pilates, HIIT, and cardio. The videos are fun and challenging, and the music is always upbeat. Plus, Cassey's energy is infectious, which makes the workouts more enjoyable.

I like Blogilates because the workouts are challenging but doable, and I always feel like I've had a good workout after completing them. Plus, the music is always on point, which makes the workouts more fun.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do these workouts at home?

A: Yes, all of the workouts listed above can be done at home with little to no equipment.

Q: Are these workouts suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all of the workouts listed above offer modifications for different fitness levels, including beginners.

Q: How long are the workouts?

A: The length of the workouts varies depending on the video. Some are as short as 10 minutes, while others are as long as an hour.

Q: Do I need to be coordinated to do these workouts?

A: No, you don't need to be coordinated to do these workouts. The trainers break down the moves and make it easy to follow along.

Conclusion of Best Workout Videos with Good Music

Working out doesn't have to be boring. With the right music and workout video, you can achieve your fitness goals while having fun. The best workout videos with good music listed above offer a variety of workouts that are suitable for people of all fitness levels. So, whether you prefer dancing or weightlifting, there's a workout video out there for you.
