How Many Calories Should You Burn From Cardio

Written by William Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you trying to lose weight or maintain a healthy lifestyle? Cardio exercises are a great way to achieve your fitness goals, but are you burning enough calories? In this article, we will discuss the recommended number of calories you should burn from cardio and provide tips on how to maximize your workout.

When it comes to cardio exercises, the main goal is to burn calories. However, determining the ideal number of calories you should burn can be difficult since it depends on several factors such as your age, weight, height, and fitness level. Additionally, the type of cardio exercise you do can also affect the amount of calories you burn.

The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises such as brisk walking, swimming, or cycling per week. To achieve weight loss, you need to burn more calories than you consume. A general rule of thumb is to burn 500 to 1,000 calories per day through exercise and diet to lose 1 to 2 pounds per week.

In summary, the recommended number of calories you should burn from cardio varies depending on your fitness goals, body composition, and exercise routine. However, the American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises per week.

How to Calculate the Calories Burned from Cardio

Calculating the number of calories you burn from cardio exercises can help you track your progress and adjust your workout routine accordingly. A heart rate monitor or fitness tracker can give you a more accurate estimate of the calories burned during your workout.

However, if you don't have access to these tools, you can use online calculators or formulas to estimate the calories burned based on your weight, duration, and intensity of the exercise. For example, a 150-pound person can burn approximately 300 calories during 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cycling.

The Benefits of Cardio Exercises

Aside from weight loss, cardio exercises have numerous health benefits such as improved heart health, reduced risk of chronic diseases, and increased energy levels. Additionally, cardio exercises can also boost your mood and reduce stress levels.

Personally, I enjoy doing cardio exercises such as jogging and dancing. I find that these activities not only improve my physical health but also my mental wellbeing.

Tips to Maximize Your Cardio Workout

If you want to maximize the number of calories you burn from cardio exercises, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose high-intensity exercises such as sprinting, HIIT, or circuit training
  • Incorporate strength training to build muscle and increase your metabolism
  • Vary your workout routine to prevent boredom and plateau
  • Stay hydrated and fuel your body with nutritious foods
  • Get enough rest and recovery time to avoid burnout and injury

The Bottom Line

Cardio exercises are a great way to burn calories and improve your overall health. However, the recommended number of calories you should burn depends on various factors such as your fitness goals and body composition. Remember to listen to your body and consult with a healthcare provider or fitness professional before starting a new exercise routine.

FAQs About How Many Calories Should You Burn From Cardio

Q: Can I lose weight by doing cardio exercises alone?

A: While cardio exercises can help you burn calories and lose weight, incorporating strength training and a healthy diet is essential for optimal results.

Q: How often should I do cardio exercises?

A: The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises per week, which can be divided into 30-minute sessions, five times a week.

Q: Is it better to do cardio exercises in the morning or evening?

A: The best time to do cardio exercises depends on your personal preference and schedule. Some people prefer to do it in the morning for an energy boost, while others prefer to do it in the evening to relieve stress.

Q: How long does it take to see results from cardio exercises?

A: The results of cardio exercises can vary depending on your fitness level and consistency. However, you can expect to see improvements in your cardiovascular health and energy levels within a few weeks of regular exercise.

Conclusion of How Many Calories Should You Burn From Cardio

In conclusion, the recommended number of calories you should burn from cardio exercises depends on various factors such as your fitness goals, body composition, and exercise routine. However, the American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercises per week to improve your overall health and wellbeing. Remember to consult with a healthcare provider or fitness professional before starting a new exercise routine.
