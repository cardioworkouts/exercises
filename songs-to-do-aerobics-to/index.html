Songs To Do Aerobics To Get Your Heart Pumping And Your Body Moving

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of doing the same old workout routine? Do you struggle to find the motivation to exercise? Adding music to your workout can be a game-changer. In this post, we will discuss songs to do aerobics to and how they can help you get your heart pumping and your body moving.

What are Songs to Do Aerobics to?

Songs to do aerobics to are upbeat and energetic tunes that are perfect for getting your heart rate up and your body moving. These songs are specifically chosen for their tempo and rhythm, making them ideal for aerobic activities like dancing, jogging, or jumping jacks.

When you exercise to music, it can increase your endurance and performance by up to 15%. Music can also distract you from the discomfort of exercise and help you push through the pain. Plus, it's just more fun to exercise to music!

Top Songs to Do Aerobics to

Here are some of the top songs to do aerobics to:

1. "Uptown Funk" by Mark Ronson ft. Bruno Mars

This catchy tune has a fast tempo and a great beat that will keep you moving throughout your workout. Plus, the lyrics are fun and upbeat, making it perfect for a high-energy exercise routine.

2. "Can't Stop the Feeling" by Justin Timberlake

This feel-good song is perfect for getting your heart rate up and your body moving. The upbeat tempo and positive lyrics will keep you motivated throughout your entire workout.

3. "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor

This classic tune is a favorite among fitness enthusiasts. The fast-paced beat and motivational lyrics will help you push through even the toughest of workouts.

4. "Shape of You" by Ed Sheeran

This upbeat song has a great tempo and a catchy beat that will keep you moving from start to finish. The lyrics are also fun and playful, making it perfect for a fun and lighthearted workout.

5. "I Gotta Feeling" by Black Eyed Peas

This song is a classic for a reason. The fast-paced beat and energetic lyrics will have you feeling like you can conquer the world. Plus, the chorus is perfect for a group workout session.

How to Choose the Right Songs to Do Aerobics to

When choosing songs to do aerobics to, it's important to consider the tempo and rhythm of the music. The ideal tempo for aerobic activities is between 120-140 beats per minute. You should also choose songs that you enjoy and that motivate you to move.

Another factor to consider is the length of the song. If you're doing a high-intensity workout, you may want to choose shorter songs that allow you to switch up your routine more frequently. If you're doing a longer workout, you may want to choose longer songs that can help you stay motivated throughout the entire workout.

The Benefits of Exercising to Music

Exercising to music has numerous benefits, including:

  • Increased endurance and performance
  • Distraction from discomfort and pain
  • Improved mood and motivation
  • More enjoyable workout experience

Tips for Exercising to Music

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your workout with music:

  • Choose songs with a fast tempo and upbeat rhythm
  • Make a playlist of your favorite songs to keep you motivated
  • Switch up your playlist to keep your workout fresh and exciting
  • Use wireless headphones to avoid getting tangled up in cords

Conclusion of Songs to Do Aerobics to

Adding music to your workout can make a huge difference in your motivation and performance. Choose songs with a fast tempo and upbeat rhythm to get your heart pumping and your body moving. Experiment with different songs and playlists to find what works best for you. With the right music, you can turn your workout into a fun and enjoyable experience.
