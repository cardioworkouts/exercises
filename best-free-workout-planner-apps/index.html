Best Free Workout Planner Apps A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to plan and track your workouts without breaking the bank? Look no further than the best free workout planner apps available on the market today. These apps offer a variety of features that can help you stay motivated, reach your fitness goals, and get the most out of your workouts.

For many people, sticking to a workout routine can be challenging. Whether it's finding the motivation to hit the gym or simply keeping track of your progress, there are a number of pain points that can make it difficult to achieve your fitness goals. Fortunately, there are a variety of workout planner apps available to help you overcome these challenges.

The target of best free workout planner apps is to provide users with a way to plan and track their workouts, set goals, and stay motivated. Some of the best free workout planner apps available today include Fitbod, JEFIT, MyFitnessPal, and Nike Training Club.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at these apps and their features, as well as provide tips for choosing the best workout planner app for your needs.

Fitbod

Fitbod is one of the most popular workout planner apps on the market today. This app uses artificial intelligence to create personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. With over 400 exercises to choose from, Fitbod offers a wide range of options for users of all fitness levels.

Personally, I love the way Fitbod adapts to my fitness level and provides me with challenging but achievable workouts. The app also offers detailed instructions and videos for each exercise, making it easy to learn proper form and technique.

JEFIT

JEFIT is another popular workout planner app that offers a variety of features for users. This app allows you to create customized workout plans, track your progress, and connect with other users for support and motivation. JEFIT also offers a large database of exercises and workouts, making it easy to find the perfect routine for your needs.

One of the things I appreciate about JEFIT is its focus on strength training. As someone who primarily lifts weights, I find the app's exercise database and tracking features to be incredibly helpful for staying on top of my workouts.

MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a workout planner app, MyFitnessPal is a great tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness and nutrition. This app allows you to track your meals and exercise, set goals, and connect with other users for support and motivation.

Personally, I find MyFitnessPal's food tracking feature to be incredibly helpful for staying on track with my nutrition goals. The app also integrates with other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and MapMyFitness, making it easy to keep all of your health and fitness data in one place.

Nike Training Club

If you're looking for a workout planner app with a focus on bodyweight exercises and at-home workouts, Nike Training Club is a great option. This app offers a variety of guided workouts led by professional trainers, as well as personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

Personally, I love using Nike Training Club when I'm traveling or don't have access to a gym. The app's workouts are challenging but fun, and the video demonstrations make it easy to learn proper form and technique.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed in this article offer a free version with basic features. However, some apps also offer a premium version with additional features for a fee.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm a beginner?

A: Absolutely! Many of these apps offer beginner-friendly workouts and personalized plans based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Do I need any special equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps, such as Fitbod, offer workouts that can be done with just bodyweight. Others, such as JEFIT, offer workouts that require gym equipment. Be sure to check the app's requirements before downloading.

Q: Can I use these apps to track my progress?

A: Yes! All of the apps listed in this article offer some form of progress tracking, whether it's through a built-in feature or by syncing with other fitness apps.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Planner Apps

Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned fitness enthusiast, the best free workout planner apps can help you stay on track and reach your fitness goals. By choosing an app that fits your needs and preferences, you can create a workout plan that works for you and stay motivated along the way. So what are you waiting for? Download one of these apps today and start crushing your fitness goals!
