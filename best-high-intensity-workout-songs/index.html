Best High Intensity Workout Songs Pump Up Your Exercise Game

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 · 5 min read

You know the feeling when you're at the gym, and you're just not feeling motivated to push yourself through those last few reps or minutes on the treadmill. It can be challenging to find the drive to keep going, especially during a high-intensity workout. But, did you know that music can be a powerful motivator during exercise? In this post, we'll be discussing the best high intensity workout songs to help you power through your next workout.

When it comes to high-intensity workouts, having the right music can make all the difference. Music can help you focus, boost your mood, and give you the energy you need to push yourself harder. But with so many songs out there, how do you know which ones are the best for an intense workout?

The target of this post is to help you find the best high intensity workout songs that will keep you motivated and energized throughout your workout. Best High Intensity Workout Song #1: "Lose Control" by Missy Elliott

One of our favorite high-energy songs is "Lose Control" by Missy Elliott. This song has a fast beat and a catchy chorus that will keep you moving throughout your workout. The lyrics are also empowering and motivational, making it an excellent choice for a high-intensity workout. Plus, who doesn't love a good throwback song?

Best High Intensity Workout Song #2: "Can't Hold Us" by Macklemore & Ryan Lewis

"Can't Hold Us" is another song with a fast beat and an epic chorus that will keep you motivated during your workout. This song is perfect for high-intensity cardio exercises like running, cycling, or jumping jacks. The lyrics also have a powerful message about perseverance and overcoming obstacles, making it a great choice for a challenging workout.

Why Music is Great for High-Intensity Workouts

There are several reasons why music is an excellent motivator during high-intensity workouts. For one, music can distract you from the pain and fatigue of exercise, allowing you to push yourself further than you might have thought possible. Music can also regulate your breathing and heart rate, helping you to maintain a consistent pace throughout your workout. Additionally, music can help you get in the zone and focus on your workout, improving your performance and reducing the risk of injury.

How to Choose the Best High Intensity Workout Songs

When choosing the best high intensity workout songs, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for songs with a fast tempo and a strong beat. These songs will help you maintain a consistent pace and stay energized throughout your workout. Second, choose songs with motivational lyrics that will inspire you to push yourself harder. Finally, choose songs that you enjoy listening to and that make you feel good. Music is a personal preference, so finding songs that you love can make a big difference in your workout.

Best High Intensity Workout Song #3: "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor

You can't talk about high-intensity workout songs without mentioning "Eye of the Tiger" by Survivor. This classic song has been used in countless workout montages and is a staple of any high-energy playlist. The driving beat and motivational lyrics make it the perfect song for pushing through those tough workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: Can listening to music during a workout improve your performance?
A: Yes, studies have shown that listening to music during exercise can improve performance, increase endurance, and reduce perceived exertion.

Q: What tempo should high-intensity workout songs have?
A: High-intensity workout songs should have a tempo of at least 120 beats per minute (BPM) to help you maintain a fast pace and stay energized.

Q: Can listening to music while working out reduce the risk of injury?
A: Yes, music can help you get in the zone and focus on your movements, reducing the risk of injury and improving your form.

Q: Are there any specific genres of music that are best for high-intensity workouts?
A: No, the best high-intensity workout songs can come from any genre of music. It's all about finding songs with a fast tempo, strong beat, and motivational lyrics that work for you.

Conclusion of Best High Intensity Workout Songs

Music can be a powerful motivator during high-intensity workouts, helping you to push yourself harder and stay energized throughout your routine. By choosing the best high intensity workout songs for your playlist, you can take your exercise game to the next level. Whether you prefer classic rock, hip-hop, or pop music, there's a high-energy song out there that's perfect for your workout. So, start building your playlist today and get ready to crush your next high-intensity workout!
