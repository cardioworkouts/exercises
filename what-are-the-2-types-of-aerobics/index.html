What Are The 2 Types Of Aerobics

Written by Daniel Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Aerobics is a form of physical exercise that involves rhythmic movements and incorporates various endurance activities like running, swimming, cycling, and dancing. However, there are two types of aerobics that are commonly practiced – high-impact aerobics and low-impact aerobics.

Are you looking to get fit and wondering what type of aerobics is right for you? In this blog post, we will discuss the two types of aerobics and help you determine which one is best suited for your fitness goals.

High-impact aerobics involve movements that put pressure on your joints and involve jumping or bouncing. Low-impact aerobics, on the other hand, require at least one foot to be in contact with the ground at all times. Both types of aerobics provide a great cardiovascular workout, but there are some key differences between the two.

If you are looking for an intense workout that involves jumping and bouncing, high-impact aerobics might be the best option for you. This type of aerobics is perfect for those who want to burn calories quickly and improve their cardiovascular fitness. Low-impact aerobics, on the other hand, is a great option for those who want to exercise without putting too much strain on their joints.

My Experience with High-Impact Aerobics

When I first started working out, I was drawn to high-impact aerobics because of its intensity. I loved the feeling of jumping and bouncing around the room, and I always left the class feeling energized and accomplished. However, I soon realized that this type of aerobics was taking a toll on my joints. I often experienced knee pain after class and had to take a break from high-impact aerobics to allow my body to recover.

My Experience with Low-Impact Aerobics

After taking a break from high-impact aerobics, I decided to try low-impact aerobics. At first, I was skeptical that this type of exercise would provide the same intensity as high-impact aerobics. However, I was pleasantly surprised. I found that I could still get a great workout without putting too much pressure on my joints. Plus, I didn't experience any knee pain after class.

The Benefits of High-Impact Aerobics

High-impact aerobics is a great way to burn calories and improve your cardiovascular fitness. This type of exercise is perfect for those who want to challenge themselves and push their limits. It can also help improve bone density and reduce the risk of osteoporosis.

The Benefits of Low-Impact Aerobics

Low-impact aerobics is an excellent option for those who want to exercise without putting too much pressure on their joints. This type of exercise can still provide a great workout and improve cardiovascular fitness. It is also ideal for people who are recovering from an injury.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I lose weight by doing aerobics?

A: Yes, aerobics is an excellent way to burn calories and lose weight.

Q: Is high-impact aerobics bad for my joints?

A: High-impact aerobics can be hard on your joints, especially if you have any pre-existing injuries. It is important to listen to your body and take breaks when needed.

Q: Can I do low-impact aerobics if I have an injury?

A: Yes, low-impact aerobics is an excellent option for people who are recovering from an injury or have joint pain.

Q: How often should I do aerobics?

A: It is recommended to do aerobic exercise for at least 150 minutes per week.

Conclusion of What are the 2 Types of Aerobics

Whether you choose high-impact or low-impact aerobics, both types of exercise provide a great cardiovascular workout. It is important to listen to your body and choose the type of aerobics that is best suited to your fitness goals and physical abilities. With regular exercise, you can improve your overall health and fitness and enjoy the many benefits that aerobics has to offer.
