What Workout Burns The Most Calories On Apple Watch A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you own an Apple Watch and want to know what workout burns the most calories? You're in the right place! Burning calories is an essential part of any weight loss journey, and with the help of your Apple Watch, you can track your progress and achieve your fitness goals. In this blog post, we'll discuss the best workouts to burn calories on Apple Watch and how to maximize your calorie burn during exercise.

When it comes to weight loss, one of the biggest challenges is burning calories efficiently. Many people struggle to find the right workout that fits their lifestyle and helps them reach their goals. With the help of Apple Watch, you can take the guesswork out of your fitness routine and track your progress in real-time. But what workout burns the most calories on Apple Watch?

The answer to this question is not straightforward, as different workouts burn different amounts of calories depending on various factors such as age, gender, weight, and intensity level. However, some workouts are known to be more effective at burning calories than others. These include high-intensity interval training (HIIT), running, cycling, swimming, and rowing.

In summary, the best workout to burn the most calories on Apple Watch is a combination of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and cardio exercises such as running, cycling, swimming, or rowing. However, the number of calories burned during a workout can vary depending on several factors.

The Benefits of High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

HIIT is a popular workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of workout is known to burn more calories in less time than traditional cardio exercises. When you perform HIIT, your body continues to burn calories even after you've finished exercising, thanks to the afterburn effect. This means that you can continue to burn calories even while resting.

Personally, I love doing HIIT workouts on my Apple Watch. It's a convenient way to get in a quick, effective workout that I can do anywhere, anytime. I usually start with a quick warm-up, followed by a series of high-intensity exercises such as jumping jacks, burpees, or mountain climbers, and then a short rest period. I repeat this cycle for around 20-30 minutes, and I'm done!

The Benefits of Running, Cycling, Swimming, and Rowing

Running, cycling, swimming, and rowing are all excellent cardio exercises that can help you burn a significant amount of calories. Running, in particular, is known to burn the most calories per minute compared to other cardio exercises. Cycling is also a great way to burn calories, especially if you incorporate sprints or hill climbs into your workout. Swimming and rowing are low-impact exercises that can be easier on your joints while still providing a full-body workout.

Personally, I enjoy running and cycling on my Apple Watch. I use the Workout app to track my progress and set goals for myself. I also love the Outdoor Walk workout, which tracks my walking pace and distance while allowing me to enjoy the great outdoors.

How to Maximize Your Calorie Burn on Apple Watch

If you want to maximize your calorie burn on Apple Watch, here are a few tips:

  • Make sure your Apple Watch is properly calibrated for your height, weight, and age.
  • Choose a workout that challenges you without causing injury or strain.
  • Incorporate high-intensity intervals into your workout to increase calorie burn.
  • Use the Workout app to track your progress and set goals for yourself.
  • Stay hydrated and fuel your body with healthy foods to support your fitness goals.

How to Start Your HIIT Workout on Apple Watch

If you're new to HIIT workouts on Apple Watch, here's how to get started:

  1. Open the Workout app on your Apple Watch.
  2. Select the workout type that best fits your routine (e.g., High-Intensity Interval Training).
  3. Set your goals for time, distance, or calorie burn.
  4. Start your workout and follow the on-screen prompts to complete each exercise.
  5. Finish your workout and save your progress to track your results over time.

Question and Answer

Q: How many calories can I burn during a HIIT workout on Apple Watch?

A: The number of calories burned during a HIIT workout on Apple Watch depends on several factors such as age, weight, gender, and intensity level. However, a typical 30-minute HIIT workout can burn anywhere from 200-500 calories.

Q: Can I lose weight by doing cardio exercises on Apple Watch?

A: Yes, you can lose weight by incorporating cardio exercises into your fitness routine on Apple Watch. When combined with a healthy diet, regular cardio exercise can help you burn calories and shed excess weight.

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts on Apple Watch?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts on Apple Watch 2-3 times per week to allow your body to rest and recover between workouts.

Q: Can I track my nutrition and calorie intake on Apple Watch?

A: Yes, you can track your nutrition and calorie intake on Apple Watch using apps like MyFitnessPal or Lose It!. These apps sync with Apple Health and allow you to log your meals and track your macronutrient intake.

Conclusion of what workout burns the most calories on Apple Watch

Burning calories and achieving your fitness goals is easier with the help of Apple Watch. By incorporating high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and cardio exercises such as running, cycling, swimming, or rowing into your fitness routine, you can burn more calories and achieve your weight loss goals. Make sure to track your progress using the Workout app and follow a healthy diet to support your fitness goals.
