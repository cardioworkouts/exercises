How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights

Written by Aprilia Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone who hits the gym regularly and spends hours working out? If so, you must have wondered how long should you do cardio after lifting weights. It's a common question that most fitness enthusiasts have. In this blog post, we will go over how long you should do cardio after lifting weights and why it's essential for your fitness journey.

Pain Points Related to How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights

One of the biggest pain points related to how long should you do cardio after lifting weights is that people tend to choose either cardio or weightlifting. They believe that doing any one of these things is enough to maintain their fitness level. However, it's not entirely true. Both cardio and weightlifting have their benefits and are essential to maintain a healthy body.

Answer to How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights

Typically, you should do cardio after lifting weights for at least 20-30 minutes. Doing cardio after lifting weights will help you burn more calories and fat. It will also assist in improving your overall health and stamina. However, it's important to note that the duration of cardio after lifting weights depends on your fitness level and the type of workout you're doing. If you're a beginner, start with a shorter duration and gradually increase it as you get fitter.

Summary of How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights

So, in summary, the answer to how long should you do cardio after lifting weights is 20-30 minutes. However, the duration depends on your fitness level and workout type. Combining both cardio and weightlifting is essential to maintain overall fitness and health.

How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights - Personal Experience

Before I started working out, I used to do cardio for 45 minutes straight. However, after joining the gym, I realized that doing cardio after lifting weights for 20-30 minutes is enough. Initially, I struggled to keep up with the routine, but gradually I started seeing improvements in my fitness level. Now, I make sure to do at least 20 minutes of cardio after lifting weights, and it has helped me tremendously in maintaining my fitness level.

The Importance of Doing Cardio After Lifting Weights

Doing cardio after lifting weights is essential for several reasons. Firstly, it helps in burning more calories and fat, leading to weight loss. Secondly, it increases your endurance and stamina, allowing you to push yourself harder during workouts. Lastly, it helps in maintaining your overall fitness level and keeps your heart healthy.

The Ideal Cardio Workout After Lifting Weights

There are several types of cardio workouts that you can do after lifting weights. However, the ideal cardio workout after lifting weights is high-intensity interval training (HIIT). HIIT is a type of cardio exercise that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. It's an excellent way to burn calories and fat, and it takes less time than traditional cardio workouts.

Tips for Doing Cardio After Lifting Weights

If you're planning to do cardio after lifting weights, here are some tips that you can follow:

  • Make sure to warm up before doing cardio
  • Start with a shorter duration and gradually increase it
  • Choose a type of cardio that you enjoy doing
  • Stay hydrated during the workout
  • Cool down and stretch after finishing the workout

How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights - Personal Experience

One thing that I've learned from my personal experience is that doing cardio after lifting weights has helped me in maintaining my fitness level. Initially, I struggled to keep up with the routine, but gradually I started seeing improvements in my endurance and stamina. Now, I make sure to do at least 20 minutes of cardio after lifting weights, and it has helped me tremendously in reaching my fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I do cardio before lifting weights?

A. Yes, you can do cardio before lifting weights. However, it's recommended to do cardio after lifting weights as it helps in burning more calories and fat.

Q. How often should I do cardio after lifting weights?

A. It depends on your fitness goals and workout routine. Ideally, you should do cardio after lifting weights 2-3 times a week.

Q. How long should I rest between lifting weights and cardio?

A. You should rest for 5-10 minutes between lifting weights and cardio. It will help you recover and get ready for the cardio workout.

Q. How can I make cardio after lifting weights more effective?

A. You can make cardio after lifting weights more effective by choosing a high-intensity workout, staying hydrated, and warming up before the workout.

Conclusion of How Long Should You Do Cardio After Lifting Weights

Doing cardio after lifting weights is essential for maintaining overall fitness and health. The ideal duration of cardio after lifting weights is 20-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and workout type. Combining both cardio and weightlifting is crucial to achieve your fitness goals. Follow the tips mentioned in this blog post, and you'll be on your way to a healthier and fitter body.
