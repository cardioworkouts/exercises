Best Free Workout Gym Apps Get Fit Without Breaking The Bank

Written by Eveline Jun 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to stay fit without breaking the bank? With the rising cost of gym memberships and personal trainers, it can be difficult to find an affordable way to stay in shape. Luckily, there are a variety of free workout gym apps available that can help you achieve your fitness goals without spending a dime.

For many people, the thought of going to the gym can be intimidating. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, finding the right workout routine can be a challenge. This is where workout gym apps come in. They provide a convenient way to access a variety of workouts, track your progress, and stay motivated.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a popular workout app that offers a variety of free workouts for users of all fitness levels. The app includes over 185 workouts and training plans designed by Nike Master Trainers. You can choose from strength, endurance, yoga, and mobility workouts, and customize your plan based on your fitness goals and preferences. The app also includes audio and video guidance, so you can follow along with the workouts and get real-time feedback on your form.

Personally, I've been using Nike Training Club for the past year and have seen significant improvements in my overall fitness level. The app is easy to use and the workouts are challenging but manageable. Plus, the app allows you to connect with friends and share your progress, which can help keep you motivated.

2. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that allows you to track your food intake and exercise. The app includes a database of over 11 million foods, so you can easily log your meals and track your calories. In addition, the app includes a variety of workouts and training plans designed by fitness experts. You can choose from cardio, strength, and flexibility workouts, and customize your plan based on your fitness goals.

I've been using MyFitnessPal for several years now and have found it to be a helpful tool for staying on track with my fitness goals. The app allows you to set daily calorie and macronutrient goals, and provides real-time feedback on your progress. Plus, the app includes a social component that allows you to connect with friends and share your progress.

3. FitOn

FitOn is a newer workout app that offers a variety of free workouts for users of all fitness levels. The app includes a variety of workouts, including HIIT, yoga, Pilates, and strength training. You can choose from a variety of trainers and customize your plan based on your fitness goals and preferences. The app also includes a social component that allows you to connect with friends and share your progress.

I recently started using FitOn and have been impressed with the variety of workouts and the quality of the trainers. The app is easy to use and the workouts are challenging but doable. Plus, the app includes a variety of features, such as progress tracking and personalized meal plans, that can help you reach your fitness goals.

Why Use Workout Gym Apps?

There are many reasons to use workout gym apps, including:

  • Convenience: You can access workouts anytime, anywhere
  • Cost: Many workout gym apps are free or low-cost
  • Variety: You can choose from a variety of workouts and trainers
  • Customization: You can customize your plan based on your fitness goals and preferences
  • Motivation: Many workout gym apps include social features that can help keep you motivated

How to Choose the Best Free Workout Gym App?

When choosing a free workout gym app, consider the following factors:

  • Workout options: Look for an app that offers a variety of workouts
  • Trainers: Look for an app that includes workouts designed by qualified trainers
  • Customization: Look for an app that allows you to customize your plan based on your fitness goals and preferences
  • Social features: Look for an app that includes social features that can help keep you motivated
  • User reviews: Look for an app that has positive user reviews

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Gym Apps

Overall, there are many free workout gym apps available that can help you achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there's an app out there that can help you stay on track and stay motivated. So why wait? Download a free workout gym app today and start your fitness journey!

Question and Answer

Q: Are all free workout gym apps safe to use?

A: While most free workout gym apps are safe to use, it's important to do your research and read user reviews before downloading an app. Look for apps that have positive user reviews and are designed by qualified trainers.

Q: Can I get a good workout using a free workout gym app?

A: Absolutely! Many free workout gym apps offer challenging workouts that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use a free workout gym app?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps offer bodyweight workouts that require no equipment, while others may require dumbbells, resistance bands, or other equipment.

Q: Can I customize my workout plan using a free workout gym app?

A: Yes! Many free workout gym apps allow you to customize your plan based on your fitness goals and preferences.
