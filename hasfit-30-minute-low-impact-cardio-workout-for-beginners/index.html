How to Do Hasfit 30 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do Hasfit's 30 minute low impact cardio workout for beginners:

  1. Start with a warm-up: Do some light stretching and gentle movements to get your body warmed up.
  2. Jumping Jacks: Do 30 seconds of jumping jacks to get your heart rate up.
  3. Lunges: Do 10 lunges on each leg.
  4. Knee Raises: Do 20 knee raises, 10 on each side.
  5. Squats: Do 10 squats.
  6. Step-Ups: Do 10 step-ups on each leg.
  7. Wall Push-Ups: Do 10 wall push-ups.
  8. Tricep Dips: Do 10 tricep dips.
  9. Cool Down: Finish off with some light stretching to cool down your body.

Tips for Making the Most of Hasfit 30 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of Hasfit's 30 minute low impact cardio workout for beginners:

  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after the workout.
  • Wear comfortable clothing: Wear loose-fitting, comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement.
  • Listen to your body: If you feel any pain or discomfort, stop the workout immediately.
