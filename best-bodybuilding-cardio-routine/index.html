Best Bodybuilding Cardio Routine A Guide For Beginners

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in bodybuilding but find yourself struggling with incorporating cardio into your routine? You're not alone. Many bodybuilders struggle with finding the right balance between strength training and cardio. In this post, we'll explore the best bodybuilding cardio routine for beginners and how it can benefit your overall fitness goals.

When it comes to bodybuilding, cardio is often seen as the enemy. Many bodybuilders believe that cardio will hinder their muscle growth and strength gains. However, this is far from the truth. Incorporating cardio into your routine can actually benefit your bodybuilding goals in many ways.

What is the Best Bodybuilding Cardio Routine?

The best bodybuilding cardio routine for beginners is a combination of high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and steady-state cardio. HIIT is a type of cardio that involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. Steady-state cardio, on the other hand, involves maintaining a consistent pace of moderate-to-high intensity exercise for an extended period of time.

By combining these two types of cardio, you can maximize your fat-burning potential while also improving your cardiovascular endurance. This will not only help you achieve a leaner physique but also improve your overall health and fitness.

My Personal Experience with the Best Bodybuilding Cardio Routine

As a beginner bodybuilder, I struggled with incorporating cardio into my routine. I was afraid that it would hinder my muscle growth and strength gains. However, after doing some research and consulting with a personal trainer, I decided to give HIIT and steady-state cardio a try.

At first, it was challenging. HIIT was intense and left me feeling exhausted, while steady-state cardio was boring and felt like it was taking forever. However, as I continued with the routine, I began to notice significant changes in my body. I was leaner, had more endurance, and was able to lift heavier weights in my strength training sessions.

Overall, I highly recommend incorporating a combination of HIIT and steady-state cardio into your bodybuilding routine. It may be challenging at first, but the benefits are well worth it.

Tips for Incorporating the Best Bodybuilding Cardio Routine into Your Workout

Here are some tips to help you incorporate the best bodybuilding cardio routine into your workout:

1. Start Slow

If you're new to cardio, start slow. Begin with one or two days of steady-state cardio per week and gradually increase the intensity and frequency as your fitness level improves.

2. Mix It Up

Don't be afraid to mix up your cardio routine. Incorporate different types of cardio, such as cycling, running, or rowing, to keep things interesting and prevent boredom.

3. Track Your Progress

Keep track of your cardio progress by monitoring your heart rate, distance, and time. This will help you see improvements in your cardiovascular endurance and keep you motivated to continue.

Question and Answer

Q: Will cardio hinder my muscle growth?

A: No, if done correctly, cardio can actually benefit your muscle growth by improving your cardiovascular endurance and allowing you to lift heavier weights in your strength training sessions.

Q: How often should I do cardio?

A: It depends on your fitness goals and current fitness level. As a beginner, start with one or two days of cardio per week and gradually increase as your fitness level improves.

Q: What is the best time to do cardio?

A: The best time to do cardio is whenever it fits best into your schedule. Some people prefer to do cardio in the morning, while others prefer to do it in the evening. The most important thing is to make sure you do it consistently.

Q: How long should my cardio sessions be?

A: Again, it depends on your fitness goals and current fitness level. As a beginner, aim for 20-30 minutes of cardio per session and gradually increase as your fitness level improves.

Conclusion of Best Bodybuilding Cardio Routine

Overall, incorporating a combination of HIIT and steady-state cardio into your bodybuilding routine can have numerous benefits for your overall health and fitness. Start slow, mix it up, and track your progress to see the best results. With dedication and consistency, you can achieve your bodybuilding goals and improve your overall fitness.
