Best Free Strength Training Apps For Android

Written by Robby Oct 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to improve your strength training routine without breaking the bank? Look no further than free strength training apps for your Android device. These apps can help you track your progress, customize your workouts, and stay motivated along the way.

But with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for you. In this article, we'll explore the best free strength training apps for Android and highlight their unique features.

Here are some of the best free strength training apps for Android:

1. JEFIT

JEFIT is a comprehensive workout planner and tracker that offers a wide range of strength training exercises. You can customize your workout routine, track your progress, and connect with a community of other fitness enthusiasts. With JEFIT, you can also create your own workout plans or choose from pre-made plans designed by fitness professionals.

2. StrongLifts 5x5

If you're looking for a simple and straightforward strength training app, StrongLifts 5x5 might be for you. This app focuses on five compound exercises (squat, bench press, deadlift, overhead press, and barbell row) and offers a 12-week program to help you increase your strength. With StrongLifts 5x5, you can track your progress and receive reminders for your next workout.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a personalized workout app that adapts to your fitness level and goals. It offers a variety of strength training exercises and creates custom workouts based on your equipment, experience, and available time. With Fitbod, you can track your progress and receive personalized guidance to help you reach your goals.

4. Gym Workout Planner

Gym Workout Planner is a simple and easy-to-use app that helps you plan and track your strength training workouts. It offers a variety of exercises and allows you to create custom workout plans based on your preferences. With Gym Workout Planner, you can also track your progress and set reminders for your next workout.

Overall, these free strength training apps for Android offer a range of features to help you improve your fitness routine. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, there's an app out there for you.

I personally use JEFIT to track my strength training progress. I love the app's exercise library, which includes detailed instructions and animations for each exercise. I also appreciate the ability to create my own workout plans and track my progress over time. The app's community feature is also a great way to connect with other fitness enthusiasts and stay motivated.

StrongLifts 5x5: A Personal Experience

One of my friends swears by StrongLifts 5x5 for his strength training routine. He likes the simplicity of the app and the focus on compound exercises. He also appreciates the app's reminders for his next workout, which help him stay on track with his fitness goals.

The Benefits of Strength Training

Strength training offers a range of benefits for your physical and mental health. It can help you build muscle mass, increase your metabolism, and improve your overall fitness level. Strength training can also boost your mood, reduce stress, and improve your sleep quality.

How to Get Started with Strength Training

If you're new to strength training, it's important to start with a workout plan that's appropriate for your fitness level. You can also consult with a personal trainer or fitness professional to help you get started. It's also important to warm up before your workout and to use proper form to prevent injury.

Fitbod: A Personal Experience

A friend of mine recently started using Fitbod for her strength training routine. She loves the app's personalized workout plans, which are tailored to her fitness level and goals. She also appreciates the app's tracking features, which help her stay accountable and motivated.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these free strength training apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these apps offer a range of exercises and workout plans that are suitable for beginners. However, it's important to start with a workout plan that's appropriate for your fitness level and to use proper form to prevent injury.

Q: Can I use these apps without any equipment?

A: Yes, many of these apps offer bodyweight exercises that don't require any equipment. However, some workouts may require equipment such as dumbbells, barbells, or resistance bands.

Q: Are these apps available on iOS?

A: Yes, many of these apps are also available on iOS devices.

Q: Can I use these apps if I have a medical condition?

A: It's important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new exercise routine, especially if you have a medical condition that may affect your ability to exercise safely.

Conclusion of Best Free Strength Training Apps for Android

Overall, free strength training apps for Android can be a great way to improve your fitness routine without breaking the bank. These apps offer a range of features to help you track your progress, customize your workouts, and stay motivated along the way. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, there's an app out there for you.
