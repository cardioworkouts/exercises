Best Tabata Bodyweight Workout A Beginner s Guide

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a quick and effective way to get in shape? Look no further than Tabata bodyweight workouts. This high-intensity interval training (HIIT) method has been proven to burn fat, increase endurance, and improve cardiovascular health. In this article, we'll explore the best Tabata bodyweight workout for beginners and offer tips for getting the most out of your workout.

Do you struggle to find time for exercise? Are you tired of monotonous gym routines? These common pain points can be addressed with Tabata bodyweight workouts. They are quick, efficient, and can be done anywhere, without any special equipment. Plus, the high-intensity nature of Tabata workouts means you can achieve great results in a short amount of time.

The target of the best Tabata bodyweight workout is to perform eight rounds of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest. The exercises can be varied, but they should be intense enough to raise your heart rate and challenge your muscles. This creates an afterburn effect, where your body continues to burn calories for up to 24 hours after your workout.

In summary, Tabata bodyweight workouts are a great way to get in shape quickly, efficiently, and without any special equipment. By performing eight rounds of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, you can achieve great results in a short amount of time.

What is the Best Tabata Bodyweight Workout?

The best Tabata bodyweight workout for beginners is a combination of squats, push-ups, lunges, and planks. These exercises target major muscle groups and can be modified to suit your fitness level. Here's an example workout:

Round 1: Squats
Round 2: Push-ups
Round 3: Lunges
Round 4: Plank
Round 5: Squats
Round 6: Push-ups
Round 7: Lunges
Round 8: Plank

For each exercise, perform as many reps as possible in 20 seconds, then rest for 10 seconds before moving to the next exercise. Repeat for eight rounds, for a total of four minutes. As you get fitter, you can increase the intensity by adding weights or increasing the number of reps.

Tabata Bodyweight Workout for Core Strength

A strong core is essential for good posture, balance, and overall fitness. Here's a Tabata bodyweight workout that targets your core:

Round 1: Bicycle Crunches
Round 2: Plank with Knee Tucks
Round 3: Russian Twists
Round 4: Leg Raises
Round 5: Bicycle Crunches
Round 6: Plank with Knee Tucks
Round 7: Russian Twists
Round 8: Leg Raises

For each exercise, perform as many reps as possible in 20 seconds, then rest for 10 seconds before moving to the next exercise. Repeat for eight rounds, for a total of four minutes. As you get fitter, you can increase the intensity by adding weights or increasing the number of reps.

Benefits of Tabata Bodyweight Workouts

Tabata bodyweight workouts offer a wide range of benefits, including:

  • Burns fat and builds muscle
  • Increases endurance and cardiovascular health
  • Boosts metabolism and afterburn effect
  • Improves flexibility, balance, and coordination
  • Requires no special equipment or gym membership

Tips for a Successful Tabata Bodyweight Workout

To get the most out of your Tabata bodyweight workout, follow these tips:

  • Choose exercises that target major muscle groups
  • Warm up before your workout and cool down afterwards
  • Stay hydrated and fuelled with healthy snacks
  • Listen to your body and adjust the intensity as needed
  • Track your progress and celebrate your achievements

FAQs About Tabata Bodyweight Workouts

1. How often should I do a Tabata bodyweight workout?

You can do a Tabata bodyweight workout up to three times a week, but it's important to give your body time to rest and recover between workouts. You can also incorporate Tabata intervals into your existing workout routine.

2. Can I do Tabata bodyweight workouts if I'm a beginner?

Yes, Tabata bodyweight workouts are suitable for beginners as well as advanced fitness enthusiasts. Start with a low-intensity workout and gradually increase the intensity as you get fitter.

3. Do I need any special equipment for a Tabata bodyweight workout?

No, Tabata bodyweight workouts require no special equipment. You can do them anywhere, using only your bodyweight.

4. Can Tabata bodyweight workouts help me lose weight?

Yes, Tabata bodyweight workouts are an effective way to burn fat and lose weight, especially when combined with a healthy diet and lifestyle.

Conclusion of Best Tabata Bodyweight Workout

If you're looking for a quick and effective way to get in shape, Tabata bodyweight workouts are a great option. By performing eight rounds of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, you can burn fat, build muscle, and improve your overall fitness. Follow the tips and workouts outlined in this article to get started on your Tabata bodyweight journey.
