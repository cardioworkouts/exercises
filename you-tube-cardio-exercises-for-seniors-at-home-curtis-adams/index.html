Sample You Tube Cardio Exercises for Seniors at Home Curtis Adams Routine

Here's a sample routine that you can try:

2. Grapevine steps: Step to the side with your right foot, cross your left foot behind your right foot, step to the side with your right foot, and then step your left foot next to your right foot. Repeat for 2 minutes.

3. Knee lifts: Lift your right knee up to your chest, lower it, and then lift your left knee up to your chest. Repeat for 2 minutes.

4. Squats: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and lower your body as if you're sitting in a chair. Return to the starting position and repeat for 2 minutes.

5. Cool-down: March in place for 2 minutes.
