Question and Answer

Q: What is resistance training?

A: Resistance training involves exercises that use resistance, such as weightlifting or resistance bands, to build strength and muscle.

Q: What are the benefits of resistance training?

A: The benefits of resistance training include building strength and muscle, improving athletic performance, and increasing bone density.

Q: How often should I do resistance training?

A: It's recommended to do resistance training at least two times per week, with a rest day in between.

Q: How long does it take to see results from resistance training?

A: You may start to see results from resistance training within a few weeks, but it can take several months to see significant changes in muscle mass and strength.
