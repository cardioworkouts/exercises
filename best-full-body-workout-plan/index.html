How to Perform Squats

Squats are an excellent exercise for toning your legs, glutes, and core. To perform squats:

  1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  2. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back.
  3. Keep your chest up and your core engaged.
  4. Raise your body back up to the starting position.

Repeat for 3 sets of 10-12 reps.

Tips for Performing Squats

Make sure that your knees are in line with your toes when you lower your body. Keep your back straight and your core engaged throughout the exercise.
