Best Free Gym Routine Apps A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym without a plan? Do you struggle to keep track of your workouts or find it difficult to come up with new exercises to try? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with creating and sticking to a gym routine. Fortunately, there are several free apps available that can help you.

In this article, we'll explore the best free gym routine apps and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals. We'll also discuss the features and benefits of each app, so you can decide which one is right for you.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is an excellent app for those who want to work out at home or in the gym. The app features over 180 workouts, ranging from bodyweight exercises to full-body strength training. Each workout includes a video tutorial and step-by-step instructions, making it easy to follow along.

I've personally used Nike Training Club for several months and have seen great results. The app is easy to use, and the workouts are challenging but doable. I also appreciate that the app allows you to track your progress and set goals.

2. JEFIT

JEFIT is another great app for those who want to build muscle and strength. The app features over 1,300 exercises and allows you to create custom workout plans based on your goals and fitness level. JEFIT also includes a workout log, so you can track your progress over time.

I've used JEFIT in the past and found it to be a valuable tool for tracking my workouts. The app is easy to navigate, and the exercise library is extensive. I also appreciate that the app includes a social aspect, allowing you to connect with other users and share your progress.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is an app that creates custom workout plans based on your goals, fitness level, and available equipment. The app features over 400 exercises and uses artificial intelligence to adjust your workouts as you progress. Fitbod also includes a workout log, so you can track your progress over time.

I haven't personally used Fitbod, but I've heard great things from friends who have. The app is highly customizable, allowing you to choose the types of exercises you want to do and the equipment you have available. I also appreciate that the app includes a rest timer, making it easy to take breaks between sets.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a workout app, MyFitnessPal is an excellent tool for tracking your diet and exercise. The app allows you to log your meals and snacks, as well as your workouts. MyFitnessPal also includes a database of over 11 million foods, making it easy to track your calorie intake.

I've used MyFitnessPal in the past and found it to be an essential tool for weight loss. The app is easy to use, and the food database is extensive. I also appreciate that the app allows you to set goals and track your progress over time.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed in this article are free to download and use. Some may offer premium features for an additional cost, but the basic functionality is free.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app. Some, like Nike Training Club, include bodyweight exercises that require no equipment. Others, like JEFIT, may require access to gym equipment. Be sure to read the app's description before downloading to ensure it's compatible with your fitness level and available equipment.

Q: Can these apps help me lose weight?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed in this article can help you lose weight when used in conjunction with a healthy diet. By tracking your workouts and calorie intake, you'll be able to create a calorie deficit and lose weight over time.

Q: Which app is the best?

A: It depends on your goals and personal preferences. Nike Training Club is great for those who want to work out at home or in the gym, while JEFIT is perfect for those who want to build muscle and strength. Fitbod is highly customizable and adjusts your workouts based on your progress, while MyFitnessPal is an excellent tool for tracking your diet and exercise.

Conclusion of Best Free Gym Routine Apps

By using one of the best free gym routine apps listed in this article, you'll be able to create and stick to a workout plan that's tailored to your goals and fitness level. Whether you want to work out at home or in the gym, build muscle and strength or lose weight, there's an app that can help you achieve your goals.
