1. Squats

When it comes to easy exercises to do at home, squats are one of the best exercises that you can do. Squats help strengthen your lower body and improve your balance. To do squats, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Bend your knees and lower your hips as if you are sitting in a chair. Keep your chest up and your back straight. Pause for a few seconds and then return to the starting position.

2. Lunges

Lunges are another easy exercise that you can do at home. Lunges help strengthen your legs and improve your balance. To do lunges, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Step forward with your right foot and bend your right knee. Your left knee should almost touch the ground. Pause for a few seconds and then return to the starting position. Repeat with your left leg.

3. Push-ups

Push-ups are another easy exercise that you can do at home. Push-ups help strengthen your upper body and core. To do push-ups, start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest almost touches the ground. Pause for a few seconds and then return to the starting position.

4. Planks

Planks are an excellent exercise for your core muscles. To do planks, start in a push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your elbows are bent at a 90-degree angle. Keep your body straight and your core engaged. Hold this position for as long as you can.

5. Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a great way to get your heart rate up and improve your cardiovascular health. To do jumping jacks, stand with your feet together and your arms at your sides. Jump up and spread your feet out to the sides while raising your arms over your head. Jump back to the starting position and repeat.
