The Best Free Weight Training App For Ipad

Written by Luffy Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and not knowing what exercises to do or how to properly perform them? Do you struggle to keep track of your progress and stay motivated during your workouts? If so, then you may be in need of a great weight training app for your iPad.

Weight training can be a challenging and rewarding experience, but it can also be overwhelming for beginners or those who are new to the gym. With so many different exercises and routines to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start or how to progress. Additionally, it can be hard to stay motivated and accountable without a clear plan or way to track your progress.

Fortunately, there are many great free weight training apps available for iPad users that can help take the guesswork out of your workouts and keep you on track towards your fitness goals. In this article, we will explore some of the best free weight training apps for iPad and what makes them so great.

What is the Best Free Weight Training App for iPad?

After researching and testing various weight training apps for iPad, we have determined that the best free option is JEFIT Workout Planner Gym Log. This app offers a wide range of features and benefits that make it an excellent choice for anyone looking to improve their strength and fitness.

JEFIT allows users to create customized workout plans based on their goals, fitness level, and available equipment. The app features a vast library of exercises with detailed instructions and animations to ensure that users are performing each movement correctly and safely. Additionally, JEFIT offers a robust tracking and analytics system that allows users to monitor their progress and see improvements over time.

Why is JEFIT the Best Free Weight Training App for iPad?

From personal experience, I can attest to the effectiveness and usability of JEFIT. As someone who is relatively new to weight training, I found the app to be incredibly user-friendly and easy to navigate. The exercise library was extensive and offered variations for different equipment or muscle groups, which allowed me to create a well-rounded workout plan that suited my needs.

One of the features that I appreciated most about JEFIT was the tracking system. The app allowed me to record my sets, reps, and weights for each exercise, which made it easy to see my progress and identify areas where I needed to improve. Additionally, JEFIT provided graphs and charts that showed my progress over time, which helped to keep me motivated and accountable.

Other Great Free Weight Training Apps for iPad

While JEFIT is our top pick for the best free weight training app for iPad, there are many other great options available. Here are a few honorable mentions:

StrongLifts 5x5

For those who are new to weight training or looking to build strength, StrongLifts 5x5 is an excellent choice. The app is based on the popular 5x5 program, which focuses on compound exercises and progressive overload. StrongLifts offers a simple interface and easy-to-follow instructions, making it ideal for beginners.

Fitbod

Fitbod is another great weight training app that offers personalized workout plans based on your goals, fitness level, and available equipment. The app uses machine learning to adjust your workouts and keep them challenging as you progress. Additionally, Fitbod offers detailed instructions and animations for each exercise to ensure that you are performing them correctly.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for both men and women?

A: Yes, these apps are designed to be used by anyone regardless of gender.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: While some exercises may require equipment, many of the apps offer variations or substitutions for those who do not have access to a gym or weight room.

Q: Can I use these apps if I am a beginner?

A: Absolutely! Each of these apps offers beginner-friendly workouts and exercises, as well as tips and guidance for those who are new to weight training.

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed in this article are available for free download from the App Store. However, some may offer in-app purchases or premium features that require a subscription or one-time payment.

Conclusion of Best Free Weight Training App for iPad

Weight training can be a challenging and rewarding experience, but it can also be overwhelming without the right guidance and support. With the help of a great weight training app for your iPad, you can take the guesswork out of your workouts and stay on track towards your fitness goals. Whether you choose JEFIT or one of the other apps listed in this article, you are sure to find a tool that will help you get stronger, fitter, and healthier.
