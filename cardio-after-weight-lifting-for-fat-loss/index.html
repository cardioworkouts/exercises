Cardio After Weight Lifting For Fat Loss A Beginner s Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight despite following a strict diet and workout routine? Do you find it challenging to incorporate cardio after weight lifting for fat loss? If yes, then this guide is for you. In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about cardio after weight lifting for fat loss.

Pain Points Related to Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss

Many people believe that lifting weights is enough to lose weight. However, this is a common misconception. Weight lifting helps build muscle, but to lose fat, you need to incorporate cardio into your workout routine. But, post-weight lifting cardio can be challenging for many people. From feeling exhausted to not knowing which exercise to choose, there are several pain points related to cardio after weight lifting for fat loss.

What is Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss?

Cardio after weight lifting for fat loss is a workout routine that involves cardio exercises after weight lifting. It is an effective way to burn calories and lose fat. Cardio exercises include activities that increase your heart rate, such as running, cycling, and jumping rope. When you perform cardio after weight lifting, your body burns stored fat for energy, resulting in weight loss.

Here are some benefits of cardio after weight lifting for fat loss:

  • It increases your heart rate, making your body burn more calories.
  • It boosts your metabolism, leading to weight loss.
  • It helps reduce the risk of heart-related diseases.

Personal Experience with Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss

When I started my weight loss journey, I focused solely on lifting weights. However, I soon realized that I wasn't losing as much weight as I wanted to. That's when I incorporated cardio after weight lifting into my workout routine. Initially, it was challenging, and I'd feel exhausted after weight lifting. However, I soon realized that cardio after weight lifting helped me burn more calories and lose fat. Now, it's an essential part of my workout routine.

How to Incorporate Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss?

If you're new to cardio after weight lifting for fat loss, here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Start with low-intensity cardio exercises, such as walking or cycling.
  • Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your cardio exercises.
  • Perform cardio exercises immediately after weight lifting.
  • Take adequate rest between weight lifting and cardio exercises.

The Best Cardio Exercises After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss

Here are some of the best cardio exercises to perform after weight lifting for fat loss:

  • Running on a treadmill or outdoors
  • Cycling
  • Jumping rope
  • Stair climbing
  • Swimming

How Long Should You Do Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss?

The duration of cardio after weight lifting for fat loss depends on your fitness level and weight loss goals. Ideally, you should perform cardio exercises for at least 30 minutes after weight lifting. However, if you're new to cardio, start with 10-15 minutes and gradually increase the duration.

Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss: Final Thoughts

Cardio after weight lifting for fat loss is an effective way to burn calories and lose fat. It helps boost your metabolism and reduces the risk of heart-related diseases. Incorporating cardio after weight lifting might be challenging initially, but with consistent effort and dedication, you can achieve your weight loss goals.

Question and Answer

Q. Is it necessary to perform cardio after weight lifting for fat loss?

A. Yes, cardio after weight lifting is necessary for fat loss. Weight lifting helps build muscle, but cardio after weight lifting helps burn calories and lose fat.

Q. How often should I do cardio after weight lifting for fat loss?

A. You can perform cardio after weight lifting for fat loss 3-4 times a week. However, the frequency depends on your fitness level and weight loss goals.

Q. Can I perform cardio before weight lifting?

A. Yes, you can perform cardio before weight lifting. However, it's essential to take adequate rest between cardio and weight lifting exercises.

Q. How long will it take to see results with cardio after weight lifting for fat loss?

A. The duration to see results with cardio after weight lifting for fat loss varies from person to person. However, with consistent effort and dedication, you can see visible results in 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion of Cardio After Weight Lifting for Fat Loss

In conclusion, cardio after weight lifting for fat loss is an effective way to burn calories and lose fat. It helps boost your metabolism and reduces the risk of heart-related diseases. By incorporating cardio after weight lifting into your workout routine and following a healthy diet, you can achieve your weight loss goals.
