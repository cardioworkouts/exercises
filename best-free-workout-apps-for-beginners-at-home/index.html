Best Free Workout Apps For Beginners At Home

Written by William Sep 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start a workout routine at home but don't know where to start? With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. Don't worry; we've got you covered! In this article, we'll introduce you to the best free workout apps for beginners at home.

Starting a workout routine can be challenging, especially if you're a beginner. You might not know the right exercises to do, how long to work out, or how to track your progress. These pain points can be overwhelming, and you might end up quitting before you even start. That's why using a workout app can be beneficial.

The target of this article is to introduce you to the best free workout apps for beginners at home. We've done the research for you and have rounded up the top apps that offer various workouts, track your progress, and provide motivation to keep going.

In conclusion, using a workout app can be an excellent way to start your fitness journey as a beginner. With the right app, you can access various workouts, track your progress, and stay motivated. Keep reading to find out our top picks for the best free workout apps for beginners at home.

The 7 Minute Workout

When it comes to working out, time can be a limiting factor. If you're looking for a quick workout that you can do anywhere, The 7 Minute Workout app is perfect for you. This app provides a high-intensity workout that lasts only seven minutes.

The app includes 12 different exercises that are each 30 seconds long, with a ten-second rest in between. The exercises target different muscle groups, including your core, arms, and legs. The app also includes a voice coach that guides you through each exercise, making it easy to follow along.

FitOn

If you're looking for a workout app that offers a variety of workouts, FitOn is an excellent choice. This app provides various workouts, including yoga, Pilates, strength training, and cardio. The app also includes workout plans that are customized to your fitness level and goals.

One of the best features of FitOn is the community aspect. You can connect with other users and join challenges, which can help keep you motivated. The app also includes a progress tracker, so you can see how far you've come.

MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a workout app, MyFitnessPal is an excellent tool to track your progress and stay accountable. This app allows you to log your meals, track your water intake, and monitor your exercise. MyFitnessPal also includes a large database of foods, making it easy to log your meals accurately.

The app also syncs with various fitness trackers, including Fitbit and Apple Watch, so you can see all your data in one place. MyFitnessPal also provides insights into your progress, such as how many calories you've burned and how many you've consumed.

Sworkit

If you're looking for a personalized workout experience, Sworkit is a great choice. This app allows you to customize your workout plan based on your fitness level, goals, and the amount of time you have available. The app also includes a variety of workouts, including strength training, yoga, and cardio.

Sworkit provides video demonstrations of each exercise, making it easy to follow along. The app also includes a progress tracker, so you can see how far you've come. Plus, with the ability to customize your workout plan, you'll never get bored with the same routine.

Pocket Yoga

If you're looking for a yoga app, Pocket Yoga is an excellent choice. This app provides various yoga practices, including vinyasa, hatha, and restorative. The app includes detailed voice and video instructions, making it easy to follow along.

Pocket Yoga also includes a progress tracker, so you can see how far you've come. The app also allows you to create your custom practice, so you can choose the poses you want to focus on.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps free?

A: Yes, all the apps mentioned in this article are free to download. However, some apps offer in-app purchases for additional features.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm not a beginner?

A: Yes, these apps are suitable for beginners and advanced users. Many of the apps allow you to customize your workout plan based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps require equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands, while others only require your body weight. Make sure to read the app description before downloading to see if any equipment is required.

Q: Can I use these apps offline?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps require an internet connection, while others allow you to download workouts to use offline. Make sure to read the app description before downloading.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Apps for Beginners at Home

Starting a workout routine as a beginner can be challenging, but with the right app, it can be more manageable. The best free workout apps for beginners at home provide various workouts, track your progress, and offer motivation to keep going. Whether you're looking for a quick workout or a personalized plan, there's an app out there for you. Download one of the apps mentioned above and start your fitness journey today!
