Who Has The Best Workout Videos On Youtube

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym every day? Do you want to work out at home but don't know where to start? Youtube is the perfect platform for you. With so many different workout videos available, it can be overwhelming to determine which ones are the best. In this blog post, we will discuss who has the best workout videos on youtube and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Working out can be difficult, especially if you don't have a gym membership or access to workout equipment. Additionally, finding the motivation to work out can be a challenge when you're doing it alone. However, the right workout video can help you overcome these obstacles and stay on track with your fitness journey.

When it comes to who has the best workout videos on youtube, there are many great options to choose from. However, some of the most popular fitness channels include Blogilates, Fitness Blender, and POPSUGAR Fitness. Each of these channels offers a variety of workouts, ranging from yoga and pilates to HIIT and strength training.

In summary, if you're looking for the best workout videos on youtube, you can't go wrong with Blogilates, Fitness Blender, or POPSUGAR Fitness. Each of these channels offers high-quality workouts that can be done from the comfort of your own home.

Blogilates

Blogilates is a fitness channel run by Cassey Ho, a certified Pilates and fitness instructor. The channel features a variety of Pilates workouts that can be done at home, as well as healthy recipes and lifestyle tips. I personally love Blogilates because Cassey is so upbeat and motivating. Her workouts are challenging, but she makes them fun with her bubbly personality.

Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a channel run by husband and wife duo, Daniel and Kelli. The channel features a wide range of workouts, including HIIT, strength training, and yoga. What I love about Fitness Blender is that their workouts are no-nonsense and effective. They don't waste any time with unnecessary chatter or distractions, which allows you to focus on your workout.

POPSUGAR Fitness

POPSUGAR Fitness is a channel that covers all things fitness, from workout videos to healthy recipes and lifestyle tips. The channel features a variety of workouts, including dance cardio, yoga, and strength training. What I love about POPSUGAR Fitness is that they have a wide range of instructors, so you can find someone who suits your style and personality.

How to Choose the Best Workout Videos on Youtube

When it comes to choosing the best workout videos on youtube, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your fitness goals. Are you looking to build strength, lose weight, or improve your flexibility? Once you know your goals, look for channels and videos that cater to those specific areas.

Second, consider your fitness level. If you're a beginner, look for videos that are labeled as "beginner-friendly" or "low-impact." If you're more advanced, look for videos that are labeled as "advanced" or "high-intensity."

Third, consider your interests. If you hate running, don't force yourself to do it. Instead, look for workouts that you enjoy, such as dance cardio or yoga.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I really get a good workout from a youtube video?

A: Absolutely! Many fitness professionals create high-quality workout videos that can be just as effective as working out in a gym.

Q: How often should I work out using youtube videos?

A: It's recommended that you work out at least three times a week, but the frequency and duration of your workouts will depend on your fitness goals.

Q: Do I need any equipment to do youtube workouts?

A: It depends on the video. Some videos require equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands, while others don't require any equipment at all.

Q: Are there any risks to working out using youtube videos?

A: As with any workout, there is a risk of injury if you don't use proper form or if you push yourself too hard. It's important to listen to your body and take breaks when needed.

Conclusion of Who Has the Best Workout Videos on Youtube

Youtube is a great resource for anyone looking to work out from home. With so many different workout videos available, it can be difficult to determine which ones are the best. However, by following the tips outlined in this post and checking out channels like Blogilates, Fitness Blender, and POPSUGAR Fitness, you can find high-quality workout videos that will help you achieve your fitness goals.
