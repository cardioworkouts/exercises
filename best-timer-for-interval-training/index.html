The Best Timer For Interval Training A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the best timer for interval training? Interval training is a popular workout method that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or low-intensity activity. It is a great way to improve cardiovascular health, burn fat, and build endurance. However, it can be hard to keep track of time during these workouts, which is where a timer comes in handy. In this guide, we will discuss some of the best timers for interval training and how to use them effectively.

What is the Target of Best Timer for Interval Training?

The target of the best timer for interval training is to help you keep track of your workout intervals accurately. A good timer should allow you to set up custom intervals, display the time remaining for each interval, and alert you when it's time to switch between high-intensity and low-intensity activity. There are many timers available in the market, but not all of them are suitable for interval training.

The best timers for interval training are designed specifically to meet the unique needs of this type of workout. They are easy to use, accurate, and come with a range of features that make it easier to stay on track during your workout. The following are some of the best timers for interval training that you should consider:

Gymboss Interval Timer

The Gymboss Interval Timer is a popular choice among fitness enthusiasts. It is compact, easy to use, and comes with a range of features that make it ideal for interval training. It allows you to set up custom intervals of up to 99 rounds, with each round lasting up to 99 minutes. You can also set up two different interval lengths, which is perfect for workouts that involve both high-intensity and low-intensity activity. The Gymboss Interval Timer is also water-resistant, making it ideal for outdoor workouts.

Seconds Pro Interval Timer

The Seconds Pro Interval Timer is another great option for interval training. It is a smartphone app that is available for both iOS and Android devices. The app allows you to create custom timers with an unlimited number of intervals. You can also choose from a range of different sounds and alerts to keep you motivated during your workout. The Seconds Pro Interval Timer also comes with a range of pre-built timers that you can use if you don't want to create your own.

Built-in Interval Timer on Your Fitness Watch

If you have a fitness watch, chances are it comes with a built-in interval timer. This is a great option if you don't want to carry an extra device with you during your workout. Most fitness watches allow you to create custom intervals of varying lengths, and they will vibrate or beep to alert you when it's time to switch between high-intensity and low-intensity activity. Some popular fitness watches with built-in interval timers include the Apple Watch, Garmin Forerunner, and Fitbit Versa.

How to Use the Best Timer for Interval Training

Using the best timer for interval training is simple. First, you need to choose the timer that best suits your needs. Once you have your timer, you need to set up your intervals. This will depend on your fitness level and the type of workout you are doing. If you are new to interval training, you may want to start with shorter intervals and gradually increase the length and intensity of your workouts over time.

After setting up your intervals, you can start your workout. The timer will alert you when it's time to switch between high-intensity and low-intensity activity. During the high-intensity intervals, you should aim to push yourself to your limits, while during the low-intensity intervals, you can take a break or do some light activity to recover. Repeat this cycle for the desired number of rounds, and you're done!

Conclusion of Best Timer for Interval Training

The best timer for interval training is an essential tool for anyone who wants to get the most out of their workouts. It helps you keep track of time accurately, so you can focus on pushing yourself to your limits during high-intensity intervals and recover during low-intensity intervals. The Gymboss Interval Timer, Seconds Pro Interval Timer, and built-in interval timer on your fitness watch are some of the best timers for interval training in the market. Choose the timer that best suits your needs, set up your intervals, and start your workout today!

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use any timer for interval training?

A: While you can use any timer for interval training, it is recommended to use a timer specifically designed for this type of workout. These timers come with features that make it easier to keep track of your intervals accurately.

Q: How long should my intervals be?

A: The length of your intervals will depend on your fitness level and the type of workout you are doing. If you are new to interval training, you may want to start with shorter intervals and gradually increase their length and intensity over time.

Q: Can I use a smartphone app as a timer for interval training?

A: Yes, there are many smartphone apps available that are specifically designed for interval training. The Seconds Pro Interval Timer is one of the most popular options.

Q: How many rounds should I do during my interval training workout?

A: The number of rounds you should do will depend on your fitness level and the type of workout you are doing. As a general rule, you should aim to do at least 3 to 5 rounds, with each round lasting between 10 to 30 minutes.
