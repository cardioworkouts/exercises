Best Exercise Apps For Beginners Free

Written by Frank Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a beginner who wants to start exercising but doesn't know where to start? Do you find it difficult to go to the gym due to a busy schedule or lack of motivation? If you answered yes to any of these questions, then you might want to consider using exercise apps. In this article, we will be discussing the best exercise apps for beginners free.

Starting an exercise routine can be challenging, especially for beginners. It can be overwhelming to figure out what exercises to do, how often to do them, and how to do them correctly. Additionally, finding the time and motivation to exercise can also be difficult. However, exercise apps can help you overcome these challenges by providing you with a personalized exercise plan, tracking your progress, and motivating you to stick to your routine.

Target of Best Exercise Apps for Beginners Free and Related Apps

The target of the best exercise apps for beginners free is to provide a simple and effective way for beginners to start exercising. These apps offer a variety of workouts that are tailored to your fitness level and goals. They also provide step-by-step instructions and videos that demonstrate how to perform each exercise correctly. Some of the best exercise apps for beginners free include:

  • 1. Nike Training Club
  • 2. FitOn
  • 3. 7 Minute Workout
  • 4. Sworkit
  • 5. Daily Burn

These apps are available for free on both Android and iOS devices. They offer a range of features, including personalized workout plans, progress tracking, and social sharing.

In summary, exercise apps can be a great tool for beginners who want to start exercising. They provide a convenient and accessible way to get started, and they offer personalized plans and tracking to help you stay motivated and on track. In the next sections, we will discuss some of the best exercise apps for beginners free in more detail.

Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a wide range of workouts for beginners and advanced users. The app features over 190 workouts, including bodyweight exercises, yoga, and cardio. Each workout is designed by Nike Master Trainers and includes step-by-step instructions and videos. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals. You can track your progress and share your achievements with friends on social media.

One of the best features of Nike Training Club is its flexibility. You can choose the length of your workout, ranging from 5 to 60 minutes, and you can also select the type of workout you want to do. The app also offers audio guidance and music integration to make your workouts more enjoyable.

FitOn

FitOn is another free exercise app that offers a variety of workouts for beginners. The app features over 1,000 workouts, including yoga, Pilates, cardio, and strength training. Each workout is led by a certified personal trainer and includes step-by-step instructions and videos. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

One of the best features of FitOn is its community aspect. The app allows you to connect with friends and other users to share your progress and motivation. You can also participate in challenges and earn rewards for completing workouts.

7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a simple and effective app that offers quick workouts for beginners. The app features 12 high-intensity bodyweight exercises that can be completed in just 7 minutes. Each exercise is demonstrated with a video, and the app includes a timer to guide you through the workout.

One of the best features of 7 Minute Workout is its simplicity. The app is easy to use and can be completed anywhere with no equipment needed. It's a great option for beginners who are short on time or want to add a quick workout to their routine.

Sworkit

Sworkit is a free app that offers customized workout plans for beginners. The app features over 400 workouts, including cardio, strength training, yoga, and Pilates. Each workout is customizable based on your fitness level and goals. The app also includes step-by-step instructions and videos for each exercise.

One of the best features of Sworkit is its flexibility. You can choose the length of your workout, ranging from 5 to 60 minutes, and you can also select the type of workout you want to do. The app also offers audio guidance and music integration to make your workouts more enjoyable.

Daily Burn

Daily Burn is a free app that offers personalized workout plans for beginners. The app features over 1,000 workouts, including cardio, strength training, yoga, and Pilates. Each workout is led by a certified personal trainer and includes step-by-step instructions and videos. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

One of the best features of Daily Burn is its variety. The app offers a wide range of workouts, so you can find one that suits your preferences and fitness level. You can also track your progress and earn rewards for completing workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article are free to download and use.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: Most of the workouts offered by these apps can be done with little to no equipment. However, some workouts may require basic equipment, such as dumbbells or resistance bands.

Q: Can I use these apps if I have a medical condition?

A: It's always best to consult with your doctor before starting a new exercise routine, especially if you have a medical condition. However, many of these apps offer modifications for certain exercises, so you can adjust them to your fitness level and abilities.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm not a beginner?

A: Yes, these apps offer workouts for all fitness levels, so you can use them whether you're a beginner or an advanced user.

Conclusion of Best Exercise Apps for Beginners Free

Exercise apps can be a great tool for beginners who want to start exercising. They offer a convenient and accessible way to get started, and they provide personalized plans and tracking to help you stay motivated and on track. Some of the best exercise apps for beginners free include Nike Training Club, FitOn, 7 Minute Workout, Sworkit, and Daily Burn. These apps offer a variety of workouts that are tailored to your fitness level and goals, and they're available for free on both Android and iOS devices.
