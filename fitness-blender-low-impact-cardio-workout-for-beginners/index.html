How to do Fitness Blender Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners?

The workout is straightforward to do, and you don't need any equipment. All you need is a mat and a water bottle. The workout consists of a warm-up, low-impact cardio movements, and a cool-down. You can do the workout at home, and it takes around 30-40 minutes to complete.

The warm-up consists of five minutes of light cardio movements such as marching in place, arm circles, and jumping jacks. The low-impact cardio movements include exercises such as knee lifts, side-to-side steps, and low-impact jumping jacks. The cool-down consists of five minutes of stretching exercises such as hamstring stretch, calf stretch, and quad stretch.
