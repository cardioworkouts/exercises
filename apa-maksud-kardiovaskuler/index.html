Common Cardiovascular Conditions

There are several common conditions that fall under the umbrella of cardiovascular disease. These include:

High blood pressure: This occurs when the force of blood against the walls of your arteries is too high. It can lead to serious health issues, including heart disease and stroke.

Coronary artery disease: This condition occurs when the arteries that supply blood to the heart become narrowed or blocked. It can cause chest pain, shortness of breath, and other symptoms.

Heart failure: This occurs when the heart is unable to pump blood effectively. It can cause fatigue, shortness of breath, and other symptoms.

Arrhythmia: This refers to an irregular heartbeat. It can cause lightheadedness, fainting, and other symptoms.
