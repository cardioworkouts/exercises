How Long Should Beginners Exercise

Written by Eveline Sep 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Starting a new exercise routine can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to figuring out how long to exercise. There are a lot of conflicting opinions out there, and it can be hard to know what is right for you. This article will help guide you through the process of figuring out how long beginners should exercise.

One of the biggest pain points for beginners is not knowing how long they should exercise for. This can lead to feelings of uncertainty and confusion, making it harder to stick to a routine. It's important to find a balance between pushing yourself enough to see progress, but not so much that you become discouraged or overwhelmed.

So, how long should beginners exercise for? The general guideline is to aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week. This can be broken down into shorter sessions throughout the day if needed. However, it's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard too soon. It's better to start slowly and gradually increase your time and intensity as you build up your fitness level.

In summary, beginners should aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week. It's important to start slowly and gradually increase your time and intensity as you become more fit.

Why 30 Minutes?

When starting out, 30 minutes may seem like a lot. However, it's important to remember that it doesn't have to be done all at once. Breaking it down into smaller sessions throughout the day can make it more manageable. Additionally, 30 minutes is a good starting point because it provides enough time to get your heart rate up and start seeing benefits, without pushing yourself too hard. It's also a manageable amount of time for most people to fit into their daily routine.

Personally, when I first started exercising, I struggled to even make it through 10 minutes. But by starting small and gradually building up, I was able to work my way up to 30 minutes and beyond. It's important to remember that everyone starts somewhere and progress takes time.

How Often Should Beginners Exercise?

In addition to figuring out how long to exercise for, it's important to consider how often to exercise. As a beginner, it's recommended to aim for most days of the week. This can be broken down into shorter sessions if needed. Consistency is key when it comes to seeing progress, so finding a routine that works for you and sticking to it is important.

Personally, I found it helpful to schedule my workouts into my calendar as if they were appointments. This helped me prioritize my exercise and make sure I was making time for it in my busy schedule. It also helped me stay accountable to myself and my goals.

What is Moderate-Intensity Exercise?

As mentioned earlier, beginners should aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week. But what exactly does that mean? Moderate-intensity exercise is any activity that gets your heart rate up and makes you breathe harder, but you can still carry on a conversation. Examples include brisk walking, cycling, and swimming.

It's important to note that everyone's fitness level is different, so what is considered moderate for one person may be different for another. The key is to listen to your body and find a pace that is challenging, but still manageable.

How to Progress Over Time

As you become more fit, it's important to gradually increase your time and intensity to continue seeing progress. This can be done by adding a few extra minutes to your workout each week, increasing the resistance or speed, or trying new exercises that challenge your body in different ways.

Personally, I found it helpful to set small goals for myself each week. For example, I would aim to add an extra minute to my workout or try a new exercise. These small goals helped me stay motivated and gave me something to work towards.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it okay to exercise for less than 30 minutes a day?

A: Absolutely! Any amount of exercise is better than none. If 30 minutes seems overwhelming, start with 10 or 15 minutes and gradually work your way up.

Q: Can I exercise every day?

A: Yes, as a beginner, it's okay to exercise every day as long as you listen to your body and don't push yourself too hard. It's also important to incorporate rest days into your routine to allow your body time to recover.

Q: What if I can't do moderate-intensity exercise?

A: That's okay! There are plenty of low-impact options available, such as yoga, Pilates, or walking at a slower pace. The key is to find an activity that you enjoy and that challenges your body in a way that feels manageable.

Q: How long does it take to see results?

A: Everyone's body is different, so it's hard to say exactly how long it will take to see results. However, by consistently exercising most days of the week, you should start to see improvements in your fitness level within a few weeks to a few months.

Conclusion of How Long to Exercise for Beginners

Starting a new exercise routine can be challenging, but by aiming for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise most days of the week, beginners can start to see progress and improve their fitness level. It's important to start slowly and gradually increase time and intensity to avoid injury and burnout. Remember, progress takes time, so be patient and consistent in your workouts.
