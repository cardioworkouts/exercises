Best Workout For Your First Day Back At The Gym

Written by Robby Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you feeling nervous about your first day back at the gym? Have you been away from the gym for a long time and don’t know where to start? It’s completely normal to feel this way, but don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this post, we’ll provide you with the best workout for your first day back at the gym, along with some tips and tricks to make it a success.

If you’re feeling a bit out of shape or just haven’t been working out for a while, your first day back at the gym can be daunting. You might be worried about feeling sore the next day or not being able to complete a full workout. But it’s important to remember that everyone has to start somewhere, and your first day back at the gym is a great place to start.

The best workout for your first day back at the gym should focus on full-body movements that target multiple muscle groups. This will help you to work on your overall fitness, rather than just targeting specific areas. Some of the best exercises for your first day back at the gym include squats, lunges, push-ups, pull-ups, and planks.

To start off, you can do a full-body workout that includes these exercises. Begin with a warm-up and then move on to a few sets of each exercise, with a short break in between. Remember to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard. It’s better to start slowly and build up gradually than to go all in and risk injury.

The Benefits of Cardio on Your First Day Back at the Gym

Cardio is a great way to get your heart pumping and to start burning off those extra calories. It’s also a great way to get your body used to working out again. If you’re feeling a bit nervous about starting cardio, start with something simple like a brisk walk or light jog on the treadmill. You can gradually increase the intensity and duration of your cardio as you get more comfortable.

The Importance of Stretching on Your First Day Back at the Gym

Stretching is an important part of any workout, but it’s especially important on your first day back at the gym. This is because your muscles may be tight and prone to injury. Take some time to stretch before and after your workout to help prevent injury and to help your muscles recover.

Tips for a Successful First Day Back at the Gym

1. Start slowly and build up gradually
2. Focus on full-body movements that target multiple muscle groups
3. Don’t push yourself too hard
4. Listen to your body
5. Take breaks when you need them
6. Stretch before and after your workout
7. Stay hydrated
8. Have fun!

How to Stay Motivated on Your First Day Back at the Gym

One of the best ways to stay motivated on your first day back at the gym is to set yourself a goal. This could be a short-term goal, such as completing a full workout, or a long-term goal, such as running a 5k. Having a goal in mind will help you to stay focused and motivated.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should my first workout be?
A: Your first workout should be around 30-45 minutes. It’s important to start slowly and build up gradually.

Q: Should I focus on cardio or strength training on my first day back at the gym?
A: It’s best to focus on a full-body workout that includes both cardio and strength training.

Q: How often should I go to the gym?
A: It’s best to start with 2-3 times a week and build up gradually from there.

Q: What should I eat before my workout?
A: It’s best to eat a light meal or snack that includes carbohydrates and protein. This will give you the energy you need to power through your workout.

Conclusion of Best Workout for Your First Day Back at the Gym

Starting back at the gym after a long break can be intimidating, but it’s important to remember that everyone has to start somewhere. The best workout for your first day back at the gym should focus on full-body movements that target multiple muscle groups. Start slowly and build up gradually, and don’t forget to stretch before and after your workout. Set yourself a goal to stay motivated, and most importantly, have fun!
