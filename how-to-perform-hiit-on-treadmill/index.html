How To Perform Hiit On Treadmill A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to burn calories and improve your fitness level? If yes, then High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) on a treadmill might be an excellent option for you. HIIT is a form of cardiovascular exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by brief recovery periods. It is an efficient way to burn calories and improve your fitness level in less time. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to perform HIIT on a treadmill.

Many people struggle with performing HIIT on a treadmill because they do not understand the proper technique or are not aware of the benefits. Some people find it challenging to push themselves to the maximum level during the high-intensity intervals, while others find it difficult to recover during the low-intensity intervals.

If you are looking to perform HIIT on a treadmill, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before starting your HIIT workout, it is essential to warm up your body. You can start with a five-minute brisk walk or jog on the treadmill to get your heart rate up and loosen up your muscles.

Step 2: Set Your Treadmill

Once you are warmed up, set your treadmill to your desired speed and incline. For beginners, we recommend starting with a moderate speed and incline. You can increase the speed and incline as you get more comfortable with the workout.

Step 3: Perform HIIT

Begin your HIIT workout by running at a high speed for 30 seconds to one minute, followed by a low-intensity recovery period of 30 seconds to one minute. Repeat this cycle for 15-20 minutes, depending on your fitness level.

Step 4: Cool Down

After completing your HIIT workout, cool down by walking on the treadmill for five to ten minutes. This will help your heart rate gradually return to its resting rate and prevent dizziness or fainting.

In conclusion, performing HIIT on a treadmill is an efficient way to burn calories and improve your fitness level. By following the above steps, you can perform HIIT on a treadmill safely and effectively. Remember to start at a moderate pace and gradually increase the speed and intensity as your fitness level improves.

Benefits of Performing HIIT on a Treadmill

Performing HIIT on a treadmill has numerous benefits, including:

1. Burns Calories

HIIT is an efficient way to burn calories in a short amount of time. Studies have shown that HIIT can burn up to 25-30% more calories than other forms of cardio exercise.

2. Improves Cardiovascular Health

HIIT can improve your cardiovascular health by strengthening your heart and lungs. It can also lower your blood pressure and improve your cholesterol levels.

3. Increases Endurance

HIIT can increase your endurance and stamina, allowing you to perform better in other forms of physical activity.

4. Time-Efficient

HIIT workouts are time-efficient, allowing you to burn calories and improve your fitness level in less time than other forms of cardio exercise.

Personal Experience

I started performing HIIT on a treadmill a few months ago, and I have noticed a significant improvement in my fitness level. Initially, I found it challenging to push myself to the maximum level during the high-intensity intervals, but with time, I have learned to push my limits. I have also increased the speed and incline of the treadmill, which has allowed me to burn more calories and improve my endurance.

FAQs

Q: How often should I perform HIIT on a treadmill?

A: It is recommended to perform HIIT on a treadmill 2-3 times per week.

Q: Can HIIT on a treadmill help me lose weight?

A: Yes, HIIT on a treadmill can help you lose weight by burning calories and increasing your metabolism.

Q: What should I do if I am unable to complete the full 15-20 minutes of HIIT?

A: It is okay to start with a shorter duration and gradually increase the time as your fitness level improves.

Q: Is it safe to perform HIIT on a treadmill?

A: Yes, it is safe to perform HIIT on a treadmill as long as you follow the proper technique and warm-up adequately.

Conclusion

In conclusion, performing HIIT on a treadmill is an excellent way to burn calories and improve your fitness level. By following the above steps and tips, you can perform HIIT on a treadmill safely and effectively. Remember to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts as your fitness level improves.
