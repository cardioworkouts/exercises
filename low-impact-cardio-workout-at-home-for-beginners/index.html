Low Impact Cardio Workout At Home For Beginners

Written by Christine Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking for a low impact cardio workout that you can do at home? Perhaps you have joint pain, want to lose weight, or just prefer a lower intensity workout. Whatever your reason, a low impact cardio workout can still provide great health benefits without putting too much stress on your body.

Low impact cardio workout at home for beginners is a form of exercise that does not require intense jumping, bouncing or any other high impact moves. These workouts are designed to be gentle on your joints, yet still help you burn calories, improve your heart and lung health, and boost your mood.

In this article, we will discuss some of the best low impact cardio workouts at home for beginners, including walking, dancing, cycling, swimming, and yoga. We will also provide tips on how to get started, what to wear, and how to stay motivated.

Walking

Walking is a simple and effective low impact cardio workout that you can do at home or outdoors. All you need is a good pair of shoes and a bit of space. You can start with a 10-minute walk and gradually increase your time and distance as you get fitter. Walking can help you lose weight, improve your heart health, and reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes and high blood pressure.

Personally, I started walking as part of my low impact cardio workout at home for beginners routine. I found it easy to fit into my daily routine and it helped me relieve stress while also improving my fitness level. I also enjoyed exploring new walking routes around my neighbourhood and listening to music or podcasts while walking.

Dancing

Dancing is a fun and creative way to get your heart pumping without putting too much stress on your joints. You can dance to your favourite music at home or follow a dance workout video online. Dancing can help you burn calories, improve your balance and coordination, and boost your mood.

Personally, I love dancing as part of my low impact cardio workout at home for beginners. It's a great way to express myself and forget about the stresses of daily life. I also find it a great way to bond with my family and friends.

Cycling

Cycling is another low impact cardio workout that you can do at home or outdoors. You can use a stationary bike at home or cycle outdoors on a quiet road or bike path. Cycling can help you improve your cardiovascular health, build leg muscles, and burn calories.

When I started cycling as part of my low impact cardio workout at home for beginners, I found it challenging at first, but I gradually built up my endurance and now I love it. It's a great way to explore new places and enjoy the scenery while also getting a good workout.

Swimming

Swimming is a low impact cardio workout that is gentle on your joints and can help you burn calories, improve your lung capacity, and reduce stress. You can swim in a pool or in open water if you have access to a safe and clean beach or lake.

Personally, I find swimming a great way to cool off on a hot day and get a full body workout without putting too much stress on my joints. I also find it a great way to challenge myself and set new fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Is low impact cardio workout effective for weight loss?

A: Yes, low impact cardio workout can still help you lose weight if you do it consistently and combine it with a healthy diet.

Q: How often should I do low impact cardio workout?

A: It's recommended to do at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week, which can be divided into 30-minute sessions five days a week.

Q: What should I wear for low impact cardio workout?

A: Wear comfortable and breathable clothing that allows you to move freely, and a good pair of shoes that fit well and provide enough support.

Q: How can I stay motivated to do low impact cardio workout?

A: Find a form of exercise that you enjoy and make it a habit by scheduling it into your daily routine. Set realistic goals and track your progress. You can also find a workout buddy or join an online community for support and accountability.

Conclusion of Low Impact Cardio Workout at Home for Beginners

Low impact cardio workout at home for beginners is a great way to improve your fitness level, lose weight, and boost your overall health without putting too much stress on your joints. Walking, dancing, cycling, swimming, and yoga are some of the best low impact cardio workouts that you can do at home or outdoors. Remember to start slowly, wear comfortable clothing, and stay motivated by setting realistic goals and tracking your progress. With consistency and patience, you can achieve your fitness goals and enjoy a healthier and happier life.
