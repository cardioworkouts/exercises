FitOn

FitOn is one of the best free exercise apps no subscription available on the market. It offers a wide range of workout programs, including cardio, strength training, yoga, and more. The app is easy to use and has a great user interface that makes it easy to find the type of workout you want to do.

Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or just stay active, FitOn has a program for you.

Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is another great free exercise app no subscription. It offers a wide range of workouts, from high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to yoga and everything in between. The app is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals, no matter how big or small they may be.

I have personally used Nike Training Club for several years now and have always found it to be a great way to stay active and fit. It also offers a variety of different programs that cater to different skill levels and fitness goals.

7 Minute Workout

Looking for a quick and easy way to get a workout in? Check out the 7 Minute Workout app. As the name suggests, this app offers a quick 7-minute workout that you can do anytime, anywhere. The app includes a variety of exercises that are designed to get your heart rate up and help you burn calories.

I have used the 7 Minute Workout app when I'm short on time or don't have access to a gym. It's a great way to get a quick workout in and keep your body moving.

Yoga for Beginners

If you're new to yoga or looking to improve your skills, check out the Yoga for Beginners app. This app offers a variety of different yoga poses and programs that are designed to help you improve your flexibility, balance, and strength.
