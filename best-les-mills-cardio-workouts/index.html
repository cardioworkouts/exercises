Best Les Mills Cardio Workouts A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old cardio routine? Do you want to try something new and challenging? Look no further than Les Mills cardio workouts. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the best Les Mills cardio workouts for all fitness levels and goals. Whether you want to burn calories, improve your endurance, or just have fun, Les Mills has a workout for you.

Many people struggle with finding a cardio workout that they enjoy and can stick to. Some find running or cycling monotonous, while others find dance-based workouts too intimidating. Les Mills cardio workouts offer a variety of options that are both fun and effective, making it easier to stay committed to your fitness routine.

The target of Les Mills cardio workouts is to provide a full-body workout that improves cardiovascular health, burns calories, and increases endurance. The Les Mills method utilizes music, choreography, and science-based programming to create workouts that are challenging, engaging, and effective. Some of the best Les Mills cardio workouts include BODYCOMBAT, BODYATTACK, RPM, and SH'BAM.

In summary, Les Mills cardio workouts provide a fun and effective way to improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and increase endurance. With a variety of workout options, there is something for everyone at any fitness level.

BODYCOMBAT

BODYCOMBAT is a high-energy martial arts-inspired workout that is designed to improve cardiovascular fitness, build strength, and burn calories. This workout combines moves from karate, boxing, taekwondo, and muay thai to create a fun and challenging workout that will leave you feeling empowered and energized.

Personally, I love BODYCOMBAT because it pushes me out of my comfort zone and challenges me to try new moves. The combination of music and choreography makes the workout feel like a fun dance party, and I always leave feeling strong and accomplished.

BODYATTACK

BODYATTACK is a sports-inspired cardio workout that combines athletic movements like running, jumping, and lunging to improve cardiovascular fitness and agility. This workout is designed to challenge the entire body and improve coordination and balance.

I enjoy BODYATTACK because it feels like a fun and challenging game. The constantly changing movements keep me engaged and motivated, and the combination of cardio and strength training makes for a well-rounded workout.

RPM

RPM is a cycling-based workout that is designed to improve cardiovascular fitness and burn calories. This workout is set to music and is led by a coach who guides you through various resistance and speed intervals.

As someone who enjoys cycling but struggles to motivate myself to do it on my own, RPM is the perfect solution. The music and coaching keep me engaged and motivated, and the intervals make the workout feel like a fun challenge.

SH'BAM

SH'BAM is a dance-based workout that is designed to improve cardiovascular fitness and coordination. This workout is set to popular music and combines easy-to-follow dance moves with bodyweight exercises.

I love SH'BAM because it feels like a fun dance party, and I always leave feeling energized and happy. The choreography is easy to follow, making it a great option for anyone who is intimidated by dance-based workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best Les Mills cardio workout for beginners?

A: RPM is a great option for beginners because it is low-impact and easy to follow. The coach provides guidance throughout the workout, making it less intimidating for newbies.

Q: Can Les Mills cardio workouts be modified for injuries or limitations?

A: Yes, Les Mills workouts can be modified to accommodate injuries or limitations. The coaches are trained to provide modifications and alternative exercises to ensure that everyone can participate safely.

Q: How often should I do Les Mills cardio workouts?

A: It is recommended to do cardio workouts at least three times a week for optimal health benefits. However, you can adjust the frequency based on your fitness goals and personal schedule.

Q: Can I do Les Mills cardio workouts at home?

A: Yes, Les Mills offers on-demand workouts that you can do from the comfort of your own home. All you need is a device and a subscription to Les Mills On Demand.

Conclusion of Best Les Mills Cardio Workouts

Les Mills cardio workouts provide a fun and effective way to improve cardiovascular health, burn calories, and increase endurance. With a variety of workout options, there is something for everyone at any fitness level. Whether you prefer martial arts, sports-inspired movements, cycling, or dance, Les Mills has a workout that will challenge and engage you.
