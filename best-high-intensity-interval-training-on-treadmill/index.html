Best High Intensity Interval Training On Treadmill

Written by William Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring treadmill workouts? Do you want to take your fitness routine to the next level? High intensity interval training (HIIT) on a treadmill might be just what you need!

HIIT is a type of workout that alternates between periods of intense exercise and periods of rest or low intensity exercise. This type of training has been shown to be more effective at burning fat and improving cardiovascular fitness than steady-state cardio workouts.

If you're looking for the best high intensity interval training on treadmill, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to warm up properly before starting your workout. You should also be sure to adjust the incline and speed of the treadmill to match your fitness level and goals.

In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for getting the most out of your high intensity interval training on treadmill. We'll cover everything from proper warm up techniques to specific workouts you can try.

Why choose high intensity interval training on treadmill?

Personally, I love high intensity interval training on a treadmill because it allows me to get a great workout in a short amount of time. I don't have hours to spend at the gym, so HIIT allows me to get in and out while still seeing results.

In addition, HIIT on a treadmill can be tailored to your fitness level and goals. You can adjust the speed and incline of the treadmill to create a challenging workout that pushes you to your limits.

Tips for Best High Intensity Interval Training on Treadmill

1. Warm up properly: Before starting your HIIT workout, be sure to warm up for at least 5-10 minutes. This can include walking or light jogging on the treadmill, or doing some dynamic stretches to get your heart rate up.

2. Alternate high intensity and low intensity periods: The key to a successful HIIT workout is alternating between periods of high intensity exercise and periods of low intensity or rest. You can do this by adjusting the speed and incline of the treadmill.

3. Use the incline: Adding incline to your treadmill workout can increase the intensity and help you burn more calories. Try doing intervals at a high incline for added challenge.

4. Mix it up: Don't be afraid to mix up your HIIT workouts. You can try different speeds, inclines, and even add in some strength training exercises like lunges or squats.

Specific Workouts for Best High Intensity Interval Training on Treadmill

1. Sprint intervals: Alternate between 30 seconds of sprinting at a high speed and 30 seconds of rest or walking at a low speed. Repeat for 10-15 minutes.

2. Hill intervals: Alternate between periods of running or walking at a high incline and periods of rest or walking at a low incline. Repeat for 10-15 minutes.

How to stay safe during high intensity interval training on treadmill?

While high intensity interval training on a treadmill can be a great way to get in shape, it's important to stay safe. Here are some tips:

1. Start slow: If you're new to HIIT, start with shorter intervals and lower speeds. Gradually increase the intensity as your fitness level improves.

2. Listen to your body: If you feel pain or discomfort during your workout, slow down or stop. It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard.

3. Stay hydrated: Be sure to drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.

Conclusion of Best High Intensity Interval Training on Treadmill

High intensity interval training on treadmill can be a great way to improve your fitness and burn fat. By following these tips and incorporating some specific workouts into your routine, you can take your treadmill workouts to the next level. Just remember to warm up properly, listen to your body, and stay safe!

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do high intensity interval training on treadmill?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between.

Q: Can I do HIIT on a treadmill if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, but start slow and gradually increase the intensity as your fitness level improves.

Q: How long should my HIIT workout be?

A: HIIT workouts can be as short as 10-15 minutes or as long as 30-45 minutes, depending on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can I do HIIT on a treadmill if I have joint pain?

A: It's best to talk to your doctor before starting any new exercise routine if you have joint pain. They can help you determine if HIIT on a treadmill is safe for you.
