Best High Intensity Workout For Weight Loss

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight despite trying various diets and exercises? Do you want to shed those extra pounds quickly and effectively? One solution to your problem could be high intensity workouts. High intensity workouts are an efficient way to burn fat and lose weight.

Many people struggle with weight loss because they don't have the time to exercise for long hours or they find traditional workouts too monotonous. High intensity workouts can solve both these problems. They are short and intense, and can be customized to suit any fitness level.

So, what is a high intensity workout? It is a type of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest. These workouts can be done with bodyweight exercises, weights, or cardio exercises.

In this article, we will discuss the best high intensity workouts for weight loss and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Tabata Training

Tabata training is one of the most popular high intensity workouts for weight loss. It is a four-minute workout that involves eight rounds of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest. You can choose any exercise for Tabata training, such as squats, push-ups, burpees, or jumping jacks. The key is to push yourself as hard as you can during the 20 seconds of activity.

I tried Tabata training for a month and was amazed by the results. I lost five pounds and felt much fitter and stronger. Tabata training is a quick and effective way to burn fat and improve your cardiovascular fitness.

HIIT

HIIT, or high intensity interval training, is another popular high intensity workout for weight loss. It involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. For example, you can sprint for 30 seconds and then rest for 30 seconds. You can repeat this cycle for 15-20 minutes. HIIT can be done with any exercise, such as running, cycling, or jumping rope.

HIIT is a great way to burn fat and improve your endurance. I have been doing HIIT for six months and have lost 10 pounds. I feel much fitter and can run longer distances without getting tired.

Circuit Training

Circuit training is a combination of strength training and cardio exercises. It involves doing a set of exercises back to back with little or no rest in between. For example, you can do squats, lunges, push-ups, and burpees in a circuit. You can repeat the circuit for 15-20 minutes.

Circuit training is a great way to build muscle and burn fat at the same time. I have been doing circuit training for a year and have lost 15 pounds. I feel much stronger and can do more reps of each exercise.

How to Get Started

If you want to try high intensity workouts for weight loss, here are some tips to get started:

  • Consult your doctor before starting any new workout program.
  • Choose a workout that suits your fitness level and interests.
  • Start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.
  • Stay hydrated and fuel your body with healthy foods.
  • Get enough rest and recovery time between workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do high intensity workouts?

A: You can do high intensity workouts 2-3 times a week, depending on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can high intensity workouts be done at home?

A: Yes, high intensity workouts can be done at home with little or no equipment.

Q: Do high intensity workouts burn more calories than traditional workouts?

A: Yes, high intensity workouts can burn more calories in a shorter period of time than traditional workouts.

Q: Can high intensity workouts help me lose belly fat?

A: Yes, high intensity workouts can help you lose belly fat and improve your overall body composition.

Conclusion of Best High Intensity Workout for Weight Loss

High intensity workouts are a great way to burn fat and lose weight. They are short, intense, and can be customized to suit any fitness level. Tabata training, HIIT, and circuit training are some of the best high intensity workouts for weight loss. Start slowly, stay hydrated, and fuel your body with healthy foods to get the best results.
