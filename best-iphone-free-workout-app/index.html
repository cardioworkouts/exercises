The Best Free Workout App For Your Iphone

Written by Frank Nov 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive gym memberships or personal trainers to keep you in shape? Do you struggle to stay motivated when working out alone? Well, the solution to your fitness woes might just be in the palm of your hand. With the abundance of free workout apps available for the iPhone, you can have a personal trainer at your fingertips anytime, anywhere.

Not sure which app to choose or where to start? We've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore the best free workout apps for your iPhone, their features, and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

The Target of Best iPhone Free Workout App

The target of the best iPhone free workout app is to provide users with a variety of workout options that can be done at home or on-the-go, without the need for expensive equipment or a gym membership. These apps offer customized workout plans, instructional videos, progress tracking, and motivation to help users stay on track and achieve their fitness goals.

Without further ado, let's dive into our top picks for the best free workout apps for your iPhone.

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club (NTC) is a free app that offers a wide range of workouts for all fitness levels, from beginner to advanced. The app features over 185 workouts, including strength, endurance, yoga, and mobility exercises. All workouts come with instructional videos and audio guidance from Nike Master Trainers. You can also customize your workout plan based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. Plus, the app offers personalized training plans and allows you to track your progress and set reminders to stay motivated.

Personally, I love using NTC for its variety of workouts and the ability to customize my plan based on my fitness goals. The app also offers challenges and rewards, which make working out feel like a game and keeps me motivated to keep going.

2. 7 Minute Workout

If you're short on time but still want to get a quick workout in, the 7 Minute Workout app is perfect for you. As the name suggests, this app offers short, high-intensity workouts that can be done in just 7 minutes. The app features 12 different exercises that target various muscle groups and can be done without any equipment. Plus, the app offers challenges and rewards to keep you motivated and track your progress.

I've found 7 Minute Workout to be a great option for days when I don't have much time to spare but still want to get my heart rate up and break a sweat. The short workouts also make it easy to fit in a workout anytime, anywhere.

The Benefits of Using a Free Workout App

Using a free workout app has many benefits, including:

  • Convenience: You can workout anytime, anywhere, without the need for expensive equipment or a gym membership
  • Customization: You can customize your workout plan based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment
  • Motivation: Many apps offer challenges, rewards, and progress tracking to keep you motivated and on track
  • Variety: Most apps offer a wide range of workouts, so you can switch it up and avoid boredom

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Free Workout App

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your free workout app:

  • Set achievable goals and track your progress
  • Try different workouts to avoid boredom and challenge yourself
  • Stick to a consistent workout schedule
  • Find a workout buddy or accountability partner

3. FitOn

FitOn is another free workout app that offers a variety of workouts for all fitness levels, from cardio and strength training to yoga and Pilates. The app features over 1,000 workouts, including live and on-demand classes led by celebrity trainers. You can also customize your workout plan based on your fitness level and goals, and track your progress and achievements with the app's built-in tracking system.

Personally, I love using FitOn for its live and on-demand classes, which make me feel like I'm working out with a group and provide a sense of community. The app also offers challenges and rewards, which keep me motivated and excited to try new workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: Are free workout apps effective?

A: Yes, free workout apps can be very effective, especially for those who are new to working out or don't have access to a gym. Most apps offer a wide range of workouts that target various muscle groups and can be customized based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can you really get in shape using a free workout app?

A: Yes, you can definitely get in shape using a free workout app, as long as you stick to a consistent workout schedule and challenge yourself with different workouts. Many apps offer progress tracking and rewards to keep you motivated and on track.

Q: Do you need equipment to use a free workout app?

A: It depends on the app and the type of workout you choose. Many apps offer workouts that can be done without any equipment, while others may require basic equipment like dumbbells or resistance bands. However, most apps offer modifications for exercises that require equipment, so you can still get a good workout without it.

Q: Can free workout apps replace a personal trainer?

A: While free workout apps can provide a lot of guidance and motivation, they cannot replace the personalized attention and expertise of a certified personal trainer. However, apps like Nike Training Club offer instructional videos and audio guidance from Nike Master Trainers, which can be very helpful for those who are new to working out.

Conclusion of Best iPhone Free Workout App

Using a free workout app is a great way to stay in shape and achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank. Our top picks for the best free workout apps for your iPhone are Nike Training Club, 7 Minute Workout, and FitOn. Remember to set achievable goals, try different workouts, and stick to a consistent schedule to get the most out of your app. Happy sweating!
