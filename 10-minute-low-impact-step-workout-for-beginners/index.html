10 Minute Low Impact Step Workout For Beginners

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a beginner looking for a low impact workout routine that won't take up too much of your time? Look no further than the 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners! This workout is perfect for those who are just starting out, or for those who want a quick and easy workout that won't put too much strain on their joints.

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you're not sure where to begin. You may be worried about the potential pain and discomfort that can come with exercise, or you may be unsure of how to properly perform certain exercises. These concerns are completely normal, but they shouldn't stop you from starting a workout routine that can improve your overall health and well-being.

The 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners is a great way to get started with a new workout routine. This workout is designed to be low impact, which means it won't put too much strain on your joints or muscles. It's also only 10 minutes long, which makes it easy to fit into your busy schedule.

In this article, we'll go over the basics of the 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners, including the target areas, benefits, and how to perform each exercise properly.

Target Areas

The 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners primarily targets the lower body, including the legs, glutes, and hips. However, it also engages the core and can help improve balance and coordination.

Each exercise in this workout routine is designed to target specific muscle groups, so you can get a full body workout in just 10 minutes.

Personal Experience

As someone who is new to working out, I was hesitant to start with a routine that was too intense. The 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners was the perfect solution for me. It was short, easy to follow, and didn't leave me feeling sore or fatigued afterwards.

I also appreciated that this workout routine didn't require any fancy equipment or a gym membership. All I needed was a step or a sturdy chair, and I was able to complete the entire routine in the comfort of my own home.

Benefits

The 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners has several benefits, including:

  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased muscle tone and strength in the lower body
  • Improved balance and coordination
  • Reduced risk of injury

Additionally, this workout routine is a great way to improve your overall mood and reduce stress levels. Exercise has been shown to release endorphins, which can help boost your mood and reduce feelings of anxiety or depression.

How to Perform Each Exercise

Now that we've gone over the benefits and target areas of the 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners, let's dive into how to perform each exercise properly.

1. Step-Ups

To perform step-ups, start by standing in front of your step or chair. Place your right foot on the step, and press through your heel to lift your body up onto the step. Step back down with your left foot, followed by your right.

Repeat this movement for 30 seconds, then switch sides and perform for another 30 seconds.

2. Knee Lifts

To perform knee lifts, stand in front of your step or chair. Place your left foot on the step, and lift your right knee up towards your chest. Lower your right foot back down to the ground, then repeat on the other side.

Repeat this movement for 30 seconds on each side.

3. Side Steps

To perform side steps, stand to the side of your step or chair. Step up onto the step with your right foot, then bring your left foot up to meet your right. Step back down with your right foot, followed by your left.

Repeat this movement for 30 seconds, then switch sides and perform for another 30 seconds.

4. Calf Raises

To perform calf raises, stand in front of your step or chair. Place the balls of your feet on the edge of the step, then press through your toes to lift your heels up towards the ceiling. Lower your heels back down, then repeat.

Repeat this movement for 30 seconds.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need any equipment to perform the 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners?

A: All you need is a sturdy step or chair to perform this workout routine.

Q: Is this workout routine suitable for beginners?

A: Yes! This workout routine is specifically designed for beginners who are just starting out with their fitness journey.

Q: How often should I perform the 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners?

A: You can perform this workout routine as often as you'd like, but it's recommended to start with 2-3 times per week and gradually increase as your fitness level improves.

Q: Can I modify the exercises if I have mobility issues?

A: Yes! You can modify the exercises as needed to suit your individual needs. For example, you can perform step-ups by starting with a lower step or chair, or you can perform calf raises while seated.

Conclusion of 10 Minute Low Impact Step Workout for Beginners

The 10 minute low impact step workout for beginners is a great way to start your fitness journey without putting too much strain on your joints or muscles. This workout routine is easy to follow, requires minimal equipment, and can be performed in the comfort of your own home.

Remember to start slow and gradually increase the frequency and intensity of your workouts as your fitness level improves. With consistent effort, you'll be able to see improvements in your overall health and well-being in no time!
