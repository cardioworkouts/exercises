Best Hiit Cardio Workouts On Youtube A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to burn calories and get in shape, you've probably heard of HIIT workouts. HIIT, or High-Intensity Interval Training, is a type of cardio workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. It's a popular way to improve cardiovascular health, burn fat, and build muscle.

However, finding the right HIIT cardio workout can be a challenge. There are so many options available on YouTube, and it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your fitness goals. Plus, some workouts may not be suitable for beginners or people with certain health conditions.

In this article, we'll explore the best HIIT cardio workouts on YouTube and help you find the right one for your needs. We'll cover the benefits of HIIT, how to choose a workout, and some of the most popular HIIT workouts available on YouTube.

So, let's get started!

What Are HIIT Cardio Workouts?

HIIT cardio workouts are a form of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. They typically last between 10 and 30 minutes and can be done with or without equipment.

The goal of HIIT cardio workouts is to get your heart rate up quickly and keep it elevated throughout the workout. This leads to a greater calorie burn and improves cardiovascular health.

There are many benefits to HIIT cardio workouts, including:

  • Increased calorie burn
  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased metabolism
  • Improved insulin sensitivity
  • Decreased body fat
  • Improved endurance

Best HIIT Cardio Workouts on YouTube

Now that you know the benefits of HIIT cardio workouts, let's take a look at some of the best workouts available on YouTube.

1. FitnessBlender

FitnessBlender is a popular YouTube channel that offers a wide variety of HIIT cardio workouts. Their workouts range from beginner to advanced and can be done with or without equipment. They also offer workouts that target specific muscle groups and body parts.

One of their most popular workouts is their HIIT Cardio and Abs Workout. This workout is 37 minutes long and includes 20 seconds of high-intensity exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest. It's a great workout for burning calories and toning your abs.

2. The Body Coach TV

The Body Coach TV is another popular YouTube channel that offers HIIT cardio workouts. Their workouts are designed to be short and intense, with most lasting between 10 and 20 minutes. They also offer workouts that require no equipment.

One of their most popular workouts is their 15 Minute Full Body HIIT Workout. This workout includes exercises like burpees, mountain climbers, and jumping jacks, and is designed to get your heart rate up quickly.

3. POPSUGAR Fitness

POPSUGAR Fitness is a YouTube channel that offers a variety of workout videos, including HIIT cardio workouts. Their workouts are designed to be fun and engaging, with many featuring popular music and dance moves.

One of their most popular workouts is their 30-Minute Cardio Latin Dance Workout. This workout combines HIIT cardio with Latin dance moves and is a great way to burn calories while having fun.

How to Choose the Right HIIT Cardio Workout

When choosing a HIIT cardio workout on YouTube, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your fitness level. If you're a beginner, look for workouts that are designed for beginners and include low-impact exercises.

You should also consider the length of the workout. If you're short on time, look for workouts that are 20 minutes or less. If you have more time, you can opt for longer workouts.

Finally, consider your goals. If you're looking to burn fat and lose weight, look for workouts that include high-intensity exercises and focus on full-body movements. If you're looking to build muscle, look for workouts that include strength exercises like push-ups and squats.

Conclusion of Best HIIT Cardio Workouts on YouTube

There are many great HIIT cardio workouts available on YouTube, and finding the right one for your needs is key to achieving your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced exerciser, there's a HIIT workout out there for you.

So, take some time to explore the workouts we've highlighted in this article and find the one that works best for you. With a little dedication and hard work, you can achieve the results you're looking for.

Question and Answer

Q. Can HIIT cardio workouts be done at home?

A. Yes, many HIIT cardio workouts can be done at home with little to no equipment.

Q. How often should I do HIIT cardio workouts?

A. It depends on your fitness goals and current fitness level. Beginners may want to start with 1-2 HIIT workouts per week, while more advanced exercisers may do 3-4 HIIT workouts per week.

Q. Are HIIT cardio workouts suitable for people with certain health conditions?

A. It depends on the condition. People with certain heart conditions, for example, may need to avoid high-intensity exercise. It's always best to check with your doctor before starting a new workout program.

Q. How long should a HIIT cardio workout be?

A. HIIT cardio workouts can range from 10 minutes to 30 minutes or more. The length of the workout will depend on your fitness level and goals.
