Beginner Gym Workout Female Cardio And Strength

Written by Petter Jul 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner starting your fitness journey and looking for a beginner gym workout that includes both cardio and strength training? If so, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with a beginner gym workout routine that focuses on both cardio and strength training for females.

Starting a new workout routine can be overwhelming, especially if you are new to the gym. You may feel unsure of what exercises to do, how to use the equipment, or how to structure your workout. Additionally, you may have concerns about staying motivated or getting discouraged if you don't see results right away.

Our target for this article is to provide you with a beginner gym workout that will address all of these concerns and help you start your fitness journey off on the right foot. By the end of this article, you will have a clear understanding of how to structure your beginner gym workout, what exercises to do, and how to stay motivated throughout your fitness journey.

The Importance of Cardio and Strength Training

Before we dive into the workout routine, let's quickly touch on why we are focusing on both cardio and strength training. Cardiovascular exercise (cardio) is essential for maintaining a healthy heart, improving endurance, and burning calories. Strength training, on the other hand, helps to build muscle mass, increase bone density, and boost metabolism.

By combining both cardio and strength training into your workout routine, you are getting the best of both worlds. You will burn calories, build muscle, and improve overall health and fitness.

My Personal Experience with Beginner Gym Workout: Female Cardio and Strength

When I first started going to the gym, I was intimidated and unsure of what to do. I would usually spend most of my time on the cardio machines, not knowing how to use the weight machines or free weights.

However, after doing some research and talking to a personal trainer, I realized the importance of strength training and began incorporating it into my workout routine. I noticed a significant difference in my body composition and overall strength. I also found that I enjoyed the challenge of lifting weights and pushing myself to get stronger.

Beginner Gym Workout: Female Cardio and Strength Routine

Now, let's dive into the actual workout routine. Remember to always warm up before starting your workout and cool down after completing it. Here is a sample beginner gym workout routine that includes both cardio and strength training:

Cardio:

  • 5-minute warm-up on the treadmill, elliptical, or stationary bike
  • 20 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio on a machine of your choice
  • 5-minute cool-down on the treadmill, elliptical, or stationary bike

Strength Training:

  • Bodyweight squats (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • Assisted pull-ups (3 sets of 12 reps)
  • Barbell bench press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Dumbbell shoulder press (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Seated cable row (3 sets of 8-10 reps)
  • Plank (3 sets of 30 seconds)

Remember to increase the weight as you get stronger and to always use proper form to prevent injury.

FAQs About Beginner Gym Workout: Female Cardio and Strength

1. How often should I do this workout routine?

It is recommended to do this workout routine 2-3 times per week with a day of rest in between each workout.

2. Do I need to lift heavy weights to see results?

No, you do not need to lift heavy weights to see results. It's more important to use proper form and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.

3. Can I substitute different exercises?

Yes, you can substitute different exercises as long as they target the same muscle groups. It is important to include a variety of exercises to prevent boredom and to challenge your muscles in different ways.

4. How long will it take to see results?

Results vary depending on individual fitness level, diet, and consistency. However, with consistent exercise and a healthy diet, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Conclusion of Beginner Gym Workout: Female Cardio and Strength

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, but incorporating both cardio and strength training into your routine will provide numerous health benefits. By following a structured beginner gym workout routine, you can ensure that you are targeting all muscle groups and getting the most out of your workout. Remember to always warm up, cool down, and listen to your body. Consistency is key to achieving your fitness goals!
