Best Cardio Video Workouts At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 31, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you searching for an effective way to burn calories and stay fit without leaving your home? Do you find it challenging to motivate yourself to exercise regularly? If yes, then you are not alone. Many people struggle to find the time, energy, and resources to visit a gym or fitness center regularly. Fortunately, there is a solution that can help you overcome these obstacles and achieve your fitness goals. It is called cardio video workouts at home.

Cardio video workouts at home can help you burn calories, improve heart health, increase endurance, and reduce stress. They are also convenient, affordable, and easy to follow. However, finding the best cardio video workouts at home can be overwhelming, given the vast array of options available online. That's why we have put together this step-by-step guide to help you choose the best cardio video workouts at home that suit your needs and preferences.

When it comes to cardio video workouts at home, there are endless possibilities. From high-intensity interval training (HIIT) to dance fitness, from kickboxing to yoga, there is something for everyone. However, not all cardio video workouts at home are created equal. Some are more effective than others in terms of burning calories, improving heart health, and building endurance. Therefore, it is essential to choose the best cardio video workouts at home that align with your fitness goals and interests.

In this article, we will share the best cardio video workouts at home that you can try today. We will also explain their target areas and benefits, share personal experiences, and provide step-by-step instructions on how to perform them correctly. By the end of this guide, you will have a clear idea of the best cardio video workouts at home that suit your fitness level, schedule, and goals.

1. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a popular form of cardio video workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief periods of rest or active recovery. HIIT is an efficient way to burn calories, improve cardiovascular fitness, and increase metabolism. It is also time-efficient, as you can complete an effective HIIT workout in as little as 20 minutes.

Personally, I have tried several HIIT workouts at home, and I found them challenging but rewarding. My favorite HIIT workout is the 7-minute workout, which involves seven high-intensity exercises for 30 seconds each, with 10 seconds of rest in between. I usually perform two or three sets of the 7-minute workout, depending on my energy level and time availability.

To perform the 7-minute workout, follow these steps:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before starting the workout, warm up your body by doing some light cardio exercises, such as jogging in place, jumping jacks, or high knees. Warm up for 5-10 minutes to increase your heart rate, loosen up your muscles, and prevent injury.

Step 2: Set up the timer

Download a timer app on your phone or use a stopwatch to time your workout. Set the timer for 30 seconds of exercise and 10 seconds of rest. You can also customize the number of sets and exercises according to your preference.

Step 3: Perform the exercises

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, with 10 seconds of rest in between. The seven exercises are:

Squats

Push-ups

Lunges

Jumping Jacks

Wall Sit

Crunches

Plank

Step 4: Cool down

After completing the workout, cool down your body by doing some stretching exercises, such as hamstring stretch, quad stretch, and shoulder stretch. Stretch for 5-10 minutes to improve flexibility, reduce muscle soreness, and promote relaxation.

2. Dance Fitness

Dance fitness is another fun and effective way to do cardio video workouts at home. Dance fitness combines high-energy dance moves with upbeat music to create an enjoyable and challenging workout. Dance fitness can help you burn calories, improve coordination, and enhance mood. It is also suitable for all fitness levels and ages.

Personally, I love dance fitness because it doesn't feel like a workout. I enjoy moving my body to the rhythm of the music and following the dance instructor's choreography. My favorite dance fitness program is Zumba, which is a Latin-inspired dance workout that features salsa, merengue, reggaeton, and other dance styles. I usually do a 30-minute Zumba workout three times a week.

To perform a Zumba workout, follow these steps:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before starting the workout, warm up your body by doing some light cardio exercises, such as marching in place, side steps, or hip circles. Warm up for 5-10 minutes to increase your heart rate, loosen up your muscles, and prevent injury.

Step 2: Choose a Zumba video

Search for a Zumba video on YouTube or any other fitness app. Choose a video that suits your fitness level and preferences. There are various Zumba videos available, ranging from beginner to advanced, and with different themes and music styles.

Step 3: Follow the instructor's moves

Start the video and follow the instructor's moves. The instructor will guide you through a series of dance moves, such as salsa, merengue, cumbia, and more. Don't worry if you can't follow all the moves at first. Just keep moving and have fun.

Step 4: Cool down

After completing the workout, cool down your body by doing some stretching exercises, such as hamstring stretch, quad stretch, and shoulder stretch. Stretch for 5-10 minutes to improve flexibility, reduce muscle soreness, and promote relaxation.

Q&A

Q1. How often should I do cardio video workouts at home?

A1. It depends on your fitness level, schedule, and goals. Ideally, you should do cardio video workouts at home at least three times a week for 20-30 minutes each session. However, you can adjust the frequency and duration according to your preference and availability.

Q2. What are the benefits of cardio video workouts at home?

A2. Cardio video workouts at home can help you burn calories, improve heart health, increase endurance, and reduce stress. They are also convenient, affordable, and easy to follow.

Q3. What are the best cardio video workouts at home for beginners?

A3. The best cardio video workouts at home for beginners are low-impact exercises, such as walking, jogging in place, cycling, or dancing. You can also try beginner-level HIIT workouts or bodyweight exercises that require no equipment.

Q4. How can I stay motivated to do cardio video workouts at home?

A4. You can stay motivated to do cardio video workouts at home by setting realistic goals, tracking your progress, varying your workouts, and finding a workout buddy or accountability partner. You can also reward yourself after reaching a milestone or completing a challenging workout.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Video Workouts at Home

Cardio video workouts at home are an excellent way to burn calories, improve heart health, increase endurance, and reduce stress. By choosing the best cardio video workouts at home that suit your needs and preferences, you can achieve your fitness goals without leaving your home. Whether you prefer HIIT, dance fitness, or any other form of cardio video workout, make sure to warm up, perform the exercises correctly, cool down, and stretch afterward. With consistency, dedication, and patience, you can enjoy the benefits of cardio video workouts at home and lead a healthier and happier life.
