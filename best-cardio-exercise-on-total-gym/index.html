Tips for Using the Rowing Machine on Total Gym

If you're new to using the rowing machine on Total Gym, here are a few tips to help you get started:

1. Start Slow: If you're new to rowing, start with a low resistance level and work your way up. It's better to start slow and gradually increase the resistance than to push yourself too hard and risk injury.

2. Focus on Form: When rowing, it's essential to focus on your form. Make sure that your back is straight, your shoulders are relaxed, and your elbows are close to your body. This will help you avoid injury and ensure that you're getting the most out of the exercise.

3. Mix It Up: While the rowing machine is an excellent exercise, it's essential to mix it up and try other cardio exercises as well. This will help prevent boredom and ensure that you're getting a well-rounded workout.
