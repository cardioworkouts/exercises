Discover The Best Free Personal Training Apps For Your Fitness Journey

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to stay motivated and keep track of your fitness goals? Do you want to have a personal trainer but don't have the budget for it? Well, good news! You can now have a virtual personal trainer with the help of the best free personal training apps. These apps can help you stay on track and achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank.

It can be overwhelming to choose the right app for you, especially with the multitude of options out there. Some apps might not cater to your specific needs or might not be user-friendly. That's why it's important to know the target of the best free personal training apps and which ones are worth trying.

The target of the best free personal training apps is to provide an accessible and convenient way for people to achieve their fitness goals. These apps offer personalized workout plans, nutrition guides, progress tracking, and motivation to keep you going. Here are some of the best free personal training apps that you can try:

Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a free app that offers over 500 workout videos ranging from HIIT, strength training, cardio, and yoga. You can filter the workouts based on your fitness level, duration, and equipment. The app also has a calendar feature that helps you plan your workouts and track your progress. I have personally tried this app, and I love how the workouts are challenging yet doable. Plus, the instructors are very informative and motivating.

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free nutrition app that helps you track your food intake, macros, and calories. The app has a vast database of food items, so you can easily log your meals. It also has a barcode scanner feature that makes tracking your food intake more convenient. The app also syncs with other fitness apps like Fitbit and MapMyRun. I have been using this app for years, and it has been a game-changer for me in terms of achieving my weight loss goals.

C25K

C25K stands for Couch to 5K, and it is a free app that is perfect for beginners who want to start running. The app provides a 8-week training plan that gradually builds up your endurance and helps you run a 5K. The app also has audio cues that guide you through the workouts, so you don't have to keep looking at your phone. I have recommended this app to my friends who are new to running, and they have all been successful in completing their first 5K.

Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers over 200 workouts designed by Nike trainers. The app has workout plans for all levels, from beginner to advanced. You can filter the workouts based on your fitness goals, equipment, and workout duration. The app also has a feature where you can challenge your friends to a workout and track each other's progress. I love the variety of the workouts in this app, and it's a great way to switch up your routine.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, these apps are completely free to download and use. However, some apps might offer premium features that require a subscription.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm a beginner?

A: Absolutely! These apps offer workout plans and videos for all levels, so you can start at your own pace.

Q: Do I need equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the workout. Some workouts might require equipment like dumbbells or resistance bands, while others only require your bodyweight.

Q: Can I use these apps to track my progress?

A: Yes, most of these apps have a progress tracking feature that allows you to log your workouts and see your progress over time.

Conclusion of Best Free Personal Training Apps

Using the best free personal training apps can be a great way to stay motivated and achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there's an app out there for you. Fitness Blender, MyFitnessPal, C25K, and Nike Training Club are just some of the best free personal training apps that you can try. Download one today and start your fitness journey!
