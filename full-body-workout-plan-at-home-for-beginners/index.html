Full Body Workout Plan At Home For Beginners

Written by Robby Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for gym memberships and not being able to make it to the gym regularly? Or are you just starting out with your fitness journey and are unsure of where to begin? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this post, we will be sharing a full body workout plan that you can do in the comfort of your home, perfect for beginners.

Starting out with a new workout routine can be overwhelming, especially when you are unsure of what exercises to do and which body parts to focus on. It can also be frustrating when you don’t see results as quickly as you would like. We understand the struggles of starting out, and that’s why we have created this full body workout plan at home for beginners.

What is a Full Body Workout Plan?

A full body workout plan is a workout routine that targets all the major muscle groups in your body. This means that you will be working out your upper body, lower body, and core. A full body workout plan is ideal for beginners because it allows you to get a full-body workout in a shorter amount of time, and it helps to improve overall strength and fitness.

In this full body workout plan, we have included exercises that do not require any equipment, so you can easily do them at home. However, if you do have access to equipment such as dumbbells or resistance bands, you can incorporate them into your workout to make it more challenging.

My Personal Experience with Full Body Workouts

Before I started working out regularly, I was intimidated by the gym and didn’t know where to start. I started doing full body workouts at home and found that it was a great way to ease into working out. I was able to target all the major muscle groups in my body without feeling overwhelmed. As I got stronger, I started incorporating equipment into my workouts to make it more challenging.

The Benefits of a Full Body Workout Plan

There are several benefits of incorporating a full body workout plan into your fitness routine. First and foremost, it saves time because you are working out all the major muscle groups in one session. It also helps to improve overall strength and fitness, and it can be a great way to burn calories and lose weight. Additionally, full body workouts can help to improve flexibility and balance.

How to Do a Full Body Workout at Home

Here is a sample full body workout plan that you can do at home:

  • Jumping jacks - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Squats - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 10 reps on each leg
  • Plank - Hold for 30 seconds, rest for 10 seconds, repeat for 3 sets
  • Mountain climbers - 3 sets of 10 reps on each leg
  • Crunches - 3 sets of 10 reps

Remember to take breaks between sets and to stay hydrated throughout your workout. As you get stronger, you can increase the number of sets and reps, or you can incorporate equipment to make it more challenging.

Tips for a Successful Full Body Workout at Home

Here are some tips to help you have a successful full body workout at home:

  • Set a schedule and stick to it
  • Find a workout buddy to hold you accountable
  • Warm up before your workout to prevent injury
  • Stay hydrated throughout your workout
  • Listen to your body and take breaks when needed

FAQs About Full Body Workout Plan at Home for Beginners

Q: How often should I do a full body workout?

A: It is recommended to do a full body workout 2-3 times per week with at least one day of rest in between.

Q: Do I need equipment to do a full body workout at home?

A: No, you do not need equipment to do a full body workout at home. However, if you do have access to equipment such as dumbbells or resistance bands, you can incorporate them into your workout to make it more challenging.

Q: Can a full body workout help me lose weight?

A: Yes, a full body workout can help you burn calories and lose weight when combined with a healthy diet.

Q: How long should a full body workout last?

A: A full body workout should last between 30-60 minutes.

Conclusion of Full Body Workout Plan at Home for Beginners

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, but it doesn’t have to be. With this full body workout plan at home for beginners, you can get a full-body workout in the comfort of your own home without any equipment. Remember to start slow and listen to your body, and you will be on your way to a healthier, stronger you!
