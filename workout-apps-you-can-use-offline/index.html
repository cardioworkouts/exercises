Stay Fit Anytime Anywhere With These Offline Workout Apps

Written by William Jul 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you often find yourself without an internet connection but still want to get your daily dose of exercise? It can be frustrating when your favorite workout apps require an internet connection to function. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we will introduce you to the best workout apps you can use offline so you can stay fit anytime, anywhere.

The target of workout apps you can use offline is for those who have limited access to the internet or are frequently in areas with poor connectivity. It's also a great option for those who don't want to use up their mobile data or those who prefer to keep their workouts private.

Without further ado, let's jump into the list of the best offline workout apps:

1. Nike Training Club

If you're looking for a comprehensive workout app that you can use offline, Nike Training Club is a great option. This app offers over 185 workouts, ranging from bodyweight exercises to full-equipment exercises. You can choose from different workout plans, depending on your fitness level and goals. Plus, the app also has video tutorials to help you perform each exercise correctly.

I personally use Nike Training Club when I travel and don't have access to the internet. The app has a wide variety of workouts that keep me motivated and challenged, and I love that I can still track my progress even without an internet connection.

2. JEFIT

JEFIT is another great app that you can use offline. This app is focused on strength training and offers over 1300 exercises with detailed instructions and animations. You can create your own workout plans or choose from pre-made ones, and the app also allows you to track your progress and set goals.

What I love about JEFIT is that it's perfect for weightlifting enthusiasts who want to improve their strength and build muscle. The app has a vast library of exercises with different variations that keep my workouts exciting and challenging.

3. Seven - 7 Minute Workout

If you're short on time but still want to get a quick workout in, Seven is the perfect app for you. This app offers 7-minute workouts that target different muscle groups and can be done anywhere, anytime. You don't need any equipment, and the app also has voice prompts to guide you through each exercise.

I often use Seven when I'm traveling and don't have access to a gym. The workouts are quick but effective, and I love that I can do them in my hotel room without any equipment.

4. Fitbod

Fitbod is an app that creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. The app has over 400 exercises with detailed instructions and animations, and it also allows you to track your progress and set goals.

What I love about Fitbod is that it takes the guesswork out of my workouts. The app creates personalized plans that are tailored to my fitness level and goals, and I love that I can still use it even without an internet connection.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still track my progress even without an internet connection?

A: Yes, all the apps on this list allow you to track your progress and set goals even without an internet connection.

Q: Do these apps require any equipment?

A: It depends on the app. Nike Training Club and JEFIT offer exercises that require equipment, while Seven and Fitbod offer bodyweight exercises that don't require any equipment.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: All the apps on this list offer free versions with limited features. If you want to unlock all the features, you'll need to upgrade to the premium version.

Q: Can I use these apps on both iOS and Android devices?

A: Yes, all the apps on this list are available on both iOS and Android devices.

Conclusion of Workout Apps You Can Use Offline

Whether you're traveling or just don't have access to the internet, these offline workout apps are a great way to stay fit and healthy. From strength training to quick bodyweight workouts, there's something for everyone on this list. So download one (or all) of these apps and start your fitness journey today!
