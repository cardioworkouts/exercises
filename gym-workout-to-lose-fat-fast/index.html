Question and Answer

Q: Do I need to go to the gym to lose fat fast?
A: No, you do not need to go to the gym to lose fat fast. You can also do bodyweight exercises at home or go for a run outside.

Q: How often should I exercise to lose fat fast?
A: You should aim to exercise for at least 30 minutes a day, five times a week, to lose fat fast.

Q: Should I do cardio or strength training first?
A: It is recommended to do strength training first and then cardio. This is because strength training requires more energy, and you want to have the energy to perform the exercises correctly.

Q: Can I eat junk food and still lose fat fast?
A: No, you cannot eat junk food and still lose fat fast. It is essential to eat a healthy and balanced diet to support your weight loss goals.
