This Best Workout Plan For Beginners To Lose Weight For Everyday

Written by Eveline Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a beginner looking to lose weight, starting a workout plan can be intimidating. With so many options out there, it can be hard to know where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we'll share the best workout plan for beginners to lose weight and give you some tips on how to get started.

Are you struggling to lose weight? Do you feel like you've tried everything but nothing seems to work? Are you looking for a workout plan that is easy to follow and will help you shed those extra pounds? If so, you're not alone. Losing weight can be a difficult journey, but with the right workout plan, you can achieve your goals.

Best workout plan for beginners to lose weight

The best workout plan for beginners to lose weight is a combination of cardio and strength training. Cardio exercises like walking, jogging, or swimming, help you burn calories and shed fat. Strength training exercises like weight lifting or bodyweight exercises, help you build muscle and boost your metabolism.

It's important to note that diet also plays a significant role in weight loss. To see results, you need to create a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than you burn. This can be achieved by eating a healthy, balanced diet and tracking your calories.

Now, let's dive into the specifics of the best workout plan for beginners to lose weight.

Cardio

Cardio exercises are great for burning calories and improving your overall fitness. Here are some cardio exercises you can try:

Walking: Walking is a great low-impact exercise that is perfect for beginners. Try to walk for 30 minutes a day, 5 days a week.

Jogging: If you're looking for a more intense cardio workout, try jogging. Start with short intervals and gradually increase the time and distance.

Swimming: Swimming is a great full-body workout that is easy on your joints. Try to swim for 30 minutes a day, 5 days a week.

Strength training

Strength training helps you build muscle, which in turn boosts your metabolism and helps you burn more calories. Here are some strength training exercises you can try:

Weight lifting: If you have access to a gym, try using weights to build muscle. Start with light weights and gradually increase the weight as you get stronger.

Bodyweight exercises: If you don't have access to a gym, you can still do strength training exercises using your bodyweight. Try push-ups, squats, lunges, and planks.

Conclusion of best workout plan for beginners to lose weight

If you're a beginner looking to lose weight, the best workout plan for you is a combination of cardio and strength training exercises. Remember, diet also plays a significant role in weight loss, so make sure you're eating a healthy, balanced diet and tracking your calories. Start with small, achievable goals and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. With patience and consistency, you can achieve your weight loss goals.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio?

A: Aim to do cardio for at least 30 minutes a day, 5 days a week. You can also break it up into shorter sessions throughout the day if that works better for you.

Q: How often should I do strength training?

A: Aim to do strength training exercises 2-3 times a week, with at least one rest day in between.

Q: Can I lose weight without exercise?

A: While exercise is an important part of weight loss, you can still lose weight by creating a calorie deficit through diet alone. However, exercise has many other health benefits and can help you maintain your weight loss in the long run.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Everyone's weight loss journey is different, but you can expect to see results within a few weeks of starting a consistent workout and diet plan. Keep in mind that weight loss is not always linear and there may be ups and downs along the way.
