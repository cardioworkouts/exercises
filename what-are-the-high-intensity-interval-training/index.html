What Are High Intensity Interval Training

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours at the gym without seeing any results? Or maybe you're looking for a new workout routine that will challenge you and help you reach your fitness goals? If so, high intensity interval training (HIIT) might be just what you need!

HIIT is a type of workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of training has become increasingly popular in recent years because it is a highly effective way to burn fat, build muscle, and improve overall fitness levels.

So, what exactly is high intensity interval training? In simple terms, it involves alternating between periods of all-out effort and periods of rest or active recovery. For example, you might sprint as fast as you can for 30 seconds, followed by a 60-second rest period, and then repeat this cycle for a set amount of time.

One of the key benefits of HIIT is that it can be done in a relatively short amount of time. Because it involves such intense effort, a typical HIIT workout might only last 20-30 minutes. However, this short time frame can be incredibly effective for burning calories and improving cardiovascular health.

My Personal Experience with HIIT

I first discovered HIIT a few years ago when I was looking for a way to switch up my workout routine. I had been doing the same exercises for months and was starting to feel bored and unmotivated. So, I decided to give HIIT a try.

At first, it was incredibly challenging. I was used to longer, slower workouts and wasn't sure if I could handle the intensity of HIIT. However, I quickly realized that the short bursts of effort followed by rest periods helped me push myself more than I ever had before.

Over time, I started to see real results from my HIIT workouts. I lost weight, gained muscle, and felt more energized and confident than ever before. Now, I'm a huge fan of HIIT and recommend it to anyone who is looking for a challenging, effective workout.

The Benefits of HIIT

There are many benefits to incorporating HIIT into your workout routine. Here are just a few:

1. Increased Fat Burning

HIIT is one of the most effective ways to burn fat. Because it involves such intense effort, your body continues to burn calories even after you've finished your workout. This is known as the "afterburn effect" or excess post-exercise oxygen consumption (EPOC).

2. Improved Cardiovascular Health

HIIT has been shown to improve cardiovascular health by increasing the strength and efficiency of your heart and lungs. This can help reduce the risk of heart disease, stroke, and other cardiovascular conditions.

3. Increased Muscle Tone

HIIT can help you build muscle and improve overall muscle tone. This is because the short bursts of intense effort help stimulate muscle growth and improve muscle endurance.

4. Time Efficiency

One of the biggest benefits of HIIT is that it is a highly efficient workout. Because it involves such intense effort, you can get a great workout in a relatively short amount of time.

How to Get Started with HIIT

If you're interested in trying HIIT, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

- Start Slow: If you're new to HIIT, start with shorter, less intense workouts and gradually work your way up to longer and more intense ones.

- Listen to Your Body: HIIT is a demanding workout, so it's important to listen to your body and take breaks when you need them.

- Mix It Up: To avoid boredom and keep your body challenged, mix up your HIIT workouts with different exercises and routines.

Q&A: What Are High Intensity Interval Training?

Q: Is HIIT suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, HIIT can be suitable for beginners as long as they start slow and gradually build up their intensity and duration.

Q: How often should I do HIIT?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week with rest days in between.

Q: Can I do HIIT at home?

A: Yes, HIIT can be done at home with little to no equipment. There are many online resources available for at-home HIIT workouts.

Q: How long should a typical HIIT workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout should last 20-30 minutes.

Conclusion of What Are High Intensity Interval Training

High intensity interval training is a highly effective workout that can help you burn fat, build muscle, and improve overall fitness levels. By incorporating HIIT into your workout routine, you can achieve your fitness goals in a shorter amount of time and with less effort. So, why not give it a try and see for yourself?
