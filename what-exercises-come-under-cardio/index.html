What Exercises Come Under Cardio A Complete Guide

Written by William Sep 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for exercises that can keep your heart healthy and improve your overall fitness level? Are you confused about what exercises come under cardio? Well, you are not alone. Many people find it challenging to determine which exercises are classified as cardio and which ones are not.

If you want to improve your cardiovascular health, you need to know what exercises come under cardio. Cardio exercise, also known as aerobic exercise, is an activity that increases your heart rate and breathing for an extended period. It helps your heart pump blood more efficiently, improves lung function, and strengthens your muscles.

So, what exercises come under cardio?

What Exercises Come Under Cardio?

Cardio exercises include any activity that increases your heart rate and breathing, such as running, cycling, swimming, dancing, or brisk walking. These exercises are usually performed for an extended period and require moderate to high intensity.

When it comes to cardio, there are two types of exercises: steady-state and high-intensity interval training (HIIT). Steady-state cardio involves maintaining a consistent intensity level for an extended period, such as jogging or cycling at a moderate pace. HIIT, on the other hand, involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise, such as sprinting or jumping jacks.

Both types of cardio exercises offer health benefits, and you can choose the one that suits your fitness level and goals.

Now that you know what exercises come under cardio let's dive deeper into each type of cardio exercise and their benefits.

Benefits of Cardio Exercises

Cardio exercises offer numerous health benefits, including:

1. Improved Cardiovascular Health

Cardio exercises help improve your heart health by strengthening your heart muscles, reducing blood pressure, and increasing blood flow. They also help lower your risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic conditions.

2. Weight Loss

Cardio exercises are an excellent way to burn calories and lose weight. They increase your metabolism, which helps you burn more calories even when you're not exercising.

3. Improved Lung Function

Cardio exercises help improve lung function by increasing the amount of oxygen you breathe in and the amount of carbon dioxide you exhale. They also help reduce shortness of breath and improve your endurance.

4. Reduced Stress and Anxiety

Cardio exercises help reduce stress and anxiety by increasing the production of endorphins, which are natural mood-boosters. They also help improve your sleep quality and reduce the risk of depression.

Now that you know the benefits of cardio exercises let's explore some of the most popular cardio exercises.

Popular Cardio Exercises

Here are some of the most popular cardio exercises:

1. Running

Running is a great form of cardio exercise that requires no equipment and can be done anywhere. It helps build strong bones, improves cardiovascular health, and burns calories.

2. Cycling

Cycling is another popular cardio exercise that can be done indoors or outdoors. It's a low-impact exercise that improves cardiovascular health, strengthens leg muscles, and burns calories.

3. Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that's easy on the joints and ideal for people with injuries or arthritis. It improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, and burns calories.

4. Dancing

Dancing is a fun and effective form of cardio exercise that improves cardiovascular health, coordination, and balance. It also burns calories and boosts your mood.

Now that you know the most popular cardio exercises let's answer some common questions about cardio exercises.

Question and Answer

1. What is the ideal duration of cardio exercise?

The ideal duration of cardio exercise depends on your fitness level and goals. If you're just starting, aim for 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per day. As you get fitter, you can increase it to 60 minutes or more.

2. Can I do cardio every day?

Yes, you can do cardio every day, but it's essential to listen to your body and avoid overtraining. It's also essential to include rest days in your workout routine to allow your body to recover.

3. Can I do cardio without losing muscle?

Yes, you can do cardio without losing muscle by combining it with strength training and a balanced diet. Strength training helps build and maintain muscle mass, while a balanced diet provides the necessary nutrients for muscle growth and repair.

4. What's the best time to do cardio?

The best time to do cardio is when you can fit it into your schedule and when you feel most energized. Some people prefer to do it in the morning, while others prefer to do it in the evening. Choose a time that works best for you and stick to it.

Conclusion of What Exercises Come Under Cardio

Cardio exercises are essential for maintaining good health and improving fitness levels. They help improve cardiovascular health, aid in weight loss, improve lung function, and reduce stress and anxiety. Running, cycling, swimming, and dancing are some of the most popular cardio exercises. Remember to include cardio exercises in your workout routine and choose the one that suits your fitness level and goals.
