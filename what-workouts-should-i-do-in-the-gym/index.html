What Workouts Should I Do In The Gym

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you new to the gym and feeling overwhelmed with all the equipment and exercises available? Or maybe you're a regular gym-goer but are looking to switch up your routine. Whatever your situation may be, figuring out what workouts you should do in the gym can be a daunting task. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to deciding what workouts to do in the gym is the fear of not seeing results or wasting time on ineffective exercises. It's important to remember that everyone's fitness journey is unique and what works for one person may not work for another. However, there are some tried and true exercises that can benefit most individuals.

The target of this article is to provide guidance on what workouts to do in the gym, as well as related keywords such as gym exercises, gym routine, and gym workout plan.

So, what workouts should you do in the gym? We recommend a combination of strength training and cardio. Strength training can include exercises such as squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and pull-ups. These exercises target major muscle groups and can help increase muscle mass and strength. Cardio, on the other hand, can include activities such as running, cycling, or using the elliptical machine. Cardio exercises can help improve cardiovascular health and aid in weight loss.

Building a Gym Routine

When it comes to building a gym routine, it's important to focus on your personal fitness goals. For example, if you're looking to build muscle mass, you may want to focus more on strength training exercises. If weight loss is your main goal, incorporating more cardio into your routine may be beneficial.

Personally, I like to start my gym routine with a few dynamic stretches to warm up my muscles. Then, I'll move onto strength training exercises such as squats and bench presses. I'll finish my workout with some cardio, usually on the treadmill or elliptical.

Trying New Exercises

If you're feeling bored with your current gym routine or are looking to switch things up, trying new exercises can be a great way to challenge your body and prevent plateauing. Some exercises you may want to try include kettlebell swings, TRX suspension training, or plyometric exercises.

I recently tried a TRX suspension training class and was pleasantly surprised with how challenging it was. The suspension straps added an extra level of difficulty to traditional bodyweight exercises such as push-ups and squats.

Gym Workout Plan

If you're looking for more structure in your gym routine, creating a workout plan can be helpful. A workout plan can help you stay on track and make sure you're targeting all the major muscle groups. There are many workout plan templates available online, or you can work with a personal trainer to create a plan tailored to your specific goals.

How to Stay Motivated

Staying motivated to go to the gym can be tough, especially on days when you're feeling tired or stressed. One thing that helps me stay motivated is setting achievable goals for myself. For example, I may set a goal to increase my squat weight by 10 pounds or run a 5k in under 30 minutes. Having a specific goal to work towards can help keep you motivated and accountable.

Conclusion of What Workouts Should I Do in the Gym

Remember, finding the right gym routine is all about trial and error. Don't be afraid to try new exercises and switch things up to find what works best for you. Incorporating a combination of strength training and cardio is a great place to start, and creating a workout plan can help provide structure and accountability. Most importantly, listen to your body and make sure to rest and recover when needed.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I go to the gym?

A: This depends on your personal fitness goals and schedule. Generally, we recommend at least 3-4 days of exercise per week.

Q: Should I do cardio or strength training first?

A: This is a personal preference, but many individuals prefer to start with strength training exercises and finish with cardio. This way, you have more energy for the heavier lifting exercises.

Q: How long should I spend at the gym each session?

A: Again, this depends on your personal fitness goals and schedule. However, we recommend aiming for at least 30-45 minutes of exercise per session.

Q: Do I need to lift heavy weights to see results?

A: Not necessarily. While lifting heavy weights can help increase muscle mass and strength, lighter weights with higher repetitions can also be effective.
