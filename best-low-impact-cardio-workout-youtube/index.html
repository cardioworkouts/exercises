Best Low Impact Cardio Workout Youtube A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of high-impact cardio workouts that leave your joints aching? Do you want to get your heart rate up without putting too much strain on your body? Look no further than the best low impact cardio workout YouTube videos.

Many people struggle with finding a workout routine that fits their needs and abilities. High-impact workouts can be tough on the joints, especially for those with arthritis or other conditions. That's why low-impact cardio workouts are becoming more popular among people of all ages and fitness levels.

If you're looking for an effective and safe way to get your heart pumping, low-impact cardio workouts are the way to go. And fortunately, there are plenty of excellent YouTube channels and videos that can help you get started.

In this comprehensive guide, we'll discuss the best low impact cardio workout YouTube channels, videos, and exercises. By the end of this article, you'll have all the information you need to get moving and improve your cardiovascular health.

Benefits of Low-Impact Cardio Workouts

Low-impact cardio workouts have a wide range of benefits, both for your physical and mental health. Here are just a few reasons why they're worth incorporating into your exercise routine:

  • They're easier on your joints, reducing the risk of injury
  • They can improve your cardiovascular health and endurance
  • They can help you burn calories and lose weight
  • They can reduce stress and improve your mood
  • They're adaptable to different fitness levels and abilities

The Best Low Impact Cardio Workout YouTube Channels

Now that you know the benefits of low-impact cardio workouts, let's take a look at some of the best YouTube channels to get you started.

1. Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a popular YouTube channel that offers a wide range of workout videos, including low-impact cardio workouts. Their videos are well-produced and easy to follow, making them perfect for beginners.

One of their most popular low-impact cardio workouts is this 30-Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners. This workout is perfect for those who are new to low-impact cardio or who want a gentle workout that's still effective. It includes a warm-up and cool-down and focuses on exercises like jumping jacks, knee lifts, and side steps.

2. POPSUGAR Fitness

POPSUGAR Fitness is another popular YouTube channel that offers a variety of workout videos, including low-impact cardio. Their videos are upbeat and fun, making them perfect for those who want to have a good time while exercising.

One of their most popular low-impact cardio workouts is this 30-Minute Low-Impact Cardio Dance Workout. This workout combines dance moves with low-impact cardio exercises to create a fun and effective workout. It's perfect for those who want to get their heart rate up while also having a good time.

The Best Low Impact Cardio Workout Exercises

In addition to the YouTube channels listed above, there are also plenty of standalone low-impact cardio exercises that you can do at home. Here are a few of our favorites:

1. Walking

Walking is one of the most accessible low-impact cardio exercises out there. All you need is a pair of comfortable shoes and a willingness to move your body. Walking can help improve your cardiovascular health, reduce stress, and even improve your mood.

2. Swimming

Swimming is another excellent low-impact cardio exercise that can help improve your cardiovascular health and endurance. It's also a great way to cool off during the hot summer months.

3. Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact exercise that can help improve your cardiovascular health and burn calories. You can do it indoors on a stationary bike or outdoors on a regular bike.

4. Yoga

While not traditionally thought of as a cardio workout, yoga can still get your heart rate up and improve your cardiovascular health. Look for low-impact yoga classes or videos that focus on flowing movements and breath work.

Question and Answer

Q: Can low-impact cardio workouts help with weight loss?

A: Yes, low-impact cardio workouts can help you burn calories and lose weight. However, it's important to combine them with a healthy diet and other forms of exercise for best results.

Q: How often should I do low-impact cardio workouts?

A: It's recommended that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week. This can be spread out over several days and can include a mix of low-impact and high-impact exercises.

Q: Can low-impact cardio workouts be modified for different fitness levels?

A: Yes, most low-impact cardio workouts can be modified to fit different fitness levels and abilities. Look for videos or classes that offer modifications or alternatives for certain exercises.

Q: Are low-impact cardio workouts safe for people with joint pain?

A: Yes, low-impact cardio workouts are generally safe for people with joint pain, as they put less stress on the joints than high-impact workouts. However, it's always a good idea to check with your doctor before starting a new exercise routine.

Conclusion of Best Low Impact Cardio Workout YouTube

Low-impact cardio workouts are an excellent way to improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and reduce stress. With the help of the best low impact cardio workout YouTube videos and exercises, you can get your heart pumping without putting too much strain on your body. Incorporate these workouts into your routine and you'll be on your way to a healthier, happier you.
