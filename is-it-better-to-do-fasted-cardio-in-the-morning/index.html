Is It Better To Do Fasted Cardio In The Morning

Written by Aprilia Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone who loves to work out and wants to make the most of it? If so, you may have heard of fasted cardio in the morning. But is it really better to do fasted cardio in the morning? In this article, we'll explore the topic and give you some insights that may help you decide whether or not it's right for you.

The idea behind fasted cardio is that you perform cardio exercises on an empty stomach, usually first thing in the morning after waking up. The theory is that your body will burn fat instead of carbohydrates for energy, which could lead to better weight loss results.

So, is it better to do fasted cardio in the morning? The answer is, it depends. There isn't a one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as it depends on several factors, such as your fitness goals, overall health, and schedule.

In summary, if you're looking to burn more fat and lose weight, fasted cardio in the morning may be worth a try. However, it's essential to listen to your body and make sure that you're not pushing yourself too hard. Additionally, it's always a good idea to consult with a medical professional before starting any new exercise routine.

Benefits of Fasted Cardio in the Morning

When it comes to fasted cardio in the morning, there are several benefits that you may experience. Personally, I've noticed that when I exercise on an empty stomach, I feel more energized and focused throughout the day. Here are some other potential benefits:

1. Increased Fat Burn

As mentioned earlier, fasted cardio can help your body burn more fat. When you exercise on an empty stomach, your body relies on stored fat for energy instead of carbohydrates, which can lead to better weight loss results.

2. Improved Insulin Sensitivity

Exercising on an empty stomach can also help improve your body's insulin sensitivity. This means that your body can more effectively regulate blood sugar levels, which can help prevent type 2 diabetes and other health issues.

3. Better Mental Clarity

Many people report feeling more focused and alert after exercising on an empty stomach. This is because your body releases endorphins during exercise, which can help improve your mood and mental clarity.

Drawbacks of Fasted Cardio in the Morning

While there are potential benefits to fasted cardio in the morning, there are also some drawbacks that you should be aware of. Here are a few:

1. Increased Risk of Injury

One of the main drawbacks of fasted cardio in the morning is that you may be more prone to injuries. This is because your body is in a fasted state, which means that it may not have enough energy to power through an intense workout.

2. Decreased Energy Levels

Exercising on an empty stomach can also lead to decreased energy levels. This can make it challenging to get through a workout, especially if you're doing high-intensity exercises.

3. Potential Muscle Loss

Another potential drawback of fasted cardio in the morning is that it may lead to muscle loss. When you exercise on an empty stomach, your body may break down muscle tissue for energy, which can lead to a loss of muscle mass over time.

Question and Answer

1. Is it better to do fasted cardio in the morning?

It depends on your fitness goals and overall health. Fasted cardio can lead to increased fat burn and improved insulin sensitivity, but it may also increase your risk of injury and lead to muscle loss.

2. What are the potential benefits of fasted cardio in the morning?

Some potential benefits of fasted cardio in the morning include increased fat burn, improved insulin sensitivity, and better mental clarity.

3. What are the potential drawbacks of fasted cardio in the morning?

Some potential drawbacks of fasted cardio in the morning include increased risk of injury, decreased energy levels, and potential muscle loss.

4. Should I consult a medical professional before starting fasted cardio in the morning?

Yes, it's always a good idea to consult with a medical professional before starting any new exercise routine, especially if you have any pre-existing health conditions.

Conclusion of Is It Better to Do Fasted Cardio in the Morning

In the end, whether or not it's better to do fasted cardio in the morning depends on your individual needs and preferences. While there are potential benefits, there are also some drawbacks that you should be aware of. It's important to listen to your body and make sure that you're not pushing yourself too hard. Consulting with a medical professional can also help you make an informed decision about whether or not fasted cardio in the morning is right for you.
