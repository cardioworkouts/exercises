What is Aerobic Exercise?

Aerobic exercise, also known as cardio, is any form of physical activity that raises your heart rate and increases your breathing. It is an essential component of any fitness routine, as it helps to improve cardiovascular health, boost energy levels, and reduce stress levels.

Why is Choosing the Right Plan Important?

Choosing the right aerobic exercise plan is crucial to achieving your fitness goals. A plan that is too easy will not challenge you, while a plan that is too difficult can lead to injury. It is essential to find a plan that is tailored to your fitness level and goals.
