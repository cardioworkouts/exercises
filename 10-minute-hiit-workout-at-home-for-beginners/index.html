How to Do a 10 Minute HIIT Workout at Home for Beginners

Now that you know the benefits of a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners, let's take a look at how you can get started.

Step 1: Warm-Up

Before you begin your workout, it's important to warm up your body to prevent injury and prepare your muscles for exercise. You can do this by doing some light cardio, such as jogging in place or doing jumping jacks, for 3-5 minutes.

Step 2: HIIT Circuit

The 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners consists of four exercises that you will do for 30 seconds each, followed by a 10 second rest. You will repeat this circuit twice, for a total of four minutes.

Exercise 1: Squats

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and lower your body as if you were sitting back into a chair. Keep your back straight and your knees over your ankles. Rise back up to standing position and repeat for 30 seconds.

Exercise 2: Push-Ups

Get into a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet hip-width apart. Lower your body towards the ground, keeping your back straight and your elbows close to your body. Push back up to plank position and repeat for 30 seconds.

Exercise 3: Mountain Climbers

Get into a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet hip-width apart. Bring your right knee towards your chest, then quickly switch to your left knee. Repeat for 30 seconds.

Exercise 4: Burpees

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Lower your body into a squat position, then place your hands on the floor in front of you. Jump your feet back into a plank position, then jump them back towards your hands. Stand up and jump into the air. Repeat for 30 seconds.

Step 3: Cool-Down

After you complete the HIIT circuit, it's important to cool down your body to prevent injury and help your muscles recover. You can do this by doing some light stretching for 3-5 minutes.

FAQs

1. How often should I do a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners?

You can do a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners up to three times a week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout.

2. Do I need any equipment for a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners?

No, you do not need any equipment for a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners. All you need is a clear space to move around in.

3. Can I modify the exercises if I am not able to do them?

Yes, you can modify the exercises to suit your fitness level. For example, you can do assisted push-ups or hold a wall sit instead of doing squats.

4. How long will it take to see results from a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners?

You may start to see results from a 10 minute HIIT workout at home for beginners within a few weeks, depending on your fitness level and diet. However, it's important to remember that consistency is key when it comes to seeing results.
