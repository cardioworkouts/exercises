Best Free Workout Fitness Apps Get In Shape Without Breaking The Bank

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the time and motivation to hit the gym? Or, are you just looking for a more cost-effective way to stay in shape? Whatever your reasons may be, the world of fitness apps has got you covered. With so many options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the best free workout fitness apps that work for you. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

When it comes to fitness, we all have different goals and preferences. Some of us want to build muscle, while others just want to stay active and healthy. Some prefer yoga and meditation, while others prefer high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or running. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there's an app for everyone.

In this article, we'll explore the best free workout fitness apps that can help you achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank. From personal training to nutrition tracking, we'll cover it all. Let's get started!

1. Nike Training Club

Target: Personal training sessions and customized workout plans

As one of the most popular fitness apps on the market, Nike Training Club offers a variety of free workout plans and personal training sessions that can be customized to your fitness level and goals. With over 190 workouts to choose from, you can find everything from yoga to HIIT to strength training. The app also includes audio and video instructions, so you can follow along with ease.

Personally, I love Nike Training Club because it offers a variety of workouts that keep me engaged and motivated. The app also tracks my progress and offers personalized recommendations, which makes it feel like I have a personal trainer without the cost.

2. MyFitnessPal

Target: Nutrition tracking and calorie counting

When it comes to fitness, nutrition is just as important as exercise. MyFitnessPal is a free app that allows you to track your daily food intake and monitor your calorie intake. The app has a database of over 11 million foods, so you can easily log your meals and snacks. MyFitnessPal also syncs with other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and Nike Training Club, to give you a complete picture of your fitness journey.

Personally, I find MyFitnessPal to be a great tool for keeping track of my meals and making sure I'm staying within my calorie goals. The app also offers personalized recommendations based on my activity level and fitness goals, which helps me make healthier choices.

3. FitOn

Target: On-demand fitness classes and community support

FitOn is a free app that offers on-demand fitness classes taught by celebrity trainers. The app has a variety of workout options, including dance, yoga, and strength training. FitOn also offers a community feature, which allows you to connect with other users and get support and motivation.

Personally, I love FitOn because it offers a variety of workout options and I can do them from the comfort of my own home. The community feature is also a great way to stay motivated and get support from others.

4. Strava

Target: Running and cycling tracking

If you're a runner or cyclist, Strava is the app for you. The app allows you to track your activity, set goals, and connect with other users. Strava also offers challenges and leaderboards to keep you motivated.

Personally, I love Strava because it allows me to track my runs and set goals for myself. The challenges and leaderboards also keep me motivated and engaged.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed above are free to download and use.

Q: Do these apps offer personalized recommendations?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed above offer personalized recommendations based on your fitness level and goals.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed above offer workouts and plans for beginners.

Q: Do these apps sync with other fitness apps?

A: Yes, some of the apps listed above, such as MyFitnessPal and Strava, sync with other fitness apps to give you a complete picture of your fitness journey.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Fitness Apps

Staying in shape doesn't have to be expensive or time-consuming. With the best free workout fitness apps, you can achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank. From personal training to nutrition tracking to on-demand fitness classes, there's an app for everyone. So, download one of these apps today and start your fitness journey!
