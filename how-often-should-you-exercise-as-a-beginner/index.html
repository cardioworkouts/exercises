How Often Should You Exercise As A Beginner

Written by Frank Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner wondering how often you should exercise? You are not alone. Many people find it challenging to determine the right frequency to exercise, especially when starting. Exercise is an essential aspect of a healthy lifestyle, and getting it right can make a significant difference in your fitness journey.

As a beginner, you may experience challenges such as muscle soreness, fatigue, and lack of motivation. These challenges can make it difficult to stick to an exercise routine, and you may be wondering how often you should exercise to achieve your fitness goals.

The answer to how often you should exercise as a beginner is dependent on several factors such as age, fitness level, health status, and exercise goals. Generally, beginners should aim to exercise at least three times a week for 30 minutes each session. As you progress, you can increase the frequency and duration of your workout sessions.

In summary, as a beginner, it is recommended to exercise at least three times a week for 30 minutes per session. It is crucial to listen to your body and adjust your exercise routine as necessary. Remember to start slowly, gradually increase the intensity and frequency, and consult a healthcare professional if you have any underlying health conditions.

Personal Experience

As a beginner, I struggled with determining the right frequency to exercise. However, after consulting with a fitness expert, I learned that consistency is key. I started with three workout sessions per week, and with time, I increased the frequency and duration of my workout routine. Today, I exercise five times a week for 45 minutes per session, and I have seen significant improvements in my fitness goals.

Factors to Consider

Several factors determine how often you should exercise as a beginner. They include:

Age and Fitness Level

Your age and fitness level play a significant role in determining how often you should exercise. If you are older or have a sedentary lifestyle, you may need to start slowly and gradually increase the frequency and intensity of your workout sessions.

Exercise Goals

Your exercise goals also determine how often you should exercise. If you are looking to lose weight, you may need to exercise more frequently than someone looking to maintain their weight.

Personal Experience

When I started my fitness journey, I was overweight and looking to lose weight. I consulted with a fitness expert who recommended exercising at least three times a week for 30 minutes each session. However, as I progressed and saw results, I increased the frequency and duration of my workout sessions to achieve my weight loss goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I exercise every day as a beginner?

A: It is not recommended to exercise every day as a beginner. Start with at least three workout sessions per week and gradually increase the frequency and duration of your workout routine.

Q: How long should a beginner workout session last?

A: A beginner workout session should last at least 30 minutes per session. It is crucial to listen to your body and adjust your workout routine as necessary.

Q: What if I experience muscle soreness as a beginner?

A: It is normal to experience muscle soreness as a beginner. However, if the soreness persists, it is recommended to take a break and consult with a healthcare professional.

Q: Can I exercise if I have an underlying health condition?

A: It is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before starting an exercise routine if you have an underlying health condition.

Conclusion of How Often Should You Exercise as a Beginner

Getting the right frequency of exercise as a beginner can be challenging. However, with the right guidance and consistency, you can achieve your fitness goals. Remember to start slowly, listen to your body, and adjust your exercise routine as necessary. Happy exercising!
