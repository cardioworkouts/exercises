How to Do Joe Wicks 15 Minute HIIT Workout for Beginners on YouTube

Here's a step-by-step guide to doing Joe Wicks 15 Minute HIIT Workout for Beginners on YouTube:

1. Start with a warm-up. Joe Wicks recommends doing 30 seconds of jogging on the spot, followed by 30 seconds of star jumps. Repeat this for 2 minutes.

2. Do each exercise for 30 seconds, then rest for 30 seconds before moving on to the next exercise. Repeat this circuit twice.

3. Finish with a cool-down. Joe Wicks recommends doing 30 seconds of walking on the spot, followed by 30 seconds of stretching. Repeat this for 2 minutes.
