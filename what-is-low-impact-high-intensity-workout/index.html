What Is Low Impact High Intensity Workout

Written by Robby Sep 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a workout that will burn calories, build strength, and improve your cardiovascular health without putting too much stress on your joints? If so, low impact high intensity workout might be just what you need.

Many people struggle with high impact workouts due to joint pain, old injuries, or other physical limitations. However, this does not mean that they cannot engage in high-intensity workouts. That's where low impact high intensity workout comes in.

Low impact high intensity workout is a type of exercise that combines high-intensity movements with low-impact exercises. These workouts are designed to get your heart rate up, burn calories, and build strength without putting too much stress on your joints.

In this article, we will discuss what low impact high intensity workout is, who can benefit from it, and some of the best exercises to include in your workout routine.

What is the Target of Low Impact High Intensity Workout?

Low impact high intensity workout is suitable for anyone who wants to get a good workout without putting too much stress on their joints. This type of exercise is particularly beneficial for people with joint pain, arthritis, or other physical limitations that prevent them from engaging in high-impact exercises.

It is also suitable for people who are new to exercise and want to start with a low-impact workout that will still challenge them and help them build strength and endurance.

Low impact high intensity workout is a great way to improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and build strength without putting too much strain on your joints. It is also a fun and challenging way to mix up your workout routine and keep things interesting.

My Personal Experience with Low Impact High Intensity Workout

As a person who has struggled with joint pain for years, I was always hesitant to engage in high-impact exercises like running or jumping jacks. However, once I discovered low impact high intensity workout, I found a type of exercise that was challenging, fun, and safe for my joints.

My favorite low impact high intensity workouts include cycling, rowing, and using the elliptical machine. I also enjoy doing bodyweight exercises like squats, lunges, and push-ups, as well as using resistance bands and dumbbells to add an extra challenge to my workout.

Benefits of Low Impact High Intensity Workout

One of the biggest benefits of low impact high intensity workout is that it allows you to get a great workout without putting too much stress on your joints. This makes it a great option for people with joint pain, arthritis, or other physical limitations.

Low impact high intensity workout also helps to improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and build strength. By incorporating high-intensity movements into your workout routine, you can challenge your body and see results faster than with low-intensity exercise alone.

Another benefit of low impact high intensity workout is that it is a fun and challenging way to mix up your workout routine. By trying new exercises and challenging yourself with high-intensity movements, you can keep your workouts interesting and prevent boredom.

Best Exercises for Low Impact High Intensity Workout

There are many different exercises that you can include in your low impact high intensity workout routine. Some of the best exercises include:

  • Cycling
  • Rowing
  • Elliptical machine
  • Bodyweight exercises (squats, lunges, push-ups)
  • Resistance band exercises
  • Dumbbell exercises

By combining these exercises with high-intensity movements like sprints, intervals, and circuit training, you can create a low impact high intensity workout that will challenge your body and get you results.

How to Get Started with Low Impact High Intensity Workout

If you're new to low impact high intensity workout, it's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. Begin with low-intensity exercises like cycling or walking, and gradually add in high-intensity movements like sprints or intervals.

It's also important to listen to your body and take breaks when you need them. If you experience pain or discomfort during your workout, stop and rest. Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts as your fitness level improves.

Conclusion of What is Low Impact High Intensity Workout

Low impact high intensity workout is a great way to get a challenging and effective workout without putting too much stress on your joints. By combining high-intensity movements with low-impact exercises, you can improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and build strength.

If you're new to low impact high intensity workout, start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. Incorporate exercises like cycling, rowing, and bodyweight exercises, and challenge yourself with high-intensity movements like sprints and intervals.
