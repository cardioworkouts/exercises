Best Cardio Apps For Beginners

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to improve your cardiovascular health and fitness? Do you struggle with finding the time or motivation to go to the gym? If so, then you may benefit from using a cardio app. Cardio apps are designed to help you achieve your fitness goals by providing you with personalized workouts and tracking your progress. In this article, we will explore the best cardio apps for beginners and how they can help you reach your fitness goals.

Starting a new fitness routine can be challenging, especially if you are new to cardio workouts. It can be overwhelming to know where to start, what exercises to do, and how long to do them for. Additionally, finding the time to go to the gym or attend a fitness class can be difficult with a busy schedule. These are common pain points that many beginners face when starting a cardio routine. Fortunately, cardio apps can help alleviate some of these challenges by providing you with a workout plan that fits your schedule and fitness level.

What are the Best Cardio Apps for Beginners?

When it comes to cardio apps, there are many options available on the market. However, not all cardio apps are created equal. Some are better suited for beginners, while others are designed for more advanced users. To help you get started, we have compiled a list of the best cardio apps for beginners:

1. Nike Training Club

One of the most popular cardio apps for beginners is Nike Training Club. This app offers a variety of cardio workouts that are tailored to your fitness level and goals. The app features both audio and video guidance, making it easy to follow along with the workouts. Additionally, Nike Training Club offers a range of workout lengths, from short 5-minute sessions to full-body workouts that last up to an hour.

2. Aaptiv

Aaptiv is another great cardio app for beginners. This app offers audio-based workouts that are led by certified personal trainers. The trainers guide you through each workout, providing motivation and encouragement along the way. Aaptiv offers a variety of cardio workouts, including running, cycling, and elliptical training.

3. FitOn

FitOn is a free cardio app that offers a variety of cardio workouts for beginners. The app features workouts that range in length from 5 to 30 minutes, making it easy to fit a workout into your busy schedule. Additionally, FitOn offers personalized workout plans that are tailored to your fitness level and goals.

4. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular fitness app that offers both cardio and strength training workouts. The app features a library of workouts that are tailored to your fitness level and goals. Additionally, MyFitnessPal offers a calorie tracker and nutrition guide, making it easy to track your progress and stay on top of your health and fitness goals.

Overall, the best cardio apps for beginners are the ones that offer personalized workouts, are easy to follow, and fit your schedule and fitness level. By using a cardio app, you can take your fitness routine to the next level and achieve your health and fitness goals.

Nike Training Club: My Personal Experience

I have been using Nike Training Club for the past few months, and I have found it to be an excellent app for beginners. The app is easy to navigate, and the workouts are challenging but manageable. I appreciate that the workouts are tailored to my fitness level, and I can choose from a variety of lengths and styles. Additionally, the app tracks my progress and provides me with feedback on my workouts, which helps me stay motivated and on track.

Aaptiv: My Personal Experience

While I have not used Aaptiv personally, I have heard great things about this app from friends and colleagues. Many people appreciate the audio-based format, which makes it easy to follow along with the workouts. Additionally, the personal trainers provide motivation and encouragement, which can be especially helpful for beginners who are just starting their fitness journey.

How Do Cardio Apps Work?

Cardio apps work by providing you with personalized workouts that are tailored to your fitness level and goals. These workouts can range in length, style, and intensity, depending on your preferences. Additionally, cardio apps may track your progress, provide feedback on your workouts, and offer nutritional guidance to help you achieve your health and fitness goals.

How to Choose the Right Cardio App for You?

Choosing the right cardio app for you depends on your preferences and fitness goals. Some apps may be better suited for beginners, while others may be geared towards more advanced users. Additionally, it is important to consider factors such as workout length, intensity, and style when choosing a cardio app. Reading reviews and trying out different apps can also help you find the right one for you.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Apps for Beginners

Overall, cardio apps can be an excellent tool for beginners who are looking to improve their cardiovascular health and fitness. By using a cardio app, you can follow personalized workouts that fit your schedule and fitness level, track your progress, and stay motivated on your fitness journey. Whether you choose Nike Training Club, Aaptiv, FitOn, or MyFitnessPal, there is a cardio app out there that can help you achieve your health and fitness goals.
