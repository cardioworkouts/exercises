The Science Behind Cardio Workouts

Cardiovascular exercise, also known as cardio, is any type of exercise that gets your heart rate up and increases your breathing rate. When you do cardio, your body uses oxygen to produce energy, which helps to increase your endurance and improve your overall health.

Research has shown that regular cardio exercise can help to improve cardiovascular health, reduce the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease and diabetes, and even improve mental health by reducing symptoms of anxiety and depression.
