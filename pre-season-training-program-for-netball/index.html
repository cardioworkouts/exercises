Pre Season Training Program For Netball A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a netball player looking to improve your game and prepare for the upcoming season? Pre season training program for netball is essential for any player who wants to perform their best on the court. It can be challenging to know where to start with your training, but with the right program, you can get in shape, develop your skills, and reduce the risk of injury.

Netball is an intense sport that requires a combination of speed, agility, strength, and endurance. Without proper preparation, players can easily become fatigued and increase their risk of injury. A pre season training program for netball aims to improve all of these areas, allowing players to perform at their best during the season.

The target of pre season training program for netball is to prepare players physically and mentally for the demands of the game. This includes building strength and endurance, improving agility and reaction time, and developing technical skills such as shooting, passing, and footwork. The program should be tailored to each player's needs, taking into account their fitness level, position, and any individual weaknesses or areas for improvement.

In summary, pre season training program for netball is essential for any player looking to improve their performance and reduce the risk of injury. It targets all areas of the game and should be tailored to each player's needs and goals.

The Importance of Cardiovascular Training

Cardiovascular training is a critical component of any pre season training program for netball. It helps players develop the endurance needed to perform at a high level throughout the game. A personal experience that I had with cardiovascular training was when I began training for netball, I found that I quickly became fatigued during games and struggled to keep up with the pace of play. After incorporating regular cardiovascular training into my program, I noticed a significant improvement in my endurance and was able to play for longer periods without becoming fatigued.

Cardiovascular training can include activities such as running, cycling, or swimming. It is essential to vary the intensity and duration of the workouts to improve overall fitness and prevent boredom. High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is an effective way to improve cardiovascular fitness while also developing speed and agility.

The Role of Strength Training

Strength training is another crucial component of pre season training program for netball. It helps players develop the strength and power needed to perform explosive movements such as jumping and sprinting. A personal experience that I had with strength training was when I began incorporating weightlifting into my program, I noticed a significant improvement in my ability to jump higher and sprint faster. It also helped me develop better balance and stability on the court.

Strength training exercises should target all major muscle groups, including the legs, back, chest, and arms. Compound exercises such as squats, deadlifts, and bench presses are effective for developing overall strength and power. It is also essential to incorporate exercises that target specific areas such as the glutes, core, and shoulders, which are vital for netball players.

The Importance of Flexibility and Mobility

Flexibility and mobility are often overlooked in pre season training program for netball, but they are essential for preventing injury and improving performance. Netball requires a wide range of movements, including twisting, jumping, and landing, which can put a strain on the joints and muscles. A personal experience that I had with flexibility and mobility training was when I began incorporating stretching and foam rolling into my program, I noticed a significant improvement in my range of motion and felt less stiff and sore after games.

Flexibility and mobility exercises should focus on stretching the major muscle groups used in netball, including the legs, hips, back, and shoulders. Foam rolling and massage can also help release tension and improve mobility. It is essential to incorporate these exercises into your program regularly to maintain flexibility and prevent injury.

The Importance of Rest and Recovery

Rest and recovery are often overlooked but are just as important as the training itself. Netball is a physically demanding sport that requires a lot of energy and can put a strain on the body. Adequate rest and recovery are necessary to allow the body to repair and rebuild, reducing the risk of injury and improving performance. A personal experience that I had with rest and recovery was when I began incorporating rest days into my program, I noticed a significant improvement in my energy levels and felt less fatigued during games.

Rest and recovery can include activities such as rest days, massage, and stretching. It is also essential to get enough sleep, eat a balanced diet, and stay hydrated to support the body's recovery process.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I train during pre season?

A: It is recommended to train at least three to four times per week during pre season, with a combination of cardiovascular, strength, and skill-based training.

Q: What should I eat before and after training?

A: Before training, it is essential to eat a meal that is high in carbohydrates and protein to fuel the body. After training, it is recommended to eat a meal or snack that is high in protein to aid in muscle recovery.

Q: How long should I rest between sets during strength training?

A: Rest periods between sets should be around 30-60 seconds for exercises targeting muscular endurance and 2-3 minutes for exercises targeting maximal strength.

Q: How can I prevent injury during pre season training?

A: To prevent injury, it is essential to warm up properly before training, gradually increase the intensity and duration of the workouts, and listen to your body. It is also essential to incorporate flexibility and mobility exercises into your program and get enough rest and recovery.

Conclusion of Pre Season Training Program for Netball

Pre season training program for netball is essential for any player looking to improve their performance and reduce the risk of injury. It targets all aspects of the game, including cardiovascular fitness, strength, flexibility, and mobility. By incorporating these elements into your program and prioritizing rest and recovery, you can prepare your body and mind for the demands of the upcoming season.
