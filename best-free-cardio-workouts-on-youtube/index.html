Best Free Cardio Workouts On Youtube A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and paying for expensive classes just to get a good cardio workout? Have you considered working out at home but don't know where to start? Look no further than Youtube! With its vast selection of free workout videos, Youtube offers a convenient and cost-effective way to get your heart pumping. In this guide, we'll explore the best free cardio workouts on Youtube and how to make the most of them.

When it comes to finding the best free cardio workouts on Youtube, it can be overwhelming to sift through the endless options. Additionally, without the guidance of a personal trainer, it can be difficult to know which workouts are effective and safe. That's why we've compiled a list of the top cardio workouts on Youtube and provided tips on how to get the most out of them.

First and foremost, the goal of cardio workouts is to get your heart rate up and keep it there for an extended period of time. This can be achieved through a variety of exercises such as running, jumping jacks, high knees, and more. The best free cardio workouts on Youtube incorporate these exercises into fun and engaging routines that keep you motivated and challenged.

To summarize, the best free cardio workouts on Youtube offer a convenient and cost-effective way to get your heart pumping. By incorporating a variety of exercises into fun and engaging routines, these workouts keep you motivated and challenged. In the following sections, we'll dive deeper into specific types of cardio workouts and provide recommendations for the best Youtube channels to follow.

Low Impact Cardio

Low impact cardio workouts are perfect for those who want to get their heart rate up without putting too much strain on their joints. These workouts typically involve movements that keep at least one foot on the ground at all times, such as marching in place or stepping side to side. One of the best Youtube channels for low impact cardio is FitnessBlender. Their Low Impact Cardio Workout for Beginners is a great place to start for those new to this type of workout. The video provides clear instructions and modifications for different fitness levels.

High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

HIIT workouts are known for their effectiveness in burning calories and improving cardiovascular health. These workouts involve short bursts of high intensity exercise followed by periods of rest. One of the best Youtube channels for HIIT workouts is PopSugar Fitness. Their HIIT Cardio and Abs Workout is a challenging but rewarding workout that will leave you feeling accomplished. The video provides modifications for different fitness levels, so you can tailor the workout to your own abilities.

Tabata

Tabata workouts are a type of HIIT workout that involves 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for a total of four minutes. These workouts are great for those short on time but still want a challenging workout. One of the best Youtube channels for Tabata workouts is BodyFit by Amy. Her 4 Minute Tabata Fat Burning Workout is a quick but effective way to get your heart rate up and burn calories.

Kickboxing

Kickboxing workouts combine cardio and strength training for a total body workout. These workouts typically involve punches, kicks, and other martial arts-inspired movements. One of the best Youtube channels for kickboxing workouts is Millionaire Hoy. His Cardio Kickboxing Workout is a high energy workout that will leave you feeling empowered and strong. The video provides modifications for different fitness levels, so you can adjust the intensity to your liking.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these workouts suitable for beginners?
A: Yes! Each of the recommended Youtube channels provides modifications for different fitness levels, so you can start at your own pace and work your way up. Q: Do I need any equipment for these workouts?
A: Most of the workouts listed above require little to no equipment. However, some workouts may use dumbbells or resistance bands. Check the video description for a list of recommended equipment. Q: Can I do these workouts at home?
A: Absolutely! That's the beauty of Youtube workouts - you can do them anywhere, anytime. Q: How often should I do these workouts?
A: It's recommended to do cardio workouts at least three times a week for optimal cardiovascular health. However, listen to your body and adjust your frequency as needed.

Conclusion of Best Free Cardio Workouts on Youtube

In conclusion, Youtube is a treasure trove of free cardio workouts that offer convenience and cost-effectiveness. By incorporating a variety of exercises into fun and engaging routines, these workouts keep you motivated and challenged. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned fitness enthusiast, there's a cardio workout on Youtube for everyone. So what are you waiting for? Grab your water bottle and get moving!
