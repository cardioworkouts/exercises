One of the essential aspects of kardiologi is diagnostic techniques. These techniques help identify heart disease and determine the best course of treatment. There are several diagnostic techniques used in kardiologi, including electrocardiograms, echocardiograms, and stress tests.

Common Heart Diseases

Heart disease is an umbrella term that encompasses a wide range of conditions. Some of the most common heart diseases include coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmia.

Coronary artery disease occurs when plaque builds up in the arteries that supply blood to the heart. This can lead to chest pain, shortness of breath, and even heart attacks. Heart failure occurs when the heart is unable to pump enough blood to meet the body's needs. Arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat that can lead to dizziness, fatigue, and even heart failure.

Q: What are the warning signs of a heart attack?
