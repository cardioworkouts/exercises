How Often Should You Do Fitness Blender Strength Training – No Equipment Required?

How often you should do Fitness Blenders’ no-equipment strength training workouts depends on your fitness level, goals, and schedule. Here are some general guidelines:

1. Beginners

If you’re a beginner, it’s recommended that you start with 2-3 strength training workouts per week, with at least one day of rest in between. You can gradually increase the frequency and intensity of the workouts as you get stronger and more comfortable with the exercises.

2. Intermediate

If you’re an intermediate level, you can do 3-4 strength training workouts per week, with at least one day of rest in between. You can also combine strength training with cardio or flexibility workouts to create a well-rounded fitness routine.

3. Advanced

If you’re an advanced level, you can do 4-5 strength training workouts per week, with at least one day of rest in between. You can also add more weight or repetitions to the exercises to increase the challenge and stimulate muscle growth.
