Should You Lift Weights And Do Cardio On The Same Day

Written by Eveline Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you one of those people who love to hit the gym for both lifting weights and cardio exercises? But, you are not sure whether doing both on the same day is beneficial or not. Well, you are not alone. Many fitness enthusiasts have the same question in mind, and this article will answer your doubts and queries regarding whether you should lift weights and do cardio on the same day or not.

The idea of lifting weights and doing cardio on the same day can be daunting for many. It is because both of these exercises require a high level of energy and can be taxing on your body. But, if you want to achieve an overall healthy and fit body, combining these two exercises can be beneficial for you.

When it comes to should you lift weights and do cardio on the same day, the answer is not a straightforward one. It depends on your fitness goals and how much time you have to devote to your workout routine.

Combining both exercises on the same day can be beneficial for those who want to achieve overall fitness and have limited time to spend in the gym. It also helps in burning more calories, which is great if you are trying to lose weight.

My Personal Experience with Lifting Weights and Doing Cardio on the Same Day

As a fitness enthusiast, I have tried both lifting weights and doing cardio on separate days and on the same day. I found that combining both exercises on the same day not only saves time but also helps in improving my overall fitness levels.

However, I would like to mention that if you are planning to do both exercises on the same day, it is important to plan your workout routine accordingly. For instance, you can start with lifting weights and then move on to cardio exercises or vice versa.

The Benefits of Lifting Weights and Doing Cardio on the Same Day

Combining lifting weights and doing cardio on the same day can have several benefits, such as:

  • Burning more calories: Doing both exercises on the same day can help in burning more calories than doing them separately.
  • Improving overall fitness: Combining both exercises can help in improving your overall fitness, including strength, endurance, and cardiovascular health.
  • Saving time: Doing both exercises on the same day can save you time, especially if you have a busy schedule.

How to Plan Your Workout Routine

If you are planning to do both exercises on the same day, it is important to plan your workout routine accordingly. Here are some tips:

  • Start with lifting weights: If you are planning to do both exercises on the same day, start with lifting weights as it requires more energy and focus.
  • Take breaks in between: It is important to take breaks in between your lifting and cardio exercises to avoid overexertion and fatigue.
  • Alternate between exercises: Alternating between lifting and cardio exercises can help in reducing the stress on your muscles and improve your overall fitness.

How to Avoid Overexertion and Injury

While combining lifting weights and doing cardio on the same day can be beneficial, it is important to avoid overexertion and injury. Here are some tips:

  • Warm-up: It is important to warm up your muscles before starting your workout routine to avoid injury.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking enough water during your workout can help in avoiding dehydration and fatigue.
  • Listen to your body: If you feel tired or fatigued, take a break or reduce the intensity of your workout.

Question and Answer

Q: Can lifting weights and doing cardio on the same day help in weight loss?

A: Yes, combining both exercises on the same day can help in burning more calories, which can aid in weight loss.

Q: Is it safe to do both exercises on the same day?

A: Yes, it is safe to combine both exercises on the same day if you plan your workout routine accordingly and listen to your body.

Q: How long should I wait between lifting weights and doing cardio?

A: It is recommended to wait for at least 30 minutes between lifting weights and doing cardio exercises.

Q: Should I do cardio before or after lifting weights?

A: It depends on your fitness goals. If you want to focus on building strength, you can start with lifting weights. If you want to improve your cardiovascular health, you can start with cardio exercises.

Conclusion of Should You Lift Weights and Do Cardio on the Same Day

Combining lifting weights and doing cardio on the same day can have several benefits, including burning more calories, improving overall fitness, and saving time. However, it is important to plan your workout routine accordingly and listen to your body to avoid overexertion and injury.
