The Best Workout On The Treadmill To Lose Weight

Written by Petter Aug 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to lose weight but find yourself struggling to stick to a regular workout routine? The treadmill can be a great choice for those looking to shed those extra pounds, but it's important to know what the best workout on the treadmill is to achieve your weight loss goals.

Losing weight can be a challenging task, and finding the right workout to do so can be even more daunting. Many people turn to the treadmill for their cardio workout, but not all treadmill workouts are created equal when it comes to weight loss.

The best workout on the treadmill to lose weight is one that combines high-intensity interval training (HIIT) with incline training. This type of workout will help you burn more calories in less time and increase your metabolism, leading to more weight loss over time.

In this article, we'll go over the best workout on the treadmill to lose weight, including how to perform it, its benefits, and tips to make the most out of your workout.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

High-intensity interval training, or HIIT for short, is a type of workout that alternates between short periods of intense exercise and periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. Studies have shown that HIIT can be more effective at burning fat and losing weight than steady-state cardio workouts.

To perform a HIIT workout on the treadmill, start with a five-minute warm-up at a moderate pace. Then, increase the speed and run or walk at a high-intensity level for 30 seconds to one minute. Follow this with a period of rest or low-intensity exercise for one to two minutes. Repeat this cycle for 20-30 minutes.

HIIT workouts can be challenging, so it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity over time. It's also essential to listen to your body and rest when needed.

Incline Training

Incline training is another effective way to burn calories on the treadmill. By increasing the incline, you can engage more muscles in your legs and burn more calories than running or walking on a flat surface. Incline training can also help you improve your endurance and build strength in your lower body.

To perform an incline workout on the treadmill, start with a five-minute warm-up at a moderate pace. Then, increase the incline to 3-5% and walk or run for 30 seconds to one minute. Follow this with a period of rest or low-intensity exercise for one to two minutes. Repeat this cycle for 20-30 minutes.

As with HIIT workouts, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the incline over time. It's also essential to listen to your body and rest when needed.

Tips for Best Results

Here are some tips to make the most out of your treadmill workout:

  • Stay hydrated by drinking water before, during, and after your workout.
  • Wear comfortable and supportive shoes to prevent injury.
  • Vary your workouts by changing the speed, incline, or duration of your treadmill workout.
  • Track your progress by keeping a record of your workouts.
  • Don't forget to cool down and stretch after your workout.

Conclusion

The best workout on the treadmill to lose weight is one that combines high-intensity interval training with incline training. This type of workout will help you burn more calories in less time and increase your metabolism, leading to more weight loss over time. Remember to start slow, listen to your body, and stay consistent with your workouts to see the best results.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do this workout on the treadmill?

A: It is recommended to do this workout 2-3 times a week with a rest day in between each workout.

Q: Can I do this workout if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, but it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity over time. It's also essential to listen to your body and rest when needed.

Q: How long should I do this workout on the treadmill?

A: Aim for 20-30 minutes of high-intensity interval training and incline training on the treadmill.

Q: Can I do this workout on the elliptical or stationary bike?

A: Yes, you can modify this workout for the elliptical or stationary bike by following the same principles of high-intensity interval training and incline training.

Conclusion of Best Workout on the Treadmill to Lose Weight

The best workout on the treadmill to lose weight is a combination of high-intensity interval training and incline training. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can make the most out of your treadmill workout and achieve your weight loss goals.
