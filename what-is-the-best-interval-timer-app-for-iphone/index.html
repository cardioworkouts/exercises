What Is The Best Interval Timer App For Iphone

Written by Frank Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of keeping track of your workout time manually? Do you find it hard to maintain consistency in your exercise routine? Whether you're an athlete, a fitness enthusiast, or someone who wants to get in shape, an interval timer app can be your ultimate solution.

If you're looking for an interval timer app for your iPhone, you might be facing some difficulties in choosing the best one. With so many options available on the App Store, it's tough to determine which one is the best.

The best interval timer app for iPhone is the one that meets your specific needs and preferences. However, some of the most popular interval timer apps for iPhone are Seconds Pro, Interval Timer - HIIT Workouts, Tabata Timer for HIIT, and Aaptiv.

If you're looking for an app that can help you with high-intensity interval training (HIIT), Tabata Timer for HIIT and Interval Timer - HIIT Workouts are good options. On the other hand, Seconds Pro is an all-in-one timer app that includes various features like music integration, voice feedback, and custom intervals. Aaptiv is a great app for those who want guided workouts with a coach's voice.

Personal Experience with Seconds Pro

I have been using Seconds Pro for a few months now, and it has significantly improved my workout sessions. With this app, I can easily customize my intervals, add music, and get voice feedback on my progress. I can also create multiple timers, which is helpful when I'm doing circuit training. Overall, Seconds Pro has made my workouts more efficient and enjoyable.

Personal Experience with Aaptiv

Aaptiv is another great app that I have tried. What I love about this app is that it has a vast library of workouts, and the coaches' voices are motivating. The app also tracks my progress, and I can see how many calories I burned during each session. However, Aaptiv is a subscription-based app, which might not be ideal for everyone.

Benefits of Using an Interval Timer App

Using an interval timer app can have several benefits, including:

  • Improved workout efficiency
  • Better time management
  • Increased motivation
  • Customizable intervals

How to Use an Interval Timer App

Using an interval timer app is quite simple. First, download the app of your choice from the App Store. Then, open the app and customize your intervals according to your workout routine. Finally, hit the start button and let the app do the rest. Some apps also offer voice feedback, music integration, and other features that can enhance your workout experience.

Personal Experience with Tabata Timer for HIIT

Tabata Timer for HIIT is another app that I have tried. This app is specifically designed for HIIT workouts and offers customizable intervals, voice feedback, and music integration. I found this app helpful when I was doing Tabata workouts, and it made my sessions more structured and organized.

Question and Answer

Q. Is Seconds Pro a free app?

A. No, Seconds Pro is a paid app, but it offers a free trial for seven days.

Q. Can I use Aaptiv without an internet connection?

A. No, Aaptiv requires an internet connection to access its library of workouts.

Q. Are interval timer apps only for athletes?

A. No, interval timer apps can be used by anyone who wants to maintain consistency in their workout routine.

Q. Can I use my own music with interval timer apps?

A. Yes, most interval timer apps offer music integration, which allows you to use your own music during your workouts.

Conclusion of What is the Best Interval Timer App for iPhone

Choosing the best interval timer app for iPhone can be overwhelming, but it ultimately depends on your specific needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for an app for HIIT workouts, guided workouts, or customizable intervals, there's an app that suits your requirements. With the right interval timer app, you can make your workouts more efficient, enjoyable, and consistent.
