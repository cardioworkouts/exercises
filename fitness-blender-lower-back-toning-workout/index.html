The Pain Points of Lower Back Pain

Lower back pain is a common problem that can affect people of all ages and fitness levels. Whether you spend your days sitting at a desk or lifting heavy objects, your lower back muscles can become weak and strained, leading to discomfort and pain. Poor posture, lack of exercise, and injuries can all contribute to lower back pain, making it difficult to perform everyday activities without discomfort.

How to Perform the Fitness Blender Lower Back Toning Workout

To perform the Fitness Blender Lower Back Toning Workout, begin by warming up with 5-10 minutes of light cardio, such as jogging in place or jumping jacks. Then, follow these steps:

Step 1: Superman

Lie face down on a mat with your arms and legs extended. Lift your arms, legs, and chest off the ground, holding for 2-3 seconds before lowering back down to the starting position. Repeat for 10-15 reps.

Step 2: Lower Back Extension

Lie face down on a mat with your hands behind your head. Lift your chest and shoulders off the ground, holding for 2-3 seconds before lowering back down to the starting position. Repeat for 10-15 reps.

Step 3: Reverse Hyperextension

Lie face down on a bench with your legs hanging off the end. Lift your legs up towards the ceiling, holding for 2-3 seconds before lowering back down to the starting position. Repeat for 10-15 reps.

Step 4: Bird Dog

Get down on all fours with your hands and knees shoulder-width apart. Extend your right arm and left leg out straight, holding for 2-3 seconds before returning to the starting position. Repeat on the opposite side, performing 10-15 reps on each side.
