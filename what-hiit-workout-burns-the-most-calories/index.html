What Hiit Workout Burns The Most Calories A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a workout that can help you burn the most calories in the least amount of time? If so, you might want to consider high-intensity interval training (HIIT). HIIT is a popular type of workout that involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity activity. In this blog post, we'll explore what HIIT workout burns the most calories and related keywords to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle to find the time to exercise, but HIIT workouts can be completed in as little as 20-30 minutes. They're also great for burning fat and building muscle, making them a popular choice among fitness enthusiasts. However, with so many different types of HIIT workouts available, it can be challenging to know which one will help you burn the most calories.

The answer to what HIIT workout burns the most calories is not straightforward, as it depends on various factors such as the intensity of the workout, the duration of the workout, and the type of exercises involved. However, some types of HIIT workouts are known to be more effective than others when it comes to burning calories.

In this post, we'll discuss some of the most effective HIIT workouts for burning calories and how to perform them. We'll also provide tips on how to maximize your calorie burn during a HIIT workout.

The Tabata Protocol

The Tabata protocol is one of the most popular HIIT workouts for burning calories. Developed by Japanese researcher Izumi Tabata, this workout involves performing eight rounds of 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest. The exercises can vary, but some popular options include burpees, jumping jacks, and mountain climbers.

During a Tabata workout, you'll be pushing yourself to your limits for those 20 seconds of intense exercise, which can help you burn a significant amount of calories in a short amount of time. According to one study, participants who performed Tabata-style workouts burned an average of 15 calories per minute.

The Bodyweight Circuit

Bodyweight circuits are another popular type of HIIT workout for burning calories. These workouts involve performing a series of bodyweight exercises back-to-back with little to no rest in between. Some popular exercises to include in a bodyweight circuit include push-ups, squats, lunges, and burpees.

Bodyweight circuits are great for burning calories because they engage multiple muscle groups at once, which can increase your heart rate and calorie burn. The intensity of the workout can also be easily adjusted by changing the number of repetitions or the amount of rest between exercises.

The EMOM Workout

EMOM stands for every minute on the minute and is another effective HIIT workout for burning calories. During an EMOM workout, you'll perform a specific exercise for a set number of repetitions at the beginning of every minute. Once you've completed the reps, you can rest for the remainder of the minute before starting the next round.

The key to maximizing your calorie burn during an EMOM workout is to choose an exercise that engages multiple muscle groups, such as the squat or push-up. By engaging more muscle groups, you'll increase your heart rate and calorie burn, making this type of workout highly effective for burning calories.

The AMRAP Workout

AMRAP stands for as many rounds as possible and is another popular type of HIIT workout for burning calories. During an AMRAP workout, you'll perform a specific set of exercises for a set amount of time, typically between 10-20 minutes. The goal is to complete as many rounds of the exercises as possible within the time frame.

AMRAP workouts are highly effective for burning calories because they're typically high-intensity and engage multiple muscle groups. By working multiple muscle groups simultaneously, you'll increase your heart rate and calorie burn, making this type of workout highly effective for burning calories.

Conclusion of What HIIT Workout Burns the Most Calories

When it comes to what HIIT workout burns the most calories, there's no one-size-fits-all answer. The most effective workout for you will depend on your fitness level, goals, and personal preferences. However, by incorporating high-intensity interval training into your fitness routine, you can burn a significant amount of calories in a short amount of time.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do a HIIT workout to see results?

A: To see results from a HIIT workout, it's recommended to do at least three sessions per week. However, you should always listen to your body and adjust your workout schedule as needed.

Q: Are HIIT workouts suitable for beginners?

A: HIIT workouts can be challenging, but they're suitable for beginners as long as they're performed at a low to moderate intensity. It's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts as your fitness level improves.

Q: How many calories can I expect to burn during a HIIT workout?

A: The number of calories you burn during a HIIT workout will depend on various factors such as your age, weight, and fitness level. However, most people can expect to burn between 300-500 calories per 30-minute HIIT workout.

Q: How can I maximize my calorie burn during a HIIT workout?

A: To maximize your calorie burn during a HIIT workout, you should choose exercises that engage multiple muscle groups, perform the exercises at a high intensity, and limit your rest periods between exercises.
