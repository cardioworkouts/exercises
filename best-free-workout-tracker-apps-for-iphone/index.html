Best Free Workout Tracker Apps For Iphone

Written by Aprilia Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a way to track your workouts and fitness goals conveniently, then you must have considered using a workout tracker app. With so many options out there, it can be challenging to choose the right one. In this post, we will discuss the best free workout tracker apps for iPhone and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Tracking your workouts is essential to achieving your fitness goals, but it can be tedious and time-consuming. You may have to take notes, use a spreadsheet, or even a pen and paper. However, with workout tracker apps, you don't have to go through all that stress. These apps help you track your workouts, monitor your progress, and stay motivated throughout your fitness journey.

The target of this post is to provide you with the best free workout tracker apps for iPhone. These apps are perfect for anyone who wants to track their workouts, monitor their progress, and achieve their fitness goals.

In summary, in this post, we will be discussing the best free workout tracker apps for iPhone and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals. We will provide a personal experience with each app and go into more detail about their features.

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular workout tracker app that helps you track your calorie intake, set fitness goals, and monitor your progress. The app has a vast database of foods, making it easy to track your nutrition. It also allows you to connect with friends and join community groups for support and motivation.

Personally, I have been using MyFitnessPal for a while now, and it has been a game-changer. It has helped me track my calorie intake, monitor my progress, and stay motivated throughout my fitness journey. The app's user interface is user-friendly, making it easy to navigate and use.

2. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is another excellent workout tracker app that provides personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals. The app offers a wide range of workouts, from bodyweight to full gym sessions, all led by Nike Master Trainers. It also allows you to connect with friends and share your progress.

I have used Nike Training Club for a while now, and it has been a great experience. The app's personalized workout plans have helped me achieve my fitness goals, and the workouts are challenging and fun.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a workout tracker app that creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals, experience level, and available equipment. The app uses machine learning to adjust your workout plan based on your progress, making it perfect for anyone who wants a tailored workout plan.

Fitbod has been an excellent workout app for me because it creates personalized workout plans that challenge me while still being achievable. The app's user interface is also user-friendly, making it easy to use and navigate.

4. JEFIT

JEFIT is a workout tracker app that offers a vast library of workouts and allows you to create your workout plans. The app includes features such as progress tracking, workout logging, and a community of users for support and motivation.

I have used JEFIT for a while now, and it has been an excellent workout tracker app. The app's vast library of workouts has helped me switch up my routine and keep my workouts fun and challenging. It also allows me to track my progress and monitor my fitness goals.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Tracker Apps for iPhone

In conclusion, there are many workout tracker apps out there, but these four are the best free workout tracker apps for iPhone. MyFitnessPal, Nike Training Club, Fitbod, and JEFIT all offer personalized workout plans, progress tracking, and community support. They are perfect for anyone who wants to track their workouts, monitor their progress, and achieve their fitness goals.
