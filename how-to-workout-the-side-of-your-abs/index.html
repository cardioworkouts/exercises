How To Workout The Side Of Your Abs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you feel like you're not getting the most out of your ab workouts? Are you struggling to target the sides of your abs? If so, you're not alone. Many people find it difficult to work out the side of their abs, but with the right techniques and exercises, it's definitely possible. In this post, we'll explore how to workout the side of your abs and help you achieve your fitness goals.

When it comes to working out the side of your abs, there are a few pain points that people commonly encounter. For example, some people may find it difficult to feel the burn in their obliques, while others may struggle to maintain proper form during exercises like side planks. Additionally, some people may not know which exercises are most effective for targeting the sides of their abs.

The good news is that there are a variety of exercises you can do to work out the side of your abs. One of the most effective is the side plank, which involves balancing on one forearm and keeping your body in a straight line. Other exercises that can help target the obliques include Russian twists, bicycle crunches, and side crunches. By incorporating these exercises into your workout routine, you can start to see results in no time.

In summary, working out the side of your abs is definitely achievable with the right techniques and exercises. By incorporating exercises like side planks, Russian twists, and side crunches into your workout routine, you can start to see results and achieve your fitness goals.

How to Do a Side Plank:

When I first started working out, I struggled to feel the burn in my obliques. However, once I started doing side planks regularly, I noticed a significant difference. Here's how to do a side plank:

1. Lie on your side with your legs straight and your feet stacked on top of each other.

2. Prop yourself up on one forearm, keeping your elbow directly under your shoulder.

3. Lift your hips off the ground, keeping your body in a straight line from your head to your feet.

4. Hold this position for 30 seconds, then switch to the other side.

How to Do Russian Twists:

Another exercise that can help work out the side of your abs is the Russian twist. Here's how to do it:

1. Sit on the ground with your knees bent and your feet flat on the floor.

2. Lean back slightly and lift your feet off the ground, balancing on your tailbone.

3. Hold a weight or medicine ball in front of your chest.

4. Twist your torso to the right, bringing the weight or ball towards the ground.

5. Twist back to the center, then twist to the left and bring the weight or ball towards the ground again.

6. Repeat this twisting motion for 10-12 reps on each side.

The Benefits of Working Out the Side of Your Abs:

In addition to helping you achieve a toned midsection, working out the side of your abs can also have other benefits. For example, strong obliques can help improve your posture and reduce your risk of injury during physical activity. Additionally, working out the side of your abs can help improve your overall athletic performance by improving your balance and stability.

How to Incorporate Side Abs Workouts into Your Fitness Routine:

If you're looking to work out the side of your abs, there are a few ways to incorporate these exercises into your fitness routine. One option is to do a dedicated abs workout once or twice a week, focusing on exercises like side planks, Russian twists, and side crunches. Alternatively, you can add these exercises to the end of your regular workouts as a finisher.

Conclusion:

Working out the side of your abs is definitely achievable with the right techniques and exercises. By incorporating exercises like side planks, Russian twists, and side crunches into your workout routine, you can start to see results and achieve your fitness goals. Remember to maintain proper form and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts over time. With dedication and consistency, you can achieve a toned and strong midsection.

Question and Answer:

Q: What are some common mistakes people make when working out the side of their abs?

A: One common mistake is not maintaining proper form during exercises like side planks or Russian twists. It's important to keep your body in a straight line and engage your core throughout the movement. Another mistake is not incorporating enough variety into your ab workouts. By doing the same exercises over and over, you may not be targeting all areas of your abs effectively.

Q: How often should I work out the side of my abs?

A: It's generally recommended to work out your abs 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between. However, everyone's fitness goals and schedules are different, so it's important to find a routine that works for you.

Q: Is it necessary to use weights during exercises like Russian twists?

A: No, it's not necessary to use weights during exercises like Russian twists. However, adding weight can help increase the intensity of the exercise and challenge your muscles in new ways. If you're new to these exercises, start with bodyweight only and gradually add weight as you get stronger.

Q: Can working out the side of my abs help me get rid of love handles?

A: While spot reduction is not possible (i.e. you can't target fat loss to a specific area of your body), working out the side of your abs can help tone and strengthen your obliques. This, in turn, can help create a more defined waistline and reduce the appearance of love handles.
