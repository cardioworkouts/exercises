How Does the Total Crunch Machine Work?

The Total Crunch Machine works by engaging your entire core in each rep. The unique design of the machine allows for a full range of motion, which means you're engaging more muscles with each exercise. The machine also provides resistance, which helps to build strength and tone your muscles.

To use the Total Crunch Machine, you start by sitting on the seat with your feet on the footrests. You then grab the handlebars and pull them toward your chest while simultaneously lifting your legs.
