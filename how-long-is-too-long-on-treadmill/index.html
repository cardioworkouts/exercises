Do you spend hours on a treadmill without realizing the time? Does your body ache after a long treadmill session? If yes, then you might be wondering how long is too long on treadmill. It's important to understand the effects of prolonged treadmill usage to prevent any injuries or health issues.

The pain points related to how long is too long on treadmill include joint pain, muscle soreness, and fatigue. Spending too much time on a treadmill can also lead to overuse injuries such as stress fractures, shin splints, and tendinitis.

Factors to Consider for How Long is Too Long on Treadmill

Tips for Preventing Injuries During Treadmill Usage
