Best Bodyweight Hiit A Tutorial To Get Fit Without Gym Equipment

Written by William Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym just to wait for your turn on the equipment? Do you want to get fit but don't have the budget for gym membership or home gym equipment? Say no more! Bodyweight HIIT is the perfect solution for you.

Many people struggle with finding the time and money to go to the gym or purchase expensive home gym equipment. Bodyweight HIIT is a great alternative as it doesn't require any gym equipment and can be done at home or outdoors. It's also a great way to challenge yourself with high intensity workouts that improve your overall fitness.

Bodyweight HIIT is a type of workout that involves high intensity intervals of bodyweight exercises. It's a great way to burn fat and build lean muscle mass. The best part? You can do it anywhere, anytime. All you need is a timer, some space, and your body.

In this article, we'll explore the benefits of bodyweight HIIT, give you some sample workouts, and answer some common questions about this type of exercise.

What is the Target of Bodyweight HIIT?

The target of bodyweight HIIT is to improve your overall fitness through high intensity exercise. It's a great way to improve cardiovascular health, build lean muscle mass, and burn fat. HIIT workouts involve short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief periods of rest. This type of workout has been proven to be more effective than steady state cardio in burning fat and improving fitness.

When I started doing bodyweight HIIT, I was surprised at how challenging it was. I had been doing steady state cardio for years, but I wasn't seeing the results I wanted. After just a few weeks of bodyweight HIIT, I noticed a significant improvement in my fitness level and body composition.

Sample Bodyweight HIIT Workouts

Here are some sample bodyweight HIIT workouts to get you started:

20-Minute Bodyweight HIIT Workout

Perform each exercise for 40 seconds, followed by a 20-second rest. Complete four rounds of the following exercises:

  • Jumping jacks
  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Mountain climbers

Tabata Bodyweight HIIT Workout

Perform each exercise for 20 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest. Complete eight rounds of the following exercises:

  • Burpees
  • Jump lunges
  • Plank jacks
  • High knees

15-Minute Bodyweight HIIT Workout

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, followed by a 15-second rest. Complete three rounds of the following exercises:

  • Squat jumps
  • Tricep dips
  • Alternating reverse lunges
  • Bicycle crunches

Benefits of Bodyweight HIIT

Bodyweight HIIT has many benefits, including:

  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased lean muscle mass
  • Burns more fat than steady state cardio
  • Can be done anywhere, anytime
  • Doesn't require any gym equipment
  • Improves overall fitness

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do bodyweight HIIT?

A: It's recommended to do bodyweight HIIT workouts three to four times a week for optimal results.

Q: Can bodyweight HIIT be modified for beginners?

A: Yes, bodyweight HIIT can be modified for beginners by reducing the intensity and duration of the workouts.

Q: Do I need any equipment for bodyweight HIIT?

A: No, bodyweight HIIT doesn't require any gym equipment. All you need is a timer, some space, and your body.

Q: How long should a bodyweight HIIT workout be?

A: Bodyweight HIIT workouts can range from 10 to 30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and goals.

Conclusion of Best Bodyweight HIIT

Bodyweight HIIT is a great way to improve your fitness level without the need for gym equipment or a gym membership. It's a challenging and effective workout that can be done anywhere, anytime. Try some of the sample workouts above and see the results for yourself. Remember to always listen to your body and modify the workouts as needed. Happy exercising!
