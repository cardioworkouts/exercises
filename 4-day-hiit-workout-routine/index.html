4 Day Hiit Workout Routine A Guide To Effective Fat Burning

Written by Aprilia Oct 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of spending hours at the gym without seeing any results? Do you want to burn fat and build muscle in a shorter amount of time? If so, then a 4 day HIIT workout routine may be the answer you're looking for. In this guide, we'll explore the benefits of HIIT, how it works, and provide you with a complete 4 day workout plan to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle with finding the time and motivation to exercise regularly. Traditional workouts can be time-consuming and may not provide the results you're looking for. HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) is a type of exercise that has gained popularity in recent years due to its effectiveness in burning fat and building muscle in a shorter amount of time. However, it can be challenging and intense, which may deter some people from trying it.

The target of a 4 day HIIT workout routine is to help you burn fat and build muscle by incorporating high-intensity exercises in short bursts. This type of exercise has been proven to be more effective at burning fat than traditional cardio exercises. Additionally, HIIT workouts can be completed in a shorter amount of time, making it more convenient for people with busy schedules.

In summary, a 4 day HIIT workout routine is an effective way to burn fat and build muscle in a shorter amount of time. By incorporating high-intensity exercises into your workout routine, you can achieve your fitness goals more efficiently.

Day 1: Full Body HIIT Workout

Personal Experience: On day 1, I start with a warm-up of jumping jacks and lunges. Then, I move onto exercises such as burpees, mountain climbers, and squat jumps. Each exercise is completed in 30-second intervals with a 10-second rest in between. I repeat this circuit for a total of 4 rounds. This workout targets all major muscle groups and gets my heart rate up quickly.

Day 1 of the 4 day HIIT workout routine focuses on a full-body workout. This workout includes exercises that target all major muscle groups while also incorporating cardio exercises to increase heart rate and burn fat. By targeting multiple muscle groups at once, this workout is more efficient than traditional weightlifting workouts.

Day 2: Upper Body HIIT Workout

Personal Experience: Day 2 is focused on upper body exercises such as push-ups, shoulder taps, and tricep dips. I complete each exercise in 30-second intervals with a 10-second rest in between. I repeat this circuit for a total of 4 rounds. This workout targets my arms, chest, and back while also providing a cardio component to burn fat.

Day 2 of the 4 day HIIT workout routine focuses on upper body exercises. By targeting specific muscle groups, this workout helps to build muscle and increase strength in your upper body. Additionally, by incorporating cardio exercises in between sets, this workout helps to burn fat and increase heart rate.

Day 3: Lower Body HIIT Workout

Day 3 of the 4 day HIIT workout routine focuses on lower body exercises such as squats, lunges, and calf raises. I complete each exercise in 30-second intervals with a 10-second rest in between. I repeat this circuit for a total of 4 rounds. This workout targets my legs and glutes while also providing a cardio component to burn fat.

By targeting your lower body, this workout helps to build muscle and increase strength in your legs and glutes. Additionally, by incorporating cardio exercises in between sets, this workout helps to burn fat and increase heart rate.

Day 4: Core HIIT Workout

Personal Experience: Day 4 is focused on core exercises such as planks, sit-ups, and bicycle crunches. I complete each exercise in 30-second intervals with a 10-second rest in between. I repeat this circuit for a total of 4 rounds. This workout targets my abs and obliques while also providing a cardio component to burn fat.

Day 4 of the 4 day HIIT workout routine focuses on core exercises. By targeting your abs and obliques, this workout helps to build a strong core and improve posture. Additionally, by incorporating cardio exercises in between sets, this workout helps to burn fat and increase heart rate.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I modify the exercises if I'm a beginner?

A: Yes, you can modify the exercises to fit your fitness level. For example, you can start with fewer sets or modify the exercises to be less intense.

Q: How many calories can I burn with a 4 day HIIT workout routine?

A: The number of calories you burn depends on your fitness level, age, and weight. However, studies have shown that HIIT workouts can burn up to 25-30% more calories than traditional cardio exercises.

Q: Do I need any equipment for a 4 day HIIT workout routine?

A: No, you don't need any equipment for a 4 day HIIT workout routine. However, you can use weights or resistance bands to add intensity to your workouts.

Q: How often should I do a 4 day HIIT workout routine?

A: You can do a 4 day HIIT workout routine 2-3 times per week. It's important to give your body time to rest and recover between workouts.

Conclusion of 4 Day HIIT Workout Routine

A 4 day HIIT workout routine is an effective way to burn fat and build muscle in a shorter amount of time. By incorporating high-intensity exercises into your workout routine, you can achieve your fitness goals more efficiently. Remember to listen to your body and modify the exercises as needed. With consistency and dedication, you can achieve a stronger, healthier body.
