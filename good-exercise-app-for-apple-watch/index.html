The Best Exercise Apps For Your Apple Watch

Written by William Oct 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to keep track of your workouts and progress on your own? Do you want to take your fitness goals to the next level? Look no further than your Apple Watch! With the right exercise app, your watch can become your personal trainer, helping you stay on track and motivated every step of the way.

If you're struggling to find the perfect exercise app for your Apple Watch, you're not alone. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll break down the best exercise apps for your Apple Watch and help you find the perfect fit for your fitness goals.

First, let's take a look at the top exercise apps in general. These apps are popular across all types of devices and are great options for anyone looking to get in shape:

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is one of the most popular fitness apps on the market, and for good reason. It's packed with features that help you track your workouts, monitor your calorie intake, and stay motivated all day long. Whether you're trying to lose weight, gain muscle, or just maintain your current fitness level, MyFitnessPal has everything you need to succeed.

Personally, I've been using MyFitnessPal for years and it's been a game-changer for my fitness journey. The app makes it easy to log my meals and workouts, and the community support is unbeatable.

2. Nike Training Club

For those who love a good sweat session, Nike Training Club is a must-have app. With hundreds of workouts led by Nike Master Trainers, you can mix and match your routine to keep things fresh and challenging. Plus, the app syncs seamlessly with your Apple Watch so you can track your progress in real-time.

I've recently started using Nike Training Club and I love the variety of workouts available. Whether I'm in the mood for yoga, strength training, or a quick cardio session, there's always something new to try.

3. Fitbod

If you're looking for a more personalized workout experience, Fitbod is the app for you. The app uses machine learning algorithms to create a custom workout plan based on your fitness goals, abilities, and equipment. Plus, the app adjusts your plan over time as you progress, so you never hit a plateau.

I haven't personally used Fitbod yet, but I've heard great things from friends who swear by it for their gym workouts.

4. Strava

For the runners and cyclists out there, Strava is a must-have app. It tracks your workouts using GPS, giving you real-time stats on your speed, distance, and elevation. Plus, the app has a social component that lets you connect with other athletes and join challenges to keep you motivated.

I've been using Strava for my outdoor runs and bike rides for years and it's been a great tool for tracking my progress and connecting with other athletes.

5. Seven

Short on time but still want to get a good workout in? Seven is the app for you. It features quick, high-intensity workouts that you can do anywhere, anytime. Plus, the app syncs with your Apple Watch so you can track your progress and stay motivated.

I love using Seven for days when I don't have a lot of time to work out but still want to get my heart rate up. The workouts are challenging but doable, and they always leave me feeling energized.

Conclusion:

When it comes to finding the perfect exercise app for your Apple Watch, there's no one-size-fits-all solution. It's important to consider your fitness goals, preferences, and budget when choosing an app. But with the right app, your Apple Watch can become your personal trainer, helping you stay on track and motivated every step of the way.

FAQs:

Q: Can I use these apps with any Apple Watch model?

A: Yes, these apps are compatible with all Apple Watch models.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: MyFitnessPal and Nike Training Club offer free versions with limited features, while Fitbod, Strava, and Seven require a subscription for full access.

Q: Can I use multiple exercise apps on my Apple Watch?

A: Yes, you can download and use as many exercise apps as you like on your Apple Watch.

Q: Do these apps sync with other fitness apps, like Apple Health?

A: Yes, all of these apps sync with Apple Health, so you can keep all your fitness data in one place.

Conclusion:

With so many great exercise apps available for your Apple Watch, there's no excuse not to stay on top of your fitness goals. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, there's an app out there that can help you achieve your best self. So what are you waiting for? Download one today and start crushing your fitness goals!
