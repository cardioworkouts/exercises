Best Workout Shoes For High Intensity Interval Training A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking to get the most out of your high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts? Look no further than your feet. The right pair of workout shoes can make all the difference in your performance and results. But with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training. In this comprehensive guide, we'll explore the key features to look for and recommend some of the top shoes for this type of workout.

When it comes to high-intensity interval training, your shoes need to be able to handle quick movements, lateral shifts, and high-impact exercises. The wrong shoes can lead to discomfort, injury, and even hinder your progress. That's why it's important to choose a pair specifically designed for this type of workout.

The best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training should have a few key features. They should offer stability and support, especially in the midfoot and heel. They should also have good shock absorption, as you'll be doing a lot of jumping and other high-impact exercises. The shoes should also have good traction, as you'll be doing a lot of lateral movements and quick changes of direction. Finally, they should be lightweight and breathable to keep your feet cool and comfortable during your workouts.

In summary, the best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training should have these features:

  • Stability and support
  • Good shock absorption
  • Good traction
  • Lightweight and breathable

Nike Metcon 6

The Nike Metcon 6 is one of the best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training on the market. It has a stable heel and midfoot, as well as a flexible forefoot for quick movements. The shoe also has a removable Hyperlift insert that allows you to adjust the heel height for better support during exercises like squats and lunges. The Metcon 6 also has good shock absorption and traction, making it a great all-around shoe for HIIT workouts.

Reebok Nano X1

The Reebok Nano X1 is another top pick for high-intensity interval training. It has a stable platform and a molded heel cup for support during lateral movements. The shoe also has Floatride Energy foam for cushioning and a durable outsole for good traction. The Nano X1 is also lightweight and breathable, making it a comfortable option for long workouts.

Other Top Picks

Other top picks for best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training include the Adidas Ultraboost 21, the Under Armour HOVR Phantom 2, and the New Balance Fresh Foam 1080v11. Each of these shoes has their own unique features that make them a great choice for this type of workout.

Tips for Choosing the Right Workout Shoes

When choosing the best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training, it's important to consider your individual needs and preferences. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

  • Try on shoes in person to ensure a proper fit
  • Look for shoes with the features discussed earlier (stability, shock absorption, traction, lightweight and breathable)
  • Consider your budget
  • Read reviews from other fitness enthusiasts

My Personal Experience with HIIT Shoes

As someone who does a lot of high-intensity interval training, I know firsthand how important it is to have the right shoes. I used to wear running shoes for my HIIT workouts, but I quickly learned that they weren't providing the support and stability I needed. After doing some research, I invested in a pair of Reebok Nanos and have never looked back. They provide the perfect combination of stability, cushioning, and traction for my workouts.

FAQs

What's the difference between running shoes and HIIT shoes?

Running shoes are designed for forward motion and don't typically provide the stability and support needed for lateral movements and quick changes of direction. HIIT shoes, on the other hand, are designed specifically for this type of workout and have features like a stable platform and good traction.

Can I use my cross-training shoes for HIIT workouts?

While cross-training shoes can be a good option for HIIT workouts, they don't always provide the same level of stability and support as shoes specifically designed for this type of workout. It's best to choose a shoe that is specifically designed for high-intensity interval training.

How often should I replace my HIIT shoes?

It's recommended to replace your workout shoes every 300-500 miles or every 6-8 months, whichever comes first. However, if you notice any signs of wear and tear or discomfort, it may be time to replace them sooner.

Can I wear my HIIT shoes for other types of workouts?

Yes, HIIT shoes can be a good option for other types of workouts that involve quick movements and lateral shifts, such as dance classes or kickboxing.

Conclusion of Best Workout Shoes for High-Intensity Interval Training

Choosing the best workout shoes for high-intensity interval training can make all the difference in your performance and results. Look for shoes with stability, shock absorption, traction, and that are lightweight and breathable. Some top picks include the Nike Metcon 6 and the Reebok Nano X1, but there are many other great options on the market. Consider your individual needs and preferences when making your choice, and don't be afraid to try on multiple pairs before making a decision.
