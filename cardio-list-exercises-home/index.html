How to Do Cardio List Exercises Home

Cardio list exercises can be done in a variety of ways, depending on your fitness level and goals. If you're new to working out, start with 10-15 minutes of cardio list exercises a day and gradually increase the time and intensity as you get stronger.

Here's a simple cardio list workout you can do at home:

  2. High knees - 30 seconds
  3. Burpees - 30 seconds

  5. Rest - 30 seconds
