Best Exercise Tracking Apps For Iphone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of manually tracking your workouts on paper or in a notebook? Do you struggle to remember how much weight you lifted or how far you ran during your last workout? If so, you need an exercise tracking app for your iPhone. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your needs. In this article, we will review the top exercise tracking apps for iPhone and help you find the perfect one for you.

When it comes to exercise tracking apps, there are a few pain points that users often experience. Some apps are too complicated to use, while others lack the features that users need to track their workouts effectively. Additionally, many users struggle to find an app that is both affordable and offers a wide range of features.

The target of this article is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best exercise tracking apps for iPhone. We will review each app's features, ease of use, and price to help you choose the right one for your fitness goals. Here are our top picks for the best exercise tracking apps for iPhone:

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is one of the most popular exercise tracking apps available for iPhone. It offers a wide range of features, including calorie tracking, exercise tracking, and meal planning. The app is easy to use, and it syncs with other fitness apps and devices, such as Fitbit and Apple Watch. MyFitnessPal is free to use, but it also offers a premium version with additional features for $9.99 per month.

Personally, I have been using MyFitnessPal for several years to track my workouts and diet. The app has helped me lose weight and stay on track with my fitness goals. I love that it offers a barcode scanner, which makes it easy to log food items, and the exercise tracking feature is simple to use.

2. Nike Training Club

Another great exercise tracking app for iPhone is Nike Training Club. This app offers a wide range of workouts, from yoga to strength training, and it is suitable for all fitness levels. Nike Training Club offers personalized workout recommendations based on your fitness level and goals, and it also includes video demonstrations of each exercise. The app is free to use and offers premium content for $14.99 per month.

I recently started using Nike Training Club to switch up my workout routine, and I love the variety of workouts available. The app is easy to use, and the video demonstrations are helpful for learning new exercises. I also appreciate that the app offers workout recommendations based on my fitness level.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a workout tracking app that offers personalized workout recommendations based on your fitness level and goals. The app includes a wide range of exercises, and it adjusts your workout plan as you progress. Fitbod also tracks your progress and provides insights into your fitness journey. The app is free to use, but it also offers a premium version with additional features for $9.99 per month.

I have not personally used Fitbod, but it is highly rated among fitness enthusiasts. Users appreciate the personalized workout recommendations and the app's ability to adjust your workout plan as you progress. Additionally, the app's tracking features make it easy to see your progress over time.

How to Choose the Best Exercise Tracking App for Your Needs

Now that you know our top picks for the best exercise tracking apps for iPhone, how do you choose the right one for your needs? Here are a few factors to consider:

  • Features: Look for an app that offers the features you need to track your workouts effectively.
  • Price: Consider the app's price, and decide whether the cost is worth the features it offers.
  • Ease of use: Choose an app that is easy to use and navigate.
  • Compatibility: Make sure the app is compatible with your iPhone and any other fitness devices you use.

How to Get the Most Out of Your Exercise Tracking App

Once you choose an exercise tracking app, how do you get the most out of it? Here are a few tips:

  • Log your workouts consistently to get an accurate picture of your progress.
  • Set realistic goals and track your progress towards them.
  • Use the app's tracking features to monitor your progress over time.
  • Connect with other users in the app's community to stay motivated and accountable.

Conclusion of Best Exercise Tracking Apps for iPhone

In conclusion, exercise tracking apps are an essential tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness level. Our top picks for the best exercise tracking apps for iPhone are MyFitnessPal, Nike Training Club, and Fitbod. When choosing the right app for your needs, consider the app's features, price, ease of use, and compatibility. Remember to log your workouts consistently and use the app's tracking features to monitor your progress. With the right exercise tracking app, you can stay motivated and accountable as you work towards your fitness goals.
