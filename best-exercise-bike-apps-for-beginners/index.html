Best Exercise Bike Apps For Beginners A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start your fitness journey on an exercise bike? It can be overwhelming to figure out where to start, but luckily, there are a variety of exercise bike apps available to help guide you through your workouts. In this guide, we'll cover the best exercise bike apps for beginners and how they can help you reach your fitness goals.

What are the Best Exercise Bike Apps for Beginners?

There are many exercise bike apps available, but not all of them are suited for beginners. The best exercise bike apps for beginners are those that offer guided workouts, customizable settings, and easy-to-use interfaces. Some of the best exercise bike apps for beginners include:

  • Peloton Digital: Peloton Digital offers a variety of guided workouts, including cycling classes, yoga, and strength training. Their app also allows you to customize your settings and track your progress.
  • Zwift: Zwift offers a virtual cycling experience, where you can ride through various locations and compete with other users. Their app also offers guided workouts and customizable settings.
  • Keelo: Keelo offers high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts, including cycling workouts. Their app also offers personalized coaching and progress tracking.
  • MyFitnessPal: MyFitnessPal offers a variety of workout tracking options, including cycling workouts. Their app also offers nutrition tracking and personalized goal setting.

These apps are great for beginners because they offer a variety of options and customizable settings, allowing you to tailor your workouts to your fitness level and goals.

My Experience with Peloton Digital

As a beginner, I found Peloton Digital to be the most helpful exercise bike app. Their guided workouts were easy to follow, and their instructors were motivating and supportive. I also appreciated the ability to customize my settings and track my progress. Overall, Peloton Digital helped me stay motivated and on track with my fitness goals.

Zwift: A Virtual Cycling Experience

Zwift offers a unique virtual cycling experience that can make your workouts more engaging and fun. As you ride through various locations, you can compete with other users and track your progress. Zwift also offers guided workouts and customizable settings, making it a great option for beginners. I personally enjoy Zwift because it adds an element of competition to my workouts, which helps me stay motivated.

Keelo: Personalized Coaching and Progress Tracking

Keelo offers personalized coaching and progress tracking, which can be helpful for beginners who are just starting their fitness journey. Their HIIT workouts, including cycling workouts, are designed to maximize calorie burn and muscle building. I appreciate the personalized coaching aspect of Keelo because it helps me stay accountable and motivated.

MyFitnessPal: Nutrition Tracking and Goal Setting

While MyFitnessPal is not specifically an exercise bike app, it offers a variety of workout tracking options, including cycling workouts. Their app also offers nutrition tracking and personalized goal setting, which can be helpful for beginners who are looking to improve their overall health and fitness. I appreciate the comprehensive approach of MyFitnessPal because it helps me stay on track with both my workouts and nutrition.

Conclusion of Best Exercise Bike Apps for Beginners

There are many great exercise bike apps available for beginners, and the best one for you will depend on your individual fitness goals and preferences. Whether you prefer guided workouts, virtual cycling experiences, personalized coaching, or comprehensive tracking options, there is an app out there that will meet your needs. By incorporating one of these apps into your fitness routine, you can stay motivated and on track towards achieving your fitness goals.

Questions and Answers

Q: Can I use these apps with any exercise bike?

A: Yes, most exercise bike apps are compatible with a variety of exercise bikes. However, it's always a good idea to check the app's compatibility before downloading.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: Some of these apps offer free versions, but many require a subscription to access all of their features. Prices vary depending on the app.

Q: Can I use these apps if I'm not a beginner?

A: Yes, these apps are designed to be useful for people of all fitness levels. However, some of the guided workouts and coaching features may be more helpful for beginners.

Q: Do I need an internet connection to use these apps?

A: Most of these apps require an internet connection to access their features. However, some, like MyFitnessPal, offer offline tracking options.
