How Many Hours For Fasted Cardio

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering how many hours of fasted cardio you should do to achieve your weight loss goals? It can be confusing to determine how much cardio you should do on an empty stomach and for how long. But don't worry, in this article, we will answer your questions related to how many hours for fasted cardio and related keywords.

Most people perform fasted cardio to lose weight quickly, but they don't get the desired results because they don't know how many hours they should do it. Some people also perform fasted cardio for too long, which can lead to muscle loss and fatigue.

The ideal duration for fasted cardio is 30-45 minutes, and you should perform it 3-4 times a week. But the duration and frequency of fasted cardio depend on your fitness level, body type, and weight loss goals.

In summary, if you want to lose weight quickly, you should perform fasted cardio for 30-45 minutes, 3-4 times a week. But the duration and frequency of fasted cardio depend on your fitness level, body type, and weight loss goals.

My Personal Experience with Fasted Cardio

When I started my weight loss journey, I was confused about how much cardio I should do on an empty stomach. I tried doing fasted cardio for an hour, but I felt exhausted and didn't get the desired results. Later, I consulted a fitness expert who advised me to perform fasted cardio for 30-45 minutes, 3-4 times a week. I followed his advice and started seeing results within a few weeks.

The Benefits of Fasted Cardio

Fasted cardio has several benefits, including increased fat burning, improved insulin sensitivity, and reduced hunger cravings. When you perform cardio on an empty stomach, your body uses stored fat as fuel, which leads to increased fat burning. Fasted cardio also improves insulin sensitivity, which means your body can use glucose more efficiently. Finally, fasted cardio reduces hunger cravings, which can lead to weight loss.

The Best Time to Perform Fasted Cardio

The best time to perform fasted cardio is in the morning before breakfast. When you wake up, your body is in a fasted state, and performing cardio at this time can lead to increased fat burning. But if you can't perform fasted cardio in the morning, you can do it at any other time of the day, as long as you haven't eaten for 2-3 hours.

Tips for Performing Fasted Cardio

Here are some tips for performing fasted cardio:

  • Drink water before and after fasted cardio to stay hydrated.
  • Perform low-intensity cardio, such as walking or cycling, to prevent muscle loss.
  • Eat a healthy meal after fasted cardio to replenish your energy levels.
  • Gradually increase the duration and frequency of fasted cardio to avoid fatigue and muscle loss.

Conclusion of How Many Hours for Fasted Cardio

In conclusion, the ideal duration for fasted cardio is 30-45 minutes, and you should perform it 3-4 times a week. But the duration and frequency of fasted cardio depend on your fitness level, body type, and weight loss goals. Fasted cardio has several benefits, including increased fat burning, improved insulin sensitivity, and reduced hunger cravings. To perform fasted cardio effectively, drink water, perform low-intensity cardio, eat a healthy meal afterward, and gradually increase the duration and frequency of cardio.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should I wait to eat after fasted cardio?

A: You should eat a healthy meal containing protein and carbohydrates within 30 minutes to an hour after fasted cardio to replenish your energy levels.

Q: Is fasted cardio safe for everyone?

A: Fasted cardio is safe for most people, but if you have a medical condition or are pregnant, you should consult your doctor before performing fasted cardio.

Q: Can I perform fasted cardio every day?

A: You can perform fasted cardio every day, but it's not recommended as it can lead to muscle loss and fatigue. It's best to perform fasted cardio 3-4 times a week and gradually increase the duration and frequency.

Q: Can I drink coffee before fasted cardio?

A: Yes, you can drink black coffee before fasted cardio as it can increase fat burning and improve performance. But avoid adding sugar or cream to your coffee as it can break your fast.
