What To Do On Treadmill To Lose Belly Fat A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose belly fat and thinking of hitting the gym? Treadmill can be a great option to burn fat and get in shape. But what to do on a treadmill to lose belly fat? In this blog post, we will guide you through some effective exercises that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle with belly fat and find it challenging to get rid of it. Belly fat is not only aesthetically unpleasing but also poses health risks such as heart diseases, diabetes, and high blood pressure. Losing belly fat requires a combination of a healthy diet, regular exercise, and a healthy lifestyle. However, exercising on a treadmill can be one of the most effective ways to burn belly fat.

So, what to do on a treadmill to lose belly fat? Below are some exercises that can help you shed those extra pounds:

1. Walking

Walking is the easiest and most basic exercise you can do on a treadmill. It is a low-impact exercise that is suitable for beginners. Start by walking at a moderate pace for 20-30 minutes, gradually increasing the pace and duration as you get comfortable. Walking can help you burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and reduce belly fat.

2. Running

Running is a high-intensity exercise that can help you burn a lot of calories in a short amount of time. It is an effective way to lose belly fat and improve overall fitness. However, it is important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your running sessions to avoid injuries.

3. Inclined Walking/Running

Walking or running on an inclined surface can increase the intensity of your workout and help you burn more calories. It also engages more muscles, including your core muscles, which can help you tone your abs and lose belly fat faster.

4. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

HIIT is a popular workout technique that involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest. HIIT can help you burn more calories in less time and is an effective way to lose belly fat. However, it is important to consult a fitness trainer before starting HIIT to avoid injuries.

5. Side Shuffles

Side shuffles are a great exercise to target your oblique muscles, which are responsible for your waistline. Start by walking sideways on the treadmill, alternating sides for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Gradually increase the duration and speed as you get comfortable.

So, these are some of the exercises you can do on a treadmill to lose belly fat. Remember to combine these exercises with a healthy diet and lifestyle to achieve your fitness goals.

My Personal Experience

When I started working out on a treadmill, I was a complete beginner and had no idea what to do to lose belly fat. However, with the help of a fitness trainer, I learned some effective exercises that helped me shed those extra pounds. Walking and running on an inclined surface and doing side shuffles were some of my favorite exercises that helped me tone my abs and lose belly fat.

FAQs

Q. How long should I workout on a treadmill to lose belly fat?

A. It is recommended to workout on a treadmill for at least 30 minutes a day to lose belly fat. However, the duration and intensity of your workout may vary depending on your fitness level and goals.

Q. Can I lose belly fat by just walking on a treadmill?

A. Walking is a low-impact exercise that can help you burn calories and lose belly fat. However, it may take longer to see visible results than high-intensity exercises such as running or HIIT.

Q. What should I eat to lose belly fat?

A. A healthy diet plays a crucial role in losing belly fat. Include foods that are high in fiber, protein, and healthy fats in your diet. Avoid processed and sugary foods and drinks.

Q. Can I do treadmill exercises at home?

A. Yes, you can buy a treadmill for home use and do these exercises at home. However, it is important to consult a fitness trainer before starting any exercise program to avoid injuries.

Conclusion of What to Do on Treadmill to Lose Belly Fat

In conclusion, exercising on a treadmill can be an effective way to lose belly fat and get in shape. Walking, running, inclined walking/running, HIIT, and side shuffles are some of the exercises you can do on a treadmill to achieve your fitness goals. Remember to combine these exercises with a healthy diet and lifestyle to see visible results.
