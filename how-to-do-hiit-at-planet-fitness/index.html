How To Do Hiit At Planet Fitness A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who's looking to get in shape but hates spending hours on the treadmill, then you might want to try out HIIT. High-Intensity Interval Training is a form of exercise that is known for its effectiveness in burning calories and building endurance. However, if you're new to the gym or have never tried HIIT before, it can be intimidating to get started. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to do HIIT at Planet Fitness.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to doing HIIT at Planet Fitness is that it can be difficult to find a good space to do it. The gym can get crowded, and the equipment can be limited. Additionally, many people find it hard to keep up with the fast-paced nature of HIIT, which can be discouraging.

To do HIIT at Planet Fitness, you'll need to find a space that's big enough for you to move around in. This could be an open area on the gym floor, a spot in the stretching room, or even a corner of the locker room. You'll also need to make sure that you have access to a timer or stopwatch, as timing is crucial in HIIT workouts.

In summary, to do HIIT at Planet Fitness, you'll need to find a space to work out in, have access to a timer or stopwatch, and be prepared to work hard in short bursts of intense exercise.

What is HIIT and How Does It Work?

High-Intensity Interval Training, or HIIT, is a form of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. The idea behind HIIT is that it pushes your body to its limits and forces it to burn more calories than it would during a steady-state workout.

When you do HIIT, your body goes through several different stages. First, you'll warm up your muscles and get your heart rate up. Then, you'll do a series of exercises that challenge your body and push it to its limits. Finally, you'll cool down and stretch out your muscles to prevent injury and promote recovery.

Overall, HIIT is a great way to get in shape because it combines cardio and strength training into one workout. It can help you burn fat, build muscle, and improve your cardiovascular health all at the same time.

My Personal Experience with HIIT at Planet Fitness

When I first started doing HIIT at Planet Fitness, I was intimidated by the fast-paced nature of the workout. However, I quickly realized that the key to success was not to focus on what other people were doing, but to focus on my own progress.

I found that the best way to do HIIT at Planet Fitness was to use a combination of bodyweight exercises and cardio machines. For example, I would start with 30 seconds of jumping jacks, then move to the treadmill for 30 seconds of sprinting, followed by 30 seconds of rest. I would repeat this cycle for 10-15 minutes, depending on how I was feeling that day.

Overall, I found that HIIT at Planet Fitness was a great way to get a full-body workout in a short amount of time. It was challenging, but also fun and rewarding.

Tips for Doing HIIT at Planet Fitness

Here are some tips to help you get started with HIIT at Planet Fitness:

1. Start Slow

If you're new to HIIT, don't try to do too much too soon. Start with shorter intervals and work your way up as you get stronger.

2. Use a Timer

Timing is crucial in HIIT workouts. Use a timer or stopwatch to keep track of your intervals, and make sure you stick to them.

3. Be Prepared to Modify

If you find that a certain exercise is too difficult or uncomfortable, don't be afraid to modify it. For example, you can do jumping jacks with lower impact or use a lower resistance on the elliptical.

4. Stay Hydrated

HIIT workouts can be intense, so make sure you drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do HIIT at Planet Fitness?

A: It depends on your fitness level and goals. If you're just starting out, aim for 1-2 HIIT workouts per week. As you get stronger, you can increase the frequency to 3-4 times per week.

Q: Can I do HIIT at Planet Fitness without equipment?

A: Yes! HIIT can be done with just your bodyweight, so you don't need any equipment to get started. However, using equipment like treadmills or ellipticals can add variety and make your workouts more challenging.

Q: How long should my HIIT workouts be?

A: HIIT workouts can range from 10-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and goals. If you're just starting out, aim for shorter workouts and work your way up as you get stronger.

Q: Can I do HIIT at Planet Fitness if I have an injury?

A: It depends on the injury. Always consult with a doctor or physical therapist before starting a new exercise routine, especially if you have an injury. They can help you determine which exercises are safe for you to do.

Conclusion of How to Do HIIT at Planet Fitness

HIIT is a great way to get in shape and can be done at Planet Fitness with just a little bit of preparation. Find a space to work out in, have access to a timer or stopwatch, and be prepared to work hard in short bursts of intense exercise. Use bodyweight exercises and cardio machines to create a challenging and rewarding workout, and don't forget to start slow and modify exercises as needed. With a little bit of practice, you'll be a HIIT pro in no time!
