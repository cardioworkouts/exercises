The Best Hiit Workout At Home For Beginners A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a beginner looking for the best HIIT workout to do at home? Are you struggling to find the right exercises that suit your fitness level? HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) is a popular form of exercise that has gained popularity in recent years. It involves short bursts of intense exercise with short periods of rest. HIIT is a great way to burn fat, increase endurance, and improve cardiovascular health. However, for beginners, it can be challenging to find the best HIIT workout to do at home. In this post, we will provide a complete guide on the best HIIT workout at home for beginners.

The target of this post is to provide beginners with the best HIIT workout to do at home. We will cover the best exercises to do, the duration of the workout, the number of sets, and the rest time. We will also provide tips on how to warm-up and cool down before and after the workout. This post will help beginners to understand the benefits of HIIT and how to perform it safely and effectively.

In summary, the best HIIT workout at home for beginners should include exercises that are easy to perform, have a low impact on the joints, and can be done in a small space. The workout should be 20-30 minutes long, with 30-60 seconds of intense exercise and 10-30 seconds of rest. The workout should be repeated for 3-4 sets with a 1-2 minute rest between sets. It is essential to warm-up and cool down before and after the workout to prevent injury and improve flexibility.

The Best HIIT Workout at Home for Beginners: Exercises to Do

When it comes to the best HIIT workout at home for beginners, there are several exercises to choose from. Some of the best exercises that are easy to perform and have a low impact on the joints are:

1. Jumping jacks: Stand with your feet together and your hands at your sides. Jump up and spread your legs apart while raising your arms above your head. Jump back to the starting position and repeat.

2. Mountain climbers: Start in a push-up position with your arms straight and your feet hip-width apart. Bring one knee up to your chest and then back to the starting position. Repeat with the other knee.

3. Squat jumps: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides. Lower yourself into a squat position and then jump up as high as you can. Land softly back into the squat position and repeat.

4. Burpees: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides. Lower yourself into a squat position and place your hands on the floor. Jump your feet back into a push-up position, then jump them back to the squat position. Jump up as high as you can and repeat.

These exercises can be combined to create a full-body HIIT workout that targets all major muscle groups. The workout should be performed for 20-30 minutes, with 30-60 seconds of intense exercise and 10-30 seconds of rest. The workout should be repeated for 3-4 sets with a 1-2 minute rest between sets.

Warming Up and Cooling Down

Warming up and cooling down are essential parts of any workout, including HIIT. Before starting the workout, it is important to warm up to prevent injury and improve flexibility. A good warm-up should include 5-10 minutes of light cardio, such as jogging in place or jumping jacks. It should also include some dynamic stretching, such as lunges or leg swings.

Cooling down after the workout is also important to prevent injury and improve recovery. A good cool-down should include 5-10 minutes of light cardio and some static stretching, such as holding a hamstring stretch or a quad stretch for 15-30 seconds.

Benefits of HIIT

There are several benefits of HIIT that make it an excellent form of exercise for beginners. Some of these benefits include:

1. Burn fat: HIIT is an excellent way to burn fat and lose weight. It increases your metabolism, which means you burn more calories even after the workout is over.

2. Improve cardiovascular health: HIIT improves cardiovascular health by increasing your heart rate and strengthening your heart and lungs.

3. Increase endurance: HIIT can increase your endurance and make you feel less tired during everyday activities.

4. Time-efficient: HIIT workouts are time-efficient, which makes them perfect for busy people who don't have a lot of time to exercise.

Tips for Performing HIIT Safely and Effectively

When performing HIIT, it is important to keep a few things in mind to ensure that you are doing it safely and effectively. Some tips for performing HIIT include:

1. Start slow: If you are a beginner, start with a shorter workout and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

2. Focus on form: It is important to maintain proper form during each exercise to prevent injury and get the most out of the workout.

3. Listen to your body: If you feel any pain or discomfort, stop the exercise immediately and rest. It is better to take a break than to risk injury.

4. Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after the workout to stay hydrated.

Question and Answer

Q1. Is HIIT suitable for beginners?

A1. Yes, HIIT is suitable for beginners. However, it is important to start with a shorter workout and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.

Q2. What are the best exercises for a HIIT workout at home?

A2. The best exercises for a HIIT workout at home include jumping jacks, mountain climbers, squat jumps, and burpees.

Q3. How long should a HIIT workout be?

A3. A HIIT workout should be 20-30 minutes long, with 30-60 seconds of intense exercise and 10-30 seconds of rest.

Q4. How many times a week should I do a HIIT workout?

A4. It is recommended to do a HIIT workout 2-3 times a week with at least one day of rest in between workouts.

Conclusion of Best HIIT Workout at Home for Beginners

In conclusion, the best HIIT workout at home for beginners should include exercises that are easy to perform, have a low impact on the joints, and can be done in a small space. The workout should be 20-30 minutes long, with 30-60 seconds of intense exercise and 10-30 seconds of rest. The workout should be repeated for 3-4 sets with a 1-2 minute rest between sets. It is important to warm-up and cool down before and after the workout to prevent injury and improve flexibility. HIIT is an excellent form of exercise for beginners, as it can help burn fat, improve cardiovascular health, increase endurance, and is time-efficient. By following these tips, beginners can perform HIIT safely and effectively at home.
