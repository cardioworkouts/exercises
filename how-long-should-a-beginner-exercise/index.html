How Long Should A Beginner Exercise

Written by Petter Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start an exercise routine? You might be wondering how long you should exercise to see results. The answer to this question depends on various factors, but don't worry, we're here to help you figure it out!

Starting an exercise routine can be overwhelming, especially if you're not sure where to begin. One of the biggest concerns for beginners is how long they should exercise. It's easy to get caught up in the idea that more equals better, but that's not always the case.

As a beginner, you should aim to exercise for 30 minutes a day, five days a week. This recommendation is based on guidelines from the American Heart Association and is a great starting point for most people.

However, it's important to remember that everyone's fitness level is different, and you should always listen to your body. If 30 minutes feels too challenging, start with 10 or 15 minutes a day and gradually increase your time as your fitness improves.

Additionally, it's important to mix up your exercise routine with both cardio and strength training. This will not only help prevent boredom but also provide a well-rounded workout that targets all areas of your body.

The Benefits of Starting Slow

When I first started exercising, I made the mistake of pushing myself too hard too soon. I thought that if I didn't feel sore the next day, I wasn't working hard enough. But this mentality only led to burnout and injury.

By starting slow and gradually increasing my time and intensity, I was able to build a strong foundation and avoid injury. Plus, I was able to stick with my routine long-term and see real results.

The Importance of Rest Days

Rest days are just as important as exercise days. Your body needs time to recover and repair itself after a workout. As a beginner, aim to take at least one or two rest days a week.

On rest days, focus on stretching, foam rolling, or low-impact activities such as walking or yoga.

The Role of Nutrition

Nutrition plays a vital role in any exercise routine. As a beginner, it's important to fuel your body with nutrient-dense foods that provide energy and support muscle recovery.

Focus on eating a balanced diet that includes lean protein, complex carbs, and healthy fats. Additionally, aim to drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.

Tips for Staying Motivated

Starting an exercise routine is easy, but sticking with it can be challenging. Here are some tips to help you stay motivated:

  • Find a workout buddy
  • Set realistic goals
  • Track your progress
  • Switch up your routine

Conclusion of How Long Should a Beginner Exercise

As a beginner, it's important to start slow and gradually increase your time and intensity. Aim to exercise for 30 minutes a day, five days a week, and mix up your routine with both cardio and strength training. Remember to take rest days, eat a balanced diet, and stay motivated by setting realistic goals and tracking your progress.

Question and Answer

Q: Should I exercise every day as a beginner?

A: No, it's important to give your body time to rest and recover. Aim for at least one or two rest days a week.

Q: How can I tell if I'm pushing myself too hard?

A: Listen to your body. If you feel pain or discomfort, it's a sign that you may be overdoing it. Start slow and gradually increase your time and intensity.

Q: Can I see results with just cardio or strength training?

A: While both forms of exercise have their benefits, it's important to mix up your routine with both cardio and strength training. This will provide a well-rounded workout that targets all areas of your body.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Everyone's fitness journey is different, but you should start to see results within a few weeks of starting your routine. Remember to set realistic goals and track your progress along the way.
