Question and Answer

Q: Can I do cardio every day?

A: Yes, you can do cardio every day, but it's important to listen to your body and not overdo it. It's also essential to incorporate rest days to allow your body to recover and prevent burnout.

Q: How long should I do cardio to see results?

A: The duration of cardio depends on various factors, such as your fitness level, goals, and preferences. However, most experts recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio, such as brisk walking or cycling, five days a week for overall health benefits.

Q: Can cardio alone help me lose weight?

A: Cardio alone can help you lose weight, but it's essential to combine it with a healthy diet and other fitness activities, such as strength training and yoga, for optimal results.

Q: Is cardio 6 days a week safe for everyone?

A: Cardio 6 days a week may not be safe for everyone, especially those with underlying medical conditions or injuries. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before starting a new fitness program.
