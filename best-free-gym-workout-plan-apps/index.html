Best Free Gym Workout Plan Apps A Beginner s Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to get fit but don't know where to start? Do you want to work out but can't afford a personal trainer? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best free gym workout plan apps available on the market today.

When it comes to working out, it can be challenging to find the right exercises and routines that work for you. Going to the gym can also be intimidating, especially if you're a beginner. That's why workout plan apps can be a great solution. They provide customized routines that cater to your fitness level, goals, and preferences, all in the comfort of your own space.

If you're looking for the best free gym workout plan apps, you're in luck. There are plenty of options available, each with its unique features and benefits. Some popular choices include Nike Training Club, FitOn, JEFIT, and MyFitnessPal.

Overall, these apps offer a range of exercises, from strength training to cardio, and can track your progress, provide nutrition plans, and even connect you with a community of fitness enthusiasts.

Nike Training Club

As one of the most popular workout plan apps, Nike Training Club offers over 190 free workouts that cater to all fitness levels, from beginner to advanced. It provides personalized training plans based on your fitness goals and features audio and visual guidance from Nike Master Trainers. Plus, it's entirely free and available on both iOS and Android devices.

Personally, I've been using Nike Training Club for a while now, and I love that it's tailored to my fitness level and provides detailed instructions on each exercise. It also allows me to track my progress and gives me a sense of accomplishment when I complete a workout.

FitOn

FitOn is another great option for those looking for free workout plans. It offers a range of workouts, from dance cardio to yoga, and can be customized to your fitness level and goals. It also has a social aspect, allowing you to connect with friends and trainers and participate in challenges. Plus, it's free and available on both iOS and Android devices.

I've recently started using FitOn, and I love that it provides a variety of workout options that cater to my preferences. The social aspect is also a great motivator and keeps me accountable.

JEFIT

JEFIT is a great option for those looking for strength training workouts. It offers a range of exercises and routines and allows you to track your progress and create custom workout plans. It also has a vast exercise library, providing detailed instructions and animations for each exercise. Plus, it's free and available on both iOS and Android devices.

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app that not only offers workout plans but also tracks your nutrition and calorie intake. It has a vast food database, allowing you to log your meals easily, and provides personalized nutrition plans based on your goals. Plus, it's free and available on both iOS and Android devices.

Overall, these apps offer a range of benefits and cater to different fitness goals and preferences. Whether you're looking for strength training, cardio, or nutrition plans, there's an app out there for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps entirely free?

A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article are entirely free to download and use.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app and the workouts you choose. Some apps offer bodyweight exercises that require no equipment, while others may require dumbbells or resistance bands. Make sure to check the workout descriptions before starting.

Q: Can I use these apps as a beginner?

A: Absolutely! These apps cater to all fitness levels, and many offer beginner-friendly workouts and guidance.

Q: Can I use these apps if I have an injury or medical condition?

A: It's always best to consult with your doctor before starting any new workout plan, especially if you have an injury or medical condition. Some apps offer modifications for certain exercises, but it's essential to prioritize your health and safety.

Conclusion of Best Free Gym Workout Plan Apps

Overall, workout plan apps can be a great solution for those looking to get fit and healthy without breaking the bank. They offer customized routines, tracking features, and even nutrition plans, all in the comfort of your own space. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced fitness enthusiast, there's an app out there for you. So, download one today and start your fitness journey!
