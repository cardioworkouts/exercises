Best Cardio Kickboxing On Youtube A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old cardio routine and looking for a fun and challenging way to get your heart pumping? Look no further than cardio kickboxing on YouTube! With a variety of workouts and instructors available, you can find the perfect routine to fit your fitness level and goals.

Maybe you're new to kickboxing and don't know where to start, or perhaps you're a seasoned pro looking for a new challenge. Whatever your level, finding the best cardio kickboxing on YouTube can be overwhelming with so many options available.

In this guide, we'll dive into the world of cardio kickboxing on YouTube, discussing the benefits, best practices, and top workouts to try. By the end, you'll be ready to kick your cardio routine up a notch!

To start, let's summarize the main points we'll be covering:

What is Cardio Kickboxing on YouTube?

Cardio kickboxing is a high-energy workout that combines martial arts techniques with cardiovascular exercise. It's a great way to improve your overall fitness while also learning self-defense techniques. And thanks to YouTube, you can access a variety of cardio kickboxing workouts from the comfort of your home.

Personally, I love cardio kickboxing because it's a full-body workout that keeps me engaged and motivated. I never get bored with the variety of moves and routines available, and I always leave feeling accomplished and energized.

Benefits of Cardio Kickboxing

Cardio kickboxing offers a variety of benefits, including:

  1. Improved cardiovascular health
  2. Increased strength and endurance
  3. Improved balance and coordination
  4. Stress relief
  5. Self-defense skills

Plus, it's a fun and challenging way to mix up your workout routine and keep things interesting.

How to Get Started with Cardio Kickboxing on YouTube

Before starting any new workout, it's important to warm up properly and ensure you have the right equipment. For cardio kickboxing, you'll need comfortable workout clothes, supportive shoes, and possibly gloves or wraps depending on the workout.

Once you're ready to start, search for cardio kickboxing workouts on YouTube and choose one that fits your fitness level and goals. It's important to start with beginner-level workouts if you're new to kickboxing to ensure you're using proper form and avoiding injury.

Best Practices for Cardio Kickboxing on YouTube

To get the most out of your cardio kickboxing workout, follow these best practices:

  1. Focus on proper form to avoid injury
  2. Stay hydrated and take breaks as needed
  3. Challenge yourself but listen to your body
  4. Mix up your routine to avoid boredom and plateaus

Top Cardio Kickboxing Workouts on YouTube

Now that you know the benefits and best practices of cardio kickboxing, it's time to try out some workouts! Here are my top picks for cardio kickboxing on YouTube:

1. FitnessBlender

FitnessBlender offers a variety of cardio kickboxing workouts for all levels, from beginner to advanced. Their routines are easy to follow and customizable, allowing you to adjust the intensity and duration to fit your needs.

2. PopSugar Fitness

PopSugar Fitness offers a wide range of workout videos, including several cardio kickboxing routines. Their workouts are high-energy and fun, with upbeat music and enthusiastic instructors.

3. Millionaire Hoy

Millionaire Hoy's channel features intense cardio kickboxing routines that will challenge even the most seasoned kickboxers. His workouts are fast-paced and high-energy, perfect for those looking for a serious sweat session.

4. Jessica Smith TV

Jessica Smith's channel offers a variety of low-impact cardio kickboxing workouts, making them perfect for those with joint pain or injuries. Her routines are easy to follow but still challenging, providing a great workout without the high impact.

Question and Answer

Q: Is cardio kickboxing a good workout for weight loss?

A: Yes! Cardio kickboxing is a great way to burn calories and lose weight, especially when combined with a healthy diet.

Q: Do I need any special equipment for cardio kickboxing on YouTube?

A: While you don't need any special equipment, supportive shoes and possibly gloves or wraps can help protect your hands and wrists during the workout.

Q: Can cardio kickboxing be dangerous?

A: Like any workout, cardio kickboxing can be dangerous if proper form isn't used. It's important to follow best practices and start with beginner-level workouts if you're new to kickboxing.

Q: How often should I do cardio kickboxing?

A: It's recommended to do cardio kickboxing (or any cardio exercise) at least 3-4 times a week for optimal health benefits.

Conclusion of Best Cardio Kickboxing on YouTube

Cardio kickboxing on YouTube is a fun and challenging way to improve your cardiovascular health, strength, and endurance. By following best practices and trying out different workouts, you can find the perfect routine to fit your fitness level and goals. So grab your gloves (or not) and get ready to kick up your cardio routine!
