Which Time Workout Is Best For Weight Gain

Written by Daniel Jun 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to gain weight despite working out regularly? Do you want to know which time workout is best for weight gain? If yes, then you're in the right place. In this post, we'll explore the best time to workout for weight gain and provide some tips to help you achieve your fitness goals.

When it comes to weight gain, everyone has their own unique challenges. Some people struggle to gain weight despite eating a lot, while others find it difficult to gain muscle mass. However, one thing that can help in both cases is the timing of your workout.

The best time to workout for weight gain is in the afternoon or evening. This is because your body temperature and hormone levels are at their highest during this time, which can help you build muscle mass more effectively. Additionally, working out in the afternoon or evening can help you sleep better at night, which is crucial for muscle recovery and growth.

In summary, the best time to workout for weight gain is in the afternoon or evening. Now, let's take a closer look at this topic and explore some tips to help you maximize your gains.

Working Out in the Afternoon

Personally, I've found that working out in the afternoon is the best time for weight gain. I usually have a light lunch around noon and then hit the gym at around 2 or 3 pm. This gives me enough time to digest my food and get my energy levels up before my workout.

When working out in the afternoon, it's important to fuel your body properly. Make sure you're eating enough carbohydrates and protein throughout the day to provide your muscles with the energy and nutrients they need to grow. You can also consider taking a pre-workout supplement to give you an extra boost of energy and focus.

Working Out in the Evening

Working out in the evening is another great option for weight gain. This is because your body is more relaxed and flexible in the evening, which can help you perform better during your workout. Additionally, working out in the evening can help you relieve stress and tension from your day, which can improve your overall mood and wellbeing.

When working out in the evening, it's important to avoid eating a heavy meal right before your workout. This can make you feel sluggish and uncomfortable during your workout. Instead, try to eat a light meal or snack a few hours before your workout to provide your body with the energy it needs.

Tips for Maximizing Your Gains

Regardless of whether you choose to workout in the afternoon or evening, there are some tips you can follow to maximize your gains. Firstly, make sure you're incorporating compound exercises into your workout routine. These are exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once, such as squats, deadlifts, and bench presses. Compound exercises are great for building overall muscle mass and strength.

Secondly, make sure you're giving your muscles enough time to recover between workouts. This means taking at least one day off between workouts to allow your muscles to heal and grow. Additionally, make sure you're getting enough sleep each night to support muscle recovery and growth.

How to Stay Motivated

Staying motivated is key to achieving your weight gain goals. One way to stay motivated is to track your progress. Take photos of yourself each week and track your weight and measurements. Seeing progress can be incredibly motivating and help you stay on track.

Another way to stay motivated is to find a workout buddy or hire a personal trainer. Having someone to workout with can make your workouts more enjoyable and help you stay accountable. Additionally, a personal trainer can provide you with personalized guidance and support to help you achieve your goals.

Conclusion of Which Time Workout is Best for Weight Gain

In conclusion, the best time to workout for weight gain is in the afternoon or evening. Both of these times can help you build muscle mass more effectively and improve your overall wellbeing. By following the tips outlined in this post and staying motivated, you can achieve your weight gain goals and become the best version of yourself.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it better to workout on an empty stomach?

A: No, it's not recommended to workout on an empty stomach. Your body needs fuel to provide energy for your workout, so make sure you eat a light meal or snack before exercising.

Q: How often should I workout to gain weight?

A: It's recommended to workout at least 3-4 times per week to gain weight. Make sure you're giving your muscles enough time to recover between workouts.

Q: Should I do cardio if I want to gain weight?

A: Yes, you can incorporate cardio into your workout routine if you want to gain weight. However, make sure you're not doing too much cardio as it can interfere with your muscle-building goals.

Q: Can supplements help me gain weight?

A: Yes, supplements can help you gain weight if used properly. Make sure you're using supplements that are safe and effective, and always consult with a healthcare professional before taking any new supplements.
