Best 45 Minute Hiit Workout For A Full Body Burn

Written by Bardi Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a workout that will help you burn calories, build muscle, and improve your cardiovascular health in just 45 minutes? Look no further than high-intensity interval training (HIIT). HIIT workouts are a great way to get a full body burn in a short amount of time. In this post, we'll go over the best 45 minute HIIT workout to help you reach your fitness goals.

Do you struggle with finding time to exercise? Are you tired of long, boring workouts that don't produce results? Do you want to improve your overall health and fitness but don't know where to start? These are common pain points that many people face when it comes to exercise. HIIT workouts are a great solution because they are short, intense, and effective, making them an ideal choice for busy people who want to get the most out of their workouts.

The target of the best 45 minute HIIT workout is to get your heart rate up and keep it up for the duration of the workout. This type of workout is designed to burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and build strength and endurance. The best 45 minute HIIT workout consists of a combination of high-intensity exercises, such as burpees and jumping jacks, and periods of rest, such as jogging or walking.

In summary, the best 45 minute HIIT workout is a combination of high-intensity exercises and periods of rest designed to burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and build strength and endurance. Now, let's dive into the specifics of this workout.

The Warm Up

Before starting any workout, it's important to warm up your muscles to prevent injury. Here's an example warm up for the best 45 minute HIIT workout:

1. Jog in place for 3 minutes
2. Do 10 jumping jacks
3. Do 10 lunges on each leg
4. Do 10 push ups
5. Do 10 squats

Repeat this circuit two times.

The Workout

Now that you're warmed up, it's time to get into the workout. Here's an example of the best 45 minute HIIT workout:

1. Jumping jacks for 30 seconds
2. Rest for 15 seconds
3. Burpees for 30 seconds
4. Rest for 15 seconds
5. Mountain climbers for 30 seconds
6. Rest for 15 seconds
7. High knees for 30 seconds
8. Rest for 15 seconds
9. Squat jumps for 30 seconds
10. Rest for 15 seconds
11. Repeat the circuit 5 times

After completing the circuit 5 times, take a 2-3 minute rest before moving on to the cool down.

The Cool Down

After completing any workout, it's important to cool down your muscles to prevent injury. Here's an example cool down for the best 45 minute HIIT workout:

1. Jog in place for 3 minutes
2. Do 10 hamstring stretches on each leg
3. Do 10 quad stretches on each leg
4. Do 10 shoulder rolls
5. Do 10 tricep stretches on each arm

Repeat this circuit two times.

The Benefits of the Best 45 Minute HIIT Workout

The best 45 minute HIIT workout has numerous benefits, including:

1. Burning calories
2. Improving cardiovascular health
3. Building strength and endurance
4. Saving time by getting an effective workout in just 45 minutes
5. Boosting metabolism for hours after the workout

How to Incorporate the Best 45 Minute HIIT Workout into Your Fitness Routine

The best way to incorporate the best 45 minute HIIT workout into your fitness routine is to do it 2-3 times per week on non-consecutive days. This will give your muscles time to recover in between workouts. You can also mix up the exercises to keep your workouts interesting and challenging.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of the Best 45 Minute HIIT Workout

Here are some tips for getting the most out of the best 45 minute HIIT workout:

1. Stay hydrated
2. Use proper form to prevent injury
3. Push yourself to your limits
4. Listen to your body and rest when needed
5. Mix up the exercises to keep your workouts interesting and challenging

Question and Answer

Q: How many calories can you burn during the best 45 minute HIIT workout?
A: The number of calories burned during the best 45 minute HIIT workout depends on various factors, such as weight, age, and intensity level. However, on average, you can expect to burn between 400-600 calories per workout.

Q: Can beginners do the best 45 minute HIIT workout?
A: Yes, beginners can do the best 45 minute HIIT workout. However, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity level as you become more comfortable with the exercises.

Q: Can the best 45 minute HIIT workout be done at home?
A: Yes, the best 45 minute HIIT workout can be done at home with little to no equipment. All you need is a timer, a mat, and some space to move.

Q: How often should you do the best 45 minute HIIT workout?
A: It's recommended to do the best 45 minute HIIT workout 2-3 times per week on non-consecutive days to give your muscles time to recover in between workouts.

Conclusion of Best 45 Minute HIIT Workout

The best 45 minute HIIT workout is a great way to get a full body burn in a short amount of time. By following the warm up, workout, and cool down outlined in this post, you can burn calories, improve cardiovascular health, and build strength and endurance. Remember to listen to your body, push yourself to your limits, and stay hydrated to get the most out of this workout.
